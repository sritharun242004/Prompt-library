Our Story

A House Built on Craft

HeritageLuxury was founded on a single conviction: that the most beautiful clothing in the world is already being made — in the villages and ateliers of India — and that it simply needs a stage worthy of its artistry.

We work directly with 400+ artisan families across twelve states, ensuring transparent wages, creative attribution, and long-term partnerships built on mutual respect. We do not source from factories. Every piece is made by a person with a name, a village, and a story.

Grassroot by HeritageLuxury

A Legacy of Empowerment

Grassroot was born from a belief that fashion can be a force for good. Every piece in this label is hand-crafted by women artisans in rural India, using natural dyes and sustainably sourced fabrics.

When you choose Grassroot, you are investing directly in the livelihood of over 1,500 women across Rajasthan, Gujarat, and Uttar Pradesh.
