Happiness Is a Choice You Keep Making

2024-10-288 tweets

  1. Happiness is not about positive thoughts. It is not about negative thoughts. It is about the absence of desire, especially the absence of desire for external things.

  2. The fundamental delusion is that there is something out there that will make me happy and fulfilled forever.

  3. Desire is a contract you make with yourself to be unhappy until you get what you want.

  4. Peace is happiness at rest. Happiness is peace in motion. A person who is calm and steady is creating happiness.

  5. Envy is the enemy of happiness. If you are envious of someone, you are making a statement that your happiness depends on what they have.

  6. Every time you catch yourself wanting something, ask: do I actually want this, or have I been conditioned to want it?

  7. The most important trick to being happy is to realize that happiness is a skill and a choice—not a condition.

  8. You can get to happiness by working on yourself. Read, meditate, exercise. Work on your mind as hard as you work on your body.
