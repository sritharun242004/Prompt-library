Value Your Time More Than Your Money

2024-03-107 tweets

  1. Time is the only non-renewable resource. Money can be made and lost. Time only goes one direction.

  2. Until you value your time, no one else will. Set an internal hourly rate and use it to make decisions.

  3. If an activity is worth less than your rate, hire it out. If you cannot afford to, change your situation until you can.

  4. The goal of money is to free up your time. If your money is not buying you more free time, something is wrong.

  5. The rich do not buy more things. They buy more time.

  6. Busyness is not a virtue. It is often a sign of poor prioritization. Protect your empty time.

  7. The most successful people I know have enormous amounts of unscheduled time. They guard it fiercely.
