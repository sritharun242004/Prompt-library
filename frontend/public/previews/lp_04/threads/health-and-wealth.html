Health Is the Prerequisite to Everything

2024-07-057 tweets

  1. Prioritize your health first. Nothing else matters if you are sick, in pain, or cognitively impaired.

  2. Exercise is not just for aesthetics. It is the single most proven intervention for mental health, cognitive function, and longevity.

  3. The best return on investment for your body is lifting weights. Start now. Compound interest applies to muscle, too.

  4. Sleep is the most underrated health intervention. Prioritize it above everything except family emergencies.

  5. Chronic stress is a disease. Remove the sources of stress from your life before they remove years from your life.

  6. Diet, sleep, and exercise. If those three are right, almost everything else in your life will improve.

  7. Your body is a machine. Feed it whole foods. Move it every day. Rest it fully. The returns are enormous.
