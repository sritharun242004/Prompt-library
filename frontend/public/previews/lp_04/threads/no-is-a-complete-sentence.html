"No" Is a Complete Sentence

2024-01-127 tweets

  1. The most successful people I know have a simple filter: is this a clear yes? If not, it is a no.

  2. Every "yes" you say is a "no" to something else. You have limited time and energy. Be intentional.

  3. You do not need to explain your "no." It is a complete sentence.

  4. Protect your mornings. Protect your attention. Protect your creative time. These are your most valuable assets.

  5. Social pressure is the enemy of clarity. Most obligations we feel are imaginary.

  6. The ability to say no without guilt is one of the highest forms of self-respect.

  7. Simplify your commitments ruthlessly. The quality of your yes depends on the quantity of your no.
