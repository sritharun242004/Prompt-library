How to Get Rich (without getting lucky)

2024-11-1510 tweets

  1. Seek wealth, not money or status. Wealth is having assets that earn while you sleep. Money is how we transfer time and wealth. Status is your rank in the social hierarchy.

  2. Understand that ethical wealth creation is possible. If you secretly despise wealth, it will elude you.

  3. Ignore people playing status games. They gain status by attacking people playing wealth creation games.

  4. You will not get rich renting out your time. You must own equity—a piece of a business—to gain your financial freedom.

  5. You will get rich by giving society what it wants but does not yet know how to get. At scale.

  6. Pick an industry where you can play long-term games with long-term people. All the returns in life, whether in wealth, relationships, or knowledge, come from compound interest.

  7. Pick business partners with high intelligence, energy, and above all, integrity. Do not partner with cynics and pessimists. Their beliefs are self-fulfilling.

  8. Learn to sell. Learn to build. If you can do both, you will be unstoppable.

  9. Arm yourself with specific knowledge, accountability, and leverage.

  10. Specific knowledge is knowledge that you cannot be trained for. If society can train you, it can train someone else and replace you.
