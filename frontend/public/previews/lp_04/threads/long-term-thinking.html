Play Long-Term Games with Long-Term People

2024-07-207 tweets

  1. All returns in life—in wealth, relationships, and knowledge—come from compound interest.

  2. Most people overestimate what they can do in a year and underestimate what they can do in a decade.

  3. Trust is the foundation of every long-term relationship. It is built slowly and destroyed quickly.

  4. The people who are most successful play infinite games. They are not trying to win—they are trying to keep playing.

  5. Short-term games have zero-sum outcomes. Long-term games have positive-sum outcomes. Choose your games wisely.

  6. Find people who are playing the same long-term game as you. Work with them. Compound together.

  7. Patience is not passive. It is an active choice to wait for the right opportunity rather than taking every opportunity that comes.
