Our Story

From the coast of Goa to the streets of Mumbai

O Pedro was born out of a love for the sun-soaked, vinegar-sharp, coconut-sweet food of the Konkan coast. Named after a celebrated Goan chef of yore, we wanted to bring that spirit — the Susegad, the warmth, the table-side laughter — to the city.

Our kitchen draws from old family recipes, fishing village traditions, and the Portuguese inflection that Goa has carried for five centuries. Every dish is an argument for simplicity: sourced well, cooked with care, eaten with good people.

The feni comes from Goa. The bebinca is made in-house. The music on Friday nights is live. And Pedro — our chef, our patron, our spirit — reminds us every service that great food is always an act of love.
