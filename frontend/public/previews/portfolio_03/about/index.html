About

We build things
that work.

Studio was founded in 2012 on a single conviction: that good design and commercial rigour are not in conflict. We are an independent agency of 45 people operating across brand identity, digital products, spatial experiences, and content. Our clients range from seed-stage startups to FTSE 250 companies. What they share is an ambition to be genuinely good at what they do — and a recognition that how they present that to the world matters.

We do not believe in design for its own sake. We believe in design that changes the number that matters — whether that is conversion rate, brand recognition, NPS, or the time it takes a new employee to understand what a company stands for.
