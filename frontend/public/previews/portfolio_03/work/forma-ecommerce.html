Challenge

Forma's handcrafted furniture deserved better than a generic Shopify template. Average order value was £4,200 — high enough to justify a bespoke commerce experience, but the existing site felt like a commodity retailer. Customers were spending significant time on the site but converting at a fraction of the industry benchmark, with post-session surveys revealing that they didn't feel sufficiently informed about materials and production processes to make a high-value purchase decision online. The custom commission enquiry process was generating leads but losing them at the estimate stage.

Approach

Every product page is built around material and process, not specification. We introduced 360° product views driven by studio-shot photography, a material selector showing real-world finishes in context, and lead-time transparency replacing the standard add-to-cart flow. The custom commission enquiry was redesigned from a generic form into a guided configuration experience — customers define their brief step by step, arriving at a request that gives Forma's makers enough information to quote accurately on first response.

Outcome

Conversion rate increased from 0.8% to 2.3% within 60 days of launch. Average order value increased 18% — attributed to the material selector driving customers toward premium finishes they had not previously considered. Custom commission enquiries doubled in 90 days, and the qualify-to-quote rate improved from 40% to 74% through the guided brief process.
