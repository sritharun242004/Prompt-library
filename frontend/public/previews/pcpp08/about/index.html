About

A studio built on
the belief that craft matters.

Founded 2017

We started as a crew.
We still think like one.

CrewCinema began in 2017 when director Vikram Anand and cinematographer Priya Sharma walked off a BBC documentary shoot in Varanasi, convinced they could do it better on their own terms. They were right.

Within two years, the studio had expanded to eight permanent crew members across directing, cinematography, editing, sound design, and production design. Today CrewCinema operates from Lower Parel in Mumbai and Connaught Place in Delhi, with productions in 18 countries.

The name is not an accident. We are not a production company that hires crew. We are the crew — and everything else is built around protecting that distinction.

Production Philosophy

01

Crew First

We believe the quality of a production is determined before the camera rolls. That means the right crew, the right brief, and enough time to find the right frame.

02

Cinematic Intention

Every decision — lens choice, colour grade, edit rhythm — is deliberate. We do not make films by committee. We make them by conviction.

03

Long-form Thinking

Even a 30-second ad deserves a story architecture. We bring feature-film thinking to every project, at every budget level.
