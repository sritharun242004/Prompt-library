The People

Eight artists.
One shared obsession.

CrewCinema is built around its crew. Each member brings a singular point of view — and together they form a production unit that thinks cinematically about every project, regardless of budget or category.

Vikram founded CrewCinema after a decade shooting across Asia for BBC and NatGeo. His observational style — long takes, natural light, unscripted truth — has earned him two national film awards and a retrospective at MAMI. He believes every frame should breathe.

Vikram Anand

Director / Founder

Priya trained at FTII Pune and cut her teeth on ad films before finding her language in documentary cinematography. She is known for building extraordinary rapport with subjects and her instinct for the decisive light moment. Her reel spans four continents.

Priya Sharma

Director of Photography

Nandita brings a playwright's ear to cinema. Her debut documentary won at Sundance; her first narrative feature screened at Venice. She specialises in intimate character studies that reveal systemic truths — never didactic, always human.

Nandita Rao

Director

Arjun heads CrewCinema's commercial division with a portfolio spanning luxury brands to grassroots campaigns. A graduate of the Art Center College of Design in Pasadena, he fuses visual design thinking with narrative urgency to make 30 seconds feel like a lifetime.

Arjun Mehta

Director / Commercial Lead

Dev is the invisible architect behind CrewCinema's pacing. With credits spanning feature films, music videos, and branded documentaries, his cutting style is instinctive and musical. He runs the studio's Avid suite and mentors the next generation of editors.

Dev Kapoor

Editor / Post-Production Head

Zara is the creative force behind CrewCinema's music video division. Her visual language — bold colour contrast, choreographic camera, lyrical editing — has made her the most sought-after music video director in India across three consecutive years.

Zara Khan

Director / Music Video Lead

Rohan's sets transform bare locations into fully realised worlds. From recreating 1980s Kolkata for Fire & Fade to designing the minimalist lighthouse interior of Half Light, his eye for period-accurate yet cinematically expressive design is unmatched.

Rohan Desai

Production Designer

Meera's soundscapes are a third actor in every CrewCinema production. Trained at Berklee Online and the NFDC Film Bazaar, she approaches audio as emotional architecture — field recordings, Foley, and score woven into a seamless sensory experience.

Meera Iyer

Sound Designer
