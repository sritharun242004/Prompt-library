About InsiderEdge

Editor & FounderCasey Newton

Casey Newton has covered the technology industry for 14 years. Before founding InsiderEdge in 2021, he was the platform columnist at The Verge for eight years, where he broke major stories on content moderation, social media policy, and AI development. His work has been cited in congressional testimony, EU regulatory proceedings, and three separate antitrust investigations.

InsiderEdge launched with 800 subscribers in January 2021 and has grown entirely through word of mouth to 142,000 paid subscribers. It has never taken venture capital, accepted advertising, or entered a content partnership with a tech platform.

Our manifesto

InsiderEdge was founded on a simple belief: that the most important journalism about technology is being left unwritten because it doesn't optimize for clicks, engagement, or advertiser sensitivities. The stories that matter most to the people who shape the industry — regulators, executives, investors, engineers, and policy staff — are often too slow, too technical, or too antagonistic to powerful companies to survive in an ad-supported newsroom.

We write those stories. We write them carefully, we verify them rigorously, and we do not soften them for access. We have been threatened with defamation suits twice. Both times, we published anyway. Both times, the stories were right.

No advertisingWe have never run an ad and never will. Our revenue comes entirely from subscriptions. That means our incentives are aligned with readers, not advertisers.
No sponsored contentWe do not accept paid placements, sponsored newsletters, or affiliate revenue. Every story we cover, we cover because we think it matters.
No algorithmic distributionWe do not depend on social media algorithms or search traffic. Our distribution is the inbox. If you subscribe, you get every story.
Source protectionWe protect every source who asks for protection. We have declined multiple legal demands for source information. We will continue to do so.
