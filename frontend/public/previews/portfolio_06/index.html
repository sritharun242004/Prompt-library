01.About Me

Hi, I'm Alex — a software engineer with eight years of experience building products that live at the intersection of design and engineering. I care deeply about the details: the 200 ms interaction, the focus ring, the semantic HTML that screen readers actually use. Good software, to me, is invisible — it just works, every time, for everyone.

I've shipped features used by millions of people at companies like Stripe, Airbnb, and Shopify. Today I specialise in full-stack TypeScript — Next.js on the front end, Node.js and GraphQL in the middle, and Postgres + AWS when things need to scale. I'm especially drawn to problems at the seam between product and infrastructure: performance budgets, real-time data pipelines, and developer experience tooling.

I'm currently open to senior and staff-level roles where I can help shape technical direction while staying close to the code. I believe the best engineering teams are built on trust, candid feedback, and a shared commitment to the craft.

When I'm not writing code, you'll find me maintaining a mechanical keyboard collection, contributing to open-source projects, hiking trails across the Pacific Northwest, or cooking overly ambitious weekend meals. I also write occasional deep-dives on performance, accessibility, and engineering culture — usually at awkward hours.
