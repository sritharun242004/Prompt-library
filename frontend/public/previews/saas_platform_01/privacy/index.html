Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025. We process only what we need — nothing more.

1. Data We Collect

We collect data that is strictly necessary to provide Plane and improve it. This includes:

  • Account information: name, work email, and password (hashed)
  • Workspace data: projects, issues, cycles, and modules you create in Plane
  • Usage telemetry: feature interactions, page views, and error reports (opt-out available)
  • Billing data: plan type, payment status (card details are processed by Stripe; we never store them)
  • Support communications: emails and in-app messages sent to our team

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Authenticate you and maintain your session
  • Sync your workspace data in real-time across team members
  • Send transactional emails (confirmations, invitations, payment receipts)
  • Diagnose bugs and improve product reliability
  • Understand which features are used most to prioritise our roadmap

We do not use your project or issue data to train AI models or share it with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Workspace data is stored on servers in the region you select during setup. Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256. Data in transit is encrypted over TLS 1.2+. We undergo annual third-party security audits.

For self-hosted deployments, you are responsible for the security and backup of the data on your infrastructure. Our security documentation provides recommended configuration guidelines.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Plane:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • PostHog — product analytics (self-hosted; data stays in our infrastructure)
  • Sentry — error monitoring

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your data. Workspace owners can export all workspace data from Settings → Export at any time.

To close your account and request data deletion, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Deletion is permanent and processed within 30 days.

6. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, email privacy@plane.so. We respond within 5 business days.
