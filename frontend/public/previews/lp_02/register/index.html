FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

What does the Enterprise Pass include?

Full 2-day access to all sessions, VIP networking dinner, priority seating in the Plenary Hall, Konnect App Premium profile, exclusive CEO Roundtable access (invite-only), physical summit kit, digital content vault, and 90-day recording access.

Are group discounts available?

Yes. Groups of 5 or more delegates receive 15% off the applicable tier. Groups of 10 or more receive 25% off. Contact nasscom-leadx@nasscom.in for group invoicing.

Is GST applicable?

Yes. 18% GST is applicable to all ticket prices. A GST-compliant invoice will be issued to all registered delegates. Corporates can claim input tax credit.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations made more than 30 days before the event receive a full refund. Cancellations within 30 days may transfer the pass to a colleague. No refunds are issued within 7 days of the event.

Can I attend virtually?

LeaderShip-X 2025 is a primarily in-person event. The Bangalore Hub at NIMHANS Convention Centre offers live-streaming and satellite sessions. The Konnect App will also provide live session Q&A for all registered delegates.

What is the Konnect App?

Konnect is the official NASSCOM summit app. It provides AI-powered delegate matchmaking, personalised agenda building, live Q&A, speaker connect requests, and real-time venue navigation. Available free to all registered delegates.
