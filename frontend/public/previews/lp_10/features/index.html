Features

We believe the best features should be free. Here's everything FormCraft can do.

Free

Instant Publishing

Publish your form with one click. Share a link or embed anywhere on the web.

What's included

  • Publish with a single click — no deployment steps
  • Auto-generated short link you can share anywhere
  • Custom slug available on Pro (yourbrand.formcraft.io/form-name)
  • QR code download included
Free

Notion-Native Embeds

Paste your form link directly into Notion. It renders as a full inline block.

What's included

  • Paste any FormCraft URL into Notion — it becomes a live form block
  • Works with Notion personal, team, and enterprise plans
  • Responses sync to your FormCraft dashboard in real time
  • No Notion API key required
Free

Conditional Logic

Show or hide questions based on previous answers. Always free, no upgrade needed.

What's included

  • Show / hide questions based on previous answers
  • Supports multi-branch logic trees of any depth
  • Visual logic builder — no code required
  • Conditional required fields and page jumps
Free

Team Collaboration

Share edit access with teammates. Comments and version history included.

What's included

  • Invite teammates by email with edit or view roles
  • Threaded comments on individual form questions
  • Full version history — restore any snapshot
  • Duplicate and fork forms across workspaces
Free

Response Analytics

Completion rates, drop-off points, and response trends in one dashboard.

What's included

  • Per-question completion and drop-off rates
  • Response trend charts (daily / weekly / monthly)
  • Device and referral source breakdown
  • Export raw response data as CSV or JSON
Pro

Multilingual Forms

Auto-translate your form to 40+ languages. Respondents pick their own.

What's included

  • Auto-detect respondent language via browser locale
  • Manual language picker on the form
  • Machine-translated by DeepL (40+ languages)
  • RTL layout support for Arabic, Hebrew, and Farsi
Pro

Privacy by Default

No cookies. GDPR-compliant. Zero tracking on your respondents.

What's included

  • Zero tracking pixels or analytics on respondents
  • No cookies set — fully GDPR, CCPA, and PECR compliant
  • Responses encrypted at rest (AES-256)
  • Self-hosted option available for enterprise
Pro

Custom Branding

Add your logo, colors, and domain. Make every form feel like yours.

What's included

  • Upload your logo and set brand colors
  • Remove FormCraft branding from forms and emails
  • Custom sending domain (forms@yourcompany.com)
  • White-label share pages — your URL, your design
