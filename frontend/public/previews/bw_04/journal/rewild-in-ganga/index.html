Rewild in Ganga

12 April 2025·5 min read

A slow journey down the river where linen shirts breathe like morning air and copper carafes hold the memory of water. The Ganga collection is an ode to stillness.

The Ganga at dawn does not rush. It carries its own logic of time — slow, deliberate, unhurried. We arrived by train, with two bags between four people, and discovered that the river had already decided our pace for us.

Linen was the obvious choice. The Ganga Linen Shirt appeared on the ghats before the sun did, catching the first light the way only open-weave fabric can. By afternoon, the copper carafe kept river-cooled water cold enough to remind us that simple things, done well, outlast everything.

The collection asks a single question: what would you carry if you knew the journey mattered more than the destination?
