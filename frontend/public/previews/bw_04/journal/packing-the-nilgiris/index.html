Packing the Nilgiris

18 November 2024·4 min read

Tea country in the early morning carries a particular chill. Here is how we packed for three days in the hills — without checking a bag.

The Nilgiris at six in the morning are twenty degrees cooler than the city you came from. We learned this the hard way on the first trip. The second trip, we packed better.

The indigo jacket. A khadi wrap for the evenings. The leather card holder with nothing in it but a bus ticket and a card. The hammam towel for the waterfall we did not plan to find.

A good travel wardrobe is not about having options. It is about having the right things — fewer, better, made to last. The Nilgiris will test your gear. Pack accordingly.
