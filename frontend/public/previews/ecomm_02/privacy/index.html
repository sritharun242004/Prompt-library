Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. What We Collect

At Nothing To Hide, what you see is what you get — and that goes for our data practices too. When you shop with us, we collect your name, email address, shipping address, and payment information to fulfil your order.

We also collect browsing data automatically — such as pages visited, device type, and referring URL — to improve your experience and our website's performance.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Processing and delivering your orders, including sending tracking information
  • Providing customer support and responding to your queries
  • Sending you product updates and promotional offers (with your consent)
  • Improving our website, products, and customer experience
  • Detecting and preventing fraud and security incidents
  • Meeting our legal and regulatory obligations

3. Sharing Your Information

We never sell your data. Period. We share information only with partners essential to operating our business: payment gateways, delivery partners, and email service providers. Every third party is vetted and bound by a data processing agreement.

We may share data with authorities when legally required or to protect our rights and the safety of our customers.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies (to keep your cart and login session working), preference cookies (to remember your settings), and analytics cookies (to understand site usage). We do not use cross-site tracking cookies for advertising.

You can decline non-essential cookies via your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect your ability to complete a purchase.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Ask us to delete your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Complain to your local data protection authority

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions, email us at privacy@nothingtohide.in or write to: Nothing To Hide, 14 Baner Road, Pune 411045, Maharashtra, India.
