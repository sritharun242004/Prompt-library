Instructor

Sunita Rao is a Jaipur-based fine-art and documentary photographer who has exhibited at the Jaipur Photo Festival, the Chobi Mela International Photography Festival in Dhaka, and the FORMAT Festival in the UK. Her teaching method is rooted in the history of photography and the visual arts.
