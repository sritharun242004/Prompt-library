Ubud, Bali · 2024

Amara & Kai

Rice terrace vows at golden hour, temple blessings, and a reception lit entirely by a thousand floating lanterns.

The story of Amara & Kai is one that unfolds in light and shadow, in the quiet pause between vows and the eruption of joy that follows. Set against the unmistakable backdrop of Ubud, Bali, their day carried the weight of something rare — a love that had found its truest form.

Every frame captured in 2024 carries the intention of the moment — the way light fell across a face at just the right angle, the geometry of joy in a single stolen glance. These are images made to endure, not merely to document.

For Amara & Kai, Ubud, Bali was not simply a destination. It was a reflection of who they are — and who they intend to become together. It is a privilege to have been there.
