The Amethyst Garden

Amethyst Garden — Jewellery 2021

Purple was once the most expensive colour in the world — available only to emperors and cardinals, its price measured in molluscs per gram. The Amethyst Garden collection restores that chromatic authority: Brazilian amethysts in 22-karat yellow gold, tanzanite ear drops beside soft lavender sapphires from Sri Lanka, and a centrepiece parure of amethyst-and-pearl that references the jewel tradition of the Nawab of Rampur. To wear purple, rightly, is still an act of sovereignty.
