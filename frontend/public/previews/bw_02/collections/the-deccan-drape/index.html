The Deccan Drape

The Deccan Drape — Saree Edit 2023

The Deccan plateau holds within its ancient volcanic rock the memory of empires — Vijayanagara, Bahmani, Golconda — each with a textile tradition as rich as its political drama. The Deccan Drape is a cross-geographical excavation: Pochampalli ikat in conversation with Paithani borders, Bidriwork-inspired metallic weaves alongside natural indigo Kalamkari pallus. To wear The Deccan Drape is to be a walking archive of southern intelligence.
