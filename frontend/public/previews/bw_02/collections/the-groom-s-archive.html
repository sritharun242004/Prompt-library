The Groom's Archive

The Groom's Archive — Bridal Menswear 2021

For too long, the Indian groom has been a footnote in the story of the Indian wedding. The Groom's Archive corrects this historical oversight: brocade sherwanis in heritage looms of Varanasi, daggers as ceremonial accessories, sehra of fresh flowers constructed with the precision of a floral architect, and footwear of hand-embroidered velvet. A groom is not an accessory to his wedding — he is its co-author. This collection arms him accordingly.
