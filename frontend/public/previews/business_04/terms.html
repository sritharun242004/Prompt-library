Terms & Placement Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Placement Guarantee — Eligibility

The placement guarantee applies to students who: (1) complete ≥90% of scheduled sessions, (2) submit all assignments on time, (3) complete ≥5 mock interviews, (4) actively apply to ≥30 companies through the SkillForge portal, and (5) maintain a performance score of ≥70%. Students who do not meet these criteria are not eligible for the guarantee. The guarantee covers job assistance — not a specific salary level.

Salary Figures — Methodology

All salary figures on the SkillForge website are based on self-reported data from alumni who consented to disclosure. "Average CTC" is the arithmetic mean of disclosed CTCs from eligible alumni placed in FY2024. "Highest package" is a single data point. Individual results vary significantly. Past results do not guarantee future outcomes.

Fee Policy

Program fees are published on individual program pages. No payment is collected at application. Payment is due upon acceptance and before cohort start. EMI options are available through partner financial institutions — SkillForge is not a lender.

Refund Policy

Full refund within 7 days of cohort start if fewer than 3 sessions have been attended. 50% refund between 7–21 days. No refund after 21 days. Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 10 business days.

Data Usage

Application data is used solely for program admission and counseling. We do not share personal data with third parties without consent. Alumni salary data is published only with explicit written consent.
