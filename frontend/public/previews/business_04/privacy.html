Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Data We Collect

We collect name, mobile number, current role, and career goals at application. No payment data is collected by SkillForge — payments are processed by third-party gateways. We do not collect sensitive personal data such as Aadhaar, PAN, or financial account details.

How We Use It

Application data is used to evaluate fit for our programs and to assign mentors. Contact data is used for counselor follow-up. We do not sell data to third parties or use it for unrelated advertising.

Alumni Data

Salary and placement data is published only with explicit, written alumni consent. Alumni may withdraw consent at any time by emailing privacy@skillforge.in.

Your Rights

You may request data deletion, correction, or export by emailing privacy@skillforge.in. Requests are processed within 30 business days.

Cookies

Essential cookies are set for session management. Analytics cookies require explicit consent. No advertising or cross-site tracking cookies are set.
