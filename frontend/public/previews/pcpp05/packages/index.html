Common Questions

Before You Enquire

When should we book?

We recommend booking at least 6–12 months before your wedding, especially for peak season (October–February). Premium dates fill up quickly.

Do you travel internationally?

Yes. Our Royal package includes international travel. We have documented weddings in Dubai, Sri Lanka, Thailand, and across Europe.

How long until we receive the images?

Standard delivery is 8–10 weeks. Platinum packages include priority delivery within 4 weeks. Royal packages within 2 weeks.

Can we add a cinematic film to Silver or Gold?

Absolutely. Cinematic films can be added to any package as an add-on. Please mention this in your enquiry and we will include it in your custom quote.

Do you cover both pre-wedding and wedding events?

Yes. All packages from Gold upward include pre-wedding coverage. Silver packages can include engagement sessions as a standalone add-on.

What format are the final images delivered in?

High-resolution JPEG files, web-optimised versions for sharing, and a private password-protected online gallery. Physical albums are printed on Fujifilm Crystal Archive paper.
