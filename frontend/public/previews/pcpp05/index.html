Our Story

The Studio

Born from a love of light, legacy, and the Indian wedding

StoryBookOpulence was founded in 2016 by photographer Aryan Mehta after years of witnessing how poorly lit snapshots failed to honour the profound beauty of Indian weddings. What began as a personal mission has grown into India's most awarded luxury wedding photography studio — with a team of five and a portfolio spanning 14 countries.

We are not event photographers. We are visual storytellers. Each wedding we document becomes a chapter in an ongoing epic of Indian love stories — cinematic in scope, intimate in feeling, and destined to be passed from generation to generation.
