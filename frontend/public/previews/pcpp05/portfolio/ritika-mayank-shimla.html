The Story

Just beyond Shimla at Mashobra, the President's Retreat overlooks an apple orchard and a sea of Himalayan ridges. Ritika and Mayank's spring wedding here felt both wildly romantic and deeply peaceful — floral-canopied ceremonies, orchard walks, and evenings by the fire wrapped in the scent of pine and apple blossom.
