Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025. We believe in transparency — in our ingredients and in our data practices.

1. What We Collect

When you place an order or create a ChaiRitual account, we collect your name, email address, delivery address, phone number, and payment details. If you subscribe to a tea plan, we also store your subscription preferences and ritual history.

We automatically collect browsing data such as pages visited, device type, and time spent — to improve your experience and understand what resonates most with our community.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Processing and delivering your orders and subscriptions
  • Sending order confirmations, shipping updates, and delivery notifications
  • Managing your subscription — including reminders, pauses, and renewals
  • Sharing tea recommendations tailored to your ritual preferences
  • Improving our product range and website based on aggregate usage data
  • Preventing fraud and keeping your account secure

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your data. We share it only with trusted partners essential to running ChaiRitual: our payment gateway (Razorpay), delivery partners, and email service provider. Each partner signs a data processing agreement.

We may share data with government authorities when legally required.

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep your cart and login session active. We also use analytics cookies (via a privacy-respecting provider) to understand traffic patterns. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

5. Your Rights

  • Access a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information in your account
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time via the unsubscribe link

Questions about your data?

Our team is happy to help. We aim to respond to all data-related requests within 3 business days.

6. Contact Us

Email us at privacy@chairitual.in or write to: ChaiRitual, 12 Kumbalagodu Industrial Area, Bengaluru 560074, Karnataka, India.
