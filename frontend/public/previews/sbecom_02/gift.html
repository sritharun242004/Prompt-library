Gifting FAQ

How is the gift packaged?

All gifts come in our signature Warm Paper box with Green tissue, a personalised message card (handwritten), and a ChaiRitual wax seal. Premium Gift Wrapping upgrades to a handcrafted jute box with silk ribbon for ₹99 extra.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery: 3-5 business days across India. Express delivery (1-2 days) available in Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, and Pune for an additional ₹99.

Can I customise what goes inside the box?

Yes! Use our Gift Builder above to personalise by recipient type and budget. You can also add specific teas by visiting our shop and choosing "Send as Gift" on any product page.

Do you offer corporate gifting?

Absolutely. We offer branded tin customisation, bulk discounts from 20+ units, and a dedicated account manager for corporate orders. Contact us at gifting@chairitual.in for a custom quote.

Can I schedule delivery for a specific date?

Yes. At checkout, select "Gift Delivery" and choose your preferred date. We'll dispatch to arrive on that day (subject to courier availability in your area).
