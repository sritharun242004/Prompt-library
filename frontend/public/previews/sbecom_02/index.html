The ChaiRitual Method

Master the Perfect Cup

Great tea isn't complicated — it just needs a little intention. Four simple steps separate an ordinary cup from an extraordinary ritual.

STEP 01

Choose Your Tea

Browse 24 blends across 6 ritual categories. Each tea tells a story of Indian origin.

STEP 02

Heat Your Water

Temperature matters. Green teas: 75-85°C. Oolong: 90°C. Black & masala: 100°C.

STEP 03

Brew with Patience

Every blend has its own time. Most teas need just 3-5 minutes. We'll guide you.

STEP 04

Savour the Ritual

No rushing. Your cup is ready. Breathe in the aroma before the first sip.

Traceable Origins

From the Gardens of India

Every tea in our collection is traceable to a specific region, garden, and harvest. We visit our sources annually and publish our supply chain.

Assam

Bold & Malty

The mighty Brahmaputra plains produce over half of India's tea. Every Assam cup carries the memory of monsoon rains and misty mornings.

Darjeeling

The Champagne of Teas

High in the Eastern Himalayas, Darjeeling gardens produce the world's most celebrated teas — GI protected and utterly irreplaceable.

Nilgiri

Blue Mountains

Tamil Nadu's Nilgiri Hills grow tea year-round at altitude. Home to India's finest oolongs and the most versatile green teas.
