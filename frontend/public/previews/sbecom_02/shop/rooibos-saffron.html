About This Blend

Rooibos (Red Bush) from the Cederberg mountains of South Africa meets Kashmiri saffron in this extraordinary evening blend. Caffeine-free by nature, rooibos is packed with antioxidants and has a naturally sweet, woody flavour that carries saffron's exotic floral notes beautifully. Golden-red in colour. Deeply nourishing.

Origin

Himachal

2,000–4,000m above sea level

Rooibos: Cederberg, South Africa · Saffron: Pampore, Kashmir

The high-altitude valleys of Himachal Pradesh — Spiti, Lahaul, Kullu, and Kangra — are home to India's most potent medicinal herbs. The extreme temperature differences between day and night concentrate essential oils and bioactive compounds in plants like chamomile, lavender, and ashwagandha. Our Himachal sourcing partners are small family farms committed to traditional, chemical-free cultivation.
