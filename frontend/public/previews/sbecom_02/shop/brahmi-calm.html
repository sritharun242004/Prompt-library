About This Blend

Brahmi is the Ayurvedic brain tonic — used for millennia to support memory, focus, and cognitive clarity. Our blend pairs organic Bacopa monnieri with European lemon balm and passionflower for a tea that calms the mind without sedating it. The ideal companion for deep work, study sessions, or creative blocks.

Origin

Kerala

Kerala backwaters region (brahmi) + Himalayan foothills (lemon balm)

Kerala's spice coast has supplied the world with cardamom, ginger, turmeric, pepper, and moringa for over 3,000 years. The Malabar Coast and Western Ghats create a perfect combination of heavy rainfall and rich laterite soil. Our Kerala sourcing focuses on the Wayanad and Ernakulam districts — certified organic farms working in partnership with tribal communities who have cultivated these spices for generations.
