About This Blend

There's a reason this is India's most loved drink. Our Masala Chai Classic uses the highest-grade Assam CTC, blended with hand-pounded cardamom pods, dried ginger root, Ceylon cinnamon, and a hint of black pepper. The exact ratio took 18 months to perfect. Brew it strong, add milk, and find your calm.

Jorhat district, Assam — the heartland of Indian CTC production

The mighty Brahmaputra plains of Assam produce over 52% of India's tea. The alluvial soil, humid tropical climate, and monsoon rains combine to create the bold, malty character that makes Assam CTC the backbone of every Indian kitchen's morning cup. Our leaves are sourced from gardens in Dibrugarh and Jorhat — the heart of Assam tea country.
