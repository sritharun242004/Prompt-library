About This Blend

Our Nilgiri Oolong is a 55% oxidised tea from the Korakundah estate — one of the world's highest tea gardens. The result is a smooth, complex cup with notes of wild honey, stone fruit, and jasmine blossom. Partially oxidised between green and black tea, it offers the best of both worlds: the liveliness of green with the depth of black.

Korakundah Estate, Nilgiri Hills — elevation 2,400m, world's highest oolong garden

The Blue Mountains of Tamil Nadu are India's most versatile tea-growing region. The Nilgiri Hills produce tea year-round thanks to their unique altitude and two monsoon cycles. Our Nilgiri teas come from the Korakundah Estate in the Anaimalai Hills — one of the world's highest tea gardens at 2,400m — where cool nights and misty mornings create outstanding oolongs and green teas.
