About This Blend

Our finest celebration tea. FTGFOP1 grade Darjeeling — the highest grading in Indian tea classification — is blended with hand-picked rosa damascena rose petals from Pushkar, Rajasthan. The result is a tea of extraordinary elegance: golden in the cup, floral and fruity, with the unmistakable Darjeeling muscatel backbone. Gift this to someone you love.

Jungpana Estate, Darjeeling + Rosa Damascena from Pushkar Valley, Rajasthan

Nestled in the foothills of the Eastern Himalayas, Darjeeling is India's most celebrated tea region and the world's first tea to receive Geographical Indication (GI) protection. The cool mists, thin air, and steep mountain slopes coax out the legendary "muscatel" character — a natural grape-like aroma found nowhere else on Earth. Our teas come from Castleton, Makaibari, Goomtee, and Jungpana estates.

Geographical Indication (GI) Tagged: This tea is legally protected — only tea grown in this specific region can carry this name. It is India\'s equivalent of French wine appellations.
