About This Blend

Born out of India's wettest season, Monsoon Masala is our most intense spice blend. Higher concentrations of dry ginger, black pepper, and rare long pepper (pippali) — which Ayurveda considers the most warming of all spices — make this the chai equivalent of a warm blanket. Brew with buffalo milk for the full monsoon experience.

Assam (CTC base) + Meghalaya (long pepper) — seasonal blend, July–September

The mighty Brahmaputra plains of Assam produce over 52% of India's tea. The alluvial soil, humid tropical climate, and monsoon rains combine to create the bold, malty character that makes Assam CTC the backbone of every Indian kitchen's morning cup. Our leaves are sourced from gardens in Dibrugarh and Jorhat — the heart of Assam tea country.
