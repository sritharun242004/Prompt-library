Fair Trade Premium

The price tells the whole story

Every product on HandmadeConfidence is priced at a Fair Trade Premium — meaning the price you see includes enough margin for the artisan to earn a liveable wage, access healthcare, and fund children's education.

We publish what percentage of every sale reaches the collective. There are no hidden middlemen. No wholesale markups designed to squeeze artisan margins.
