About the collective

Chhattisgarh Tribal Weavers

Kondagaon, Chhattisgarh 54 artisans 15 years active

Rooted in the Bastar region, these Gond and Halba women weave geometric patterns passed down over seventeen generations. The collective was formed in 2009 to preserve the endangered kosa silk traditions and ensure fair wages reach every weaver directly.
