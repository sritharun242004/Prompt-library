About this item

An 8-inch traditional deepam (oil lamp) cast using the lost-wax (cire perdue) method in Panchaloha alloy — five metals including gold and silver in trace amounts. Each lamp takes 3 days from wax carving to final polish. Used in homes and temples across South India.

About the collective

Tamil Nadu Bell Metal

Swamimalai, Tamil Nadu 28 artisans 6 years active

Swamimalai's sthapathis have cast bronze for Chola temples for over 1,000 years using the lost-wax process. Today, 28 families — now including women for the first time in collective history — produce ritual objects and home decor, keeping an ancient metallurgical tradition alive and economically viable.
