Our story

Raghurajpur is India's only heritage crafts village where every household is a studio. These 42 chitrakaras — including 19 women who traditionally stayed behind the scenes — now lead the collective and price their work based on hours, not buyer bargaining.

The artisans

42 artisans across Raghurajpur and surrounding villages. Every artisan in this collective is a verified member, earns the collective rate, and has access to the collective's healthcare and skill fund.
