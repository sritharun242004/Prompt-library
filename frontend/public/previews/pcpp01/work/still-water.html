Fine Art · Kerala Backwaters, India · 2019

Still Water

for Hauser & Wirth

Made during monsoon season on a rice boat moving through the Kerala backwaters, Still Water is a meditation on reflection — literal and psychic. The series was exhibited at Hauser & Wirth Somerset and later acquired in part by the Tate Modern collection.
