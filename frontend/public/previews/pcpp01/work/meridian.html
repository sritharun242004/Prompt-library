Fine Art · Atacama Desert, Chile · 2023

Meridian

for Gagosian Gallery

A fine art series made over fourteen nights in the Atacama, the driest non-polar desert on Earth. Meridian explores the coordinates of solitude — the precise geographic and psychic point where a human presence becomes cosmologically insignificant. Exhibited at Gagosian Gallery, New York, Spring 2023.
