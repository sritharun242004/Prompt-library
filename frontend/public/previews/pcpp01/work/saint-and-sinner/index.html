Saint & Sinner

for Saint Laurent

Saint Laurent's SS19 campaign, shot across three nights in Los Angeles — the Salton Sea, the Mojave freeway, and a 1970s Hollywood Hills house. Saint & Sinner renders the California mythology as pure noir: desire and dust, glamour and desolation.
