What does GI Certified mean?

A Geographical Indication (GI) tag is granted by the Government of India to products that have a specific geographical origin and possess qualities attributable to that origin. When you buy a GI-certified product — like Kanjivaram silk, Banarasi brocade, or Ajrakh block prints — you are guaranteed that the product was made using traditional methods in the correct region by trained artisans. This protects both buyers from counterfeits and artisans from mass-market imitation.
