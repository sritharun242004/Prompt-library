Chennai Silk House

Meenakshi Subramaniam

Tamil Nadu·Silk Weaving·Est. 1991·4.8·4 products

Meenakshi Subramaniam weaves on a traditional pit loom in Kanchipuram, carrying forward a tradition of Kanjivaram silk weaving that dates to the Chola period. Her sarees are known for their rich zari borders, temple motifs, and heavy silk that only improves with age. She works with a collective of 12 women weavers from her village.

The craft tradition

Silk Weaving from Tamil Nadu

Silk Weaving is one of India's living craft traditions, practiced for generations in Tamil Nadu. Artisans like Meenakshi Subramaniam keep these techniques alive — passing knowledge from parent to child, from one generation to the next. When you buy from Chennai Silk House, you directly support the continuation of this heritage and the economic wellbeing of the artisan community.
