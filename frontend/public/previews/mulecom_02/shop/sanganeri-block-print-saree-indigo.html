About this item

A stunning 6.3-metre Sanganeri block-print saree in pure cotton. Hand-printed with ancestral teakwood blocks using natural indigo and iron-rust dyes. The geometric buta motif repeats across the body, with a contrasting rust border. Each saree takes two days to print and three days to dry naturally in the Jaipur sun.

The artisan's story

Ramesh Chhipa

Jaipur Block Prints · Rajasthan · Est. 1978

Ramesh Chhipa is a fifth-generation block printer from Sanganer, Jaipur. Using ancestral hand-carved teakwood blocks and natural dyes — indigo, madder, pomegranate rind — his family has practised this craft for over 150 years. Every yard of cloth tells a story of Rajasthan's desert landscape through geometry, florals, and traditional buta motifs.
