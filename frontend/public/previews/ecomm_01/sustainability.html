Our factories

The people who make your clothes

Partner since 2019

Texteis Penedo

Guimarães, Portugal · 280 workers

A family-run mill in the heart of Portugal's textile region, Texteis Penedo has been weaving for three generations. When we first visited in 2019, their commitment to worker welfare — on-site childcare, above-market wages, a worker profit-share scheme — matched our own values exactly. Every NaturalComfort merino and linen garment is finished here.

Partner since 2020

Green Mile Vietnam

Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam · 420 workers

Green Mile pioneered clean production in Vietnam with a solar roof covering 80% of energy needs and a zero-discharge water treatment system. All 420 workers receive a living wage 40% above the local minimum, paid parental leave, and free health coverage. Our footwear line is assembled here.

Partner since 2021

Calzados Artesanos Mora

Alicante, Spain · 95 workers

This 95-person artisan workshop in Alicante has maintained hand-stitching traditions since 1947. Their Leather Working Group Gold rating means full chemical transparency, and their mechanical construction (no adhesives) means every boot can be resoled indefinitely. Our Chelsea boots and leather sandals are made here.
