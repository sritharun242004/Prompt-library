Materials

Nature makes the best fibres. We just don't ruin them.

Carbon neutral

ZQ Merino Wool

From certified farms to your wardrobe

Our merino comes from New Zealand farms holding ZQ certification — the most rigorous animal welfare and environmental standard in the wool industry. Every farm is GPS-mapped, audited annually, and paid a premium above market price.

Biodegradable

TENCEL™ Lyocell

Eucalyptus forests, closed-loop production

TENCEL™ is made in a closed-loop process where 99.5% of the organic solvent is recovered and reused. Sourced from FSC-certified eucalyptus plantations on marginal land — no food crops displaced, no irrigation required.

GRS certified

Recycled Fibres

Waste transformed into wardrobe staples

From ECONYL® ocean nylon to post-consumer recycled polyester fleece — we divert tonnes of waste from landfill and ocean each year. Every recycled piece we make is GRS certified with full chain-of-custody traceability.
