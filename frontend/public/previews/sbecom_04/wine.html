Sommelier's Guide

Why wine pairing matters

Why does wine pairing matter?

A well-chosen wine does not compete with food — it amplifies it. The acidity in a white Burgundy cuts through butter sauce and makes the next bite taste brighter. The tannins in a Barolo absorb the fat in a braise and reveal flavour beneath it. This is not mysticism — it is chemistry.

What if I don't drink wine?

The Wine Cellar is entirely optional and can be added or removed at any time. Our pairings notes are included in every recipe card regardless — understanding why a wine works deepens your understanding of flavour, whether or not you pour a glass.

How are wines selected each month?

Isabelle reviews your confirmed recipe selections each Monday and finalises the wine choices by Tuesday. She considers the dominant flavours, cooking methods, and sauces — not just the protein. Every choice is personal and written with a specific dish in mind.
