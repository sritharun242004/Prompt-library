Process

How BlueChef works

From choosing your plan to plating your first dish — four steps that transform your kitchen into a professional dining experience.

01

Choose Your Plan

Select your serving size (2 or 4 people) and how many nights per week you want to cook. Add the Wine Cellar for sommelier-curated pairings delivered alongside your ingredients.

02

Chefs Design Your Menu

Each week, our chef team creates 18 recipes across French, Italian, Japanese, and Global Modern cuisine. You select your favourites — every dish comes with chef notes, technique guides, and video support.

03

Ingredients Delivered Fresh

Pre-portioned, sustainably sourced ingredients arrive Wednesday or Thursday in insulated, temperature-controlled packaging. Wine is included in the same delivery if selected.

04

Cook & Rate Your Experience

Follow the step-by-step recipe guide. Pour the paired wine. Rate your meal in your account to help us refine future menus to your taste. This is restaurant cooking — in your kitchen.
