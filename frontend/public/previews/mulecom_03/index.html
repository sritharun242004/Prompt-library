How we ship your craft

1

Order confirmed

Artisan workshop is notified within 2 hours of your order.

2

Craft & quality check

Your item is inspected, photographed, and wrapped in acid-free tissue.

3

Dispatched with tracking

Shipped via our courier partner — you receive a tracking link by SMS/email.

4

Delivered to your door

Delivered within the estimated window. We follow up to confirm receipt.
