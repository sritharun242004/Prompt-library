About

We explain Indian business the way it deserves to be explained

AnalyticalKen launched in 2020 with one proposition: Indian business journalism had a quality problem. The financial press was chasing earnings, the business press was chasing access, and long-form analysis existed in neither. We built what we wanted to read.

The founding story

AnalyticalKen was founded in Bengaluru in 2020 by a small team of journalists who had spent years at national business publications and found the model increasingly untenable. The pressure to produce volume. The dependency on advertising from companies we wrote about. The structural impossibility of holding power accountable while needing its approval to survive.

The name comes from a simple idea: analytical rigour applied to the Ken — the range of what you know and understand. Not breaking news. Not opinion. Analysis: the careful, reported, evidence-based work of explaining what is actually happening in Indian business and why it matters.

We launched with forty-three subscribers. They paid before a single story existed because they believed in what we were building. Today we have 14,800 subscribers — founders, executives, investors, policy researchers, and journalists who want to think more clearly about Indian business.

Editorial charter

No advertisements

We do not run advertisements, sponsored content, or brand partnerships. The companies we write about cannot buy space in our publication. This is a non-negotiable structural commitment.

Sources on record

We request all sources to go on record. We use anonymous sourcing only when the source faces credible professional risk and the information is verified by at least one additional source.

Data over assertions

Every significant claim in our work is supported by primary data — regulatory filings, financial results, or original research. We link to our sources. We show our methodology. We acknowledge uncertainty.

Corrections policy

We correct errors promptly and prominently. Corrections appear at the top of the article and in our weekly subscriber briefing. We do not delete errors — we acknowledge and correct them.

No AI-generated content

Every story at AnalyticalKen is reported and written by a human correspondent. We do not use AI to generate stories, summaries, or analysis. We may use AI tools to assist with research.

Reader privacy

We do not sell reader data. We do not use tracking advertisers. Analytics are self-hosted and aggregate. We know how many people read a story. We do not know who you are.
