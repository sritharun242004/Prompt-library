India's Industrial Water Crisis

Factories, farms, and cities are competing for the same depleting aquifers

Karthik Sundar·16 March 2026·14 min read

India extracts more groundwater than any country on earth. Industrial demand has tripled since 2010. The central groundwater authority says 23 states are in crisis. The conflict between economic growth and water security has become the defining policy challenge of the next decade.
