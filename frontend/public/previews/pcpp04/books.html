Steidl2024

The Long Light

Documentary Photographs from South Asia, 2015–2024

A decade of documentary photography across South Asia, collected in a single volume of 240 pages. From the salt pans of Kutch to the akharas of Varanasi, from the monsoon rains of Kerala to the Partition landscapes of Punjab. The Long Light is UniverseStory's first comprehensive retrospective, with an introductory essay by the critic Girish Shahane and afterwords from three of the photographers' subjects. Printed in tritone on 170gsm uncoated stock, designed by Uta Schneider at Steidl.

Salt and Ceremony

The Agaria Salt-makers of the Rann of Kutch

An expanded edition of the Salt and Ceremony photo essay, featuring 78 photographs made over two seasons in the Rann of Kutch along with extended interviews with five Agaria families and a sociological essay by Dr. Indira Hirway of the Centre for Development Alternatives, Ahmedabad. Published simultaneously in English and Gujarati. A portion of proceeds from each copy sold is directed to the Agaria welfare fund administered by the Kutch Craft and Artisans Foundation.

City of Widows

Vrindavan: Faith, Abandonment, and the Long Wait

The book that accompanied the exhibition at the Serendipity Arts Festival, Goa, 2023, and subsequently at the Goethe-Institut, New Delhi. City of Widows brings together three months of photographic work in Vrindavan with personal testimonies from twelve of the women photographed, translated from Bengali, Odia, and Rajasthani. The book includes a legal appendix documenting Supreme Court orders related to the welfare of the widows of Vrindavan and an afterword by the social worker Sulabha Arya, who has worked in the city for thirty years.
