Desert Crossings

Migration routes across the Sahara and Thar deserts

The Essay

A desert crossing is not a metaphor. It is a physical act of desperation, endurance, and sometimes grace. These photographs were made in the margins of survival.

There is no romantic reading of these images. I offer them without commentary because I do not have the right to frame this suffering aesthetically. These are records.
