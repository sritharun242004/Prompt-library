Frequently asked questions

Still have questions? Chat with us.

Do I need a credit card to start?

No. Create your free account and start selling immediately. We only charge transaction fees when you make a sale.

What is a transaction fee?

A transaction fee is a small percentage of each sale, plus a flat $0.50. This covers payment processing, tax remittance, and platform costs.

Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?

Yes. You can change your plan at any time. Upgrades are prorated and take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at your next billing cycle.

Who handles taxes on my behalf?

Squeezed acts as the Merchant of Record. We calculate, collect, and remit VAT, GST, and US Sales Tax in 50+ countries — you never file a digital goods tax return.

Is there a free trial for Plus/Pro?

Yes. Plus and Pro plans include a 14-day free trial with full feature access. No credit card required to start the trial.

What payment methods do customers get?

All plans support credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal, SEPA Debit, and 15+ other payment methods across 130+ currencies.
