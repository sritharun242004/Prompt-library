Auric — Jewellery Flagship

ClientAuric Jewels
DisciplineInteriors
CategoryRetail
LocationNew Delhi, Delhi
Year2023
Area400 sqm
Synopsis

A 400 sqm jewellery flagship in Khan Market that uses light as its primary material. Display cases are lit from within; the ceiling is a field of suspended brass rods that diffuse and reflect. The store is organised as a sequence of intimate chambers rather than a single open floor — a deliberate strategy to slow the visitor's pace and encourage a contemplative relationship with the collection. Each chamber has a distinct ambient quality: one is dramatically dim with pooled light on individual pieces; another is day-lit through an alabaster screen that softens the harsh Delhi sun into a warm diffused glow. The entrance is designed as a moment of decompression — a narrow passage lined with woven silk panels that transition the visitor from the noise of the market to the quiet of the store.

Approach

Jewellery is about intimacy and touch. We designed the store to slow the visitor down — no immediate sightlines to the full collection, but a sequence of intimate alcoves that reward exploration. The display cases were custom-fabricated in polished sandstone with internal LED strips tuned to a colour temperature of 2700K — warm enough to enhance the gold tones of the jewellery without creating the overly theatrical atmosphere typical of luxury retail. The ceiling installation of 2,400 individually suspended brass rods was developed in collaboration with a Delhi metalsmith, who hand-finished each rod to create subtle variation in reflectivity. No two rods are identical. The result is a ceiling that reads as a unified field of light while rewarding close inspection with infinite detail.

Materials & Finishes

  • Polished sandstone
  • Handwoven silk wall panels
  • Solid brass
  • Museum-grade glass vitrines
  • Alabaster screens
