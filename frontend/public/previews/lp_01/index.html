FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Where is EventImpact 2025 held?

EventImpact 2025 takes place at Altice Arena and FIL Lisbon, Portugal — November 12–14, 2025.

Are there student or startup discounts?

Yes. Verified students receive 50% off General tickets. Accepted Startup Alpha companies receive two General passes complimentary.

Can I transfer my ticket to a colleague?

Tickets are transferable up to 14 days before the event. Log into your EventImpact account and use the Transfer Ticket flow.

What is the refund policy?

Full refunds are available up to 60 days before the event. After that, tickets may be transferred but not refunded.

Will sessions be live-streamed or recorded?

Selected keynotes are live-streamed free of charge. All 200+ sessions are available on-demand 30 days post-event via the EventImpact app.

How do I apply for the Startup Alpha programme?

Apply at eventimpact.com/alpha. The deadline is August 31, 2025. Accepted startups exhibit free and receive two General passes.
