Books

Authored Books

Three books of investigative narrative non-fiction. Published by Farrar, Straus and Giroux, Penguin Press, and Riverhead Books.

2019

The Paper Republic

Farrar, Straus and Giroux

A landmark investigation into America's immigration bureaucracy — how a system built on paper has become an instrument of mass exclusion. Drawing on 300 interviews and access to sealed court records, this book reconstructs the hidden architecture of a policy that has separated 70,000 families since 2014. Longlisted for the National Book Award. Named a Best Book of the Year by The New York Times, The Atlantic, and The Economist.

2021

The Price Equation

Penguin Press

How American pharmaceutical pricing became the most consequential — and least understood — public health crisis of our time. Following the money from patent offices to executive compensation committees, this book is both an investigative exposé and a moral reckoning. Winner of the Helen Bernstein Book Award for Excellence in Journalism. A Washington Post Notable Book.

Order Book →

2024

Borders of the Mind

Riverhead Books

A reported narrative spanning five countries and a decade of work on forced displacement, detention, and the psychology of belonging. From the shores of Lesbos to detention centres in Texas to resettlement apartments in Winnipeg, this book follows eight individuals through the grinding machinery of the global asylum system. Shortlisted for the Baillie Gifford Prize for Non-Fiction.
