The Migrant Detention Archipelago

Elena VasquezInvestigative Reporter · The Guardian

Investigation — Context

This is a long-form investigative piece. Reporting involved document review, FOIA requests, and on-the-record interviews. Source materials are available to credentialed journalists on request.

Inside the sprawling network of private detention facilities that now holds more than 40,000 migrants on any given day. Interviews with 87 detainees, 12 guards, and leaked internal records reveal systematic abuse, medical neglect, and a contractor model designed to profit from misery.
