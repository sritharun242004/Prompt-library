Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for students and parents. More questions? Contact us.

What is the admission process at AceInstitute?▾

Book a free counseling session. Our counselor evaluates your current level, recommends the right program and batch, and guides you through fee and scholarship options. Seat confirmation happens after fee payment.

What is the fee structure and are EMI options available?▾

Fees vary by program (from ₹45,000 for Olympiad to ₹1,85,000 for JEE Advanced 2-Year). EMI options are available for most programs through our banking partners at 0% interest for up to 6 months. Contact our counselors for exact fee breakdowns.

What scholarship concessions are available?▾

Students can earn up to 90% fee waiver through our Scholarship Cum Admission Test (SCAT). Concessions range from 10% to 90% based on your SCAT rank. Existing merit students from board exams may qualify for additional discounts.

Are online programs as effective as offline classroom programs?▾

Our online programs use the same faculty and curriculum as offline batches. With live doubt resolution, recorded access, and digital test platforms, online students have consistently matched offline students in results over the past 3 years.

What is the batch size and student-to-faculty ratio?▾

Regular batches have 30–45 students. Rankers' batches (top SCAT scorers) have 15–20 students. Doubt-clearing sessions are conducted in groups of 8–10 for personalized attention.

What is the refund policy?▾

Full refund within 7 days of payment if seat not yet allocated. Partial refund (50%) within 15 days. No refund after 15 days. Scholarship registration fees are non-refundable. Full details in our Terms & Conditions.

Still have questions? Our counselors are available Mon–Sat, 9am–7pm.
