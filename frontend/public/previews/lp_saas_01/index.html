Features

The tools high-performing engineering teams rely on.

IssuesCreate and track issues with priority levels, status workflows, and assignees that mirror how engineering teams actually work.
CyclesTime-boxed sprint planning with automatic rollover of incomplete work — no manual cleanup at the end of every iteration.
ProjectsGroup related issues into projects and projects into initiatives for full roadmap-level visibility across teams.
RoadmapA timeline view that reflects actual work in progress, not a slide deck that goes stale the day it is published.
AnalyticsBurndown, velocity, and throughput charts updated in real time so you know exactly where every cycle stands.
