Frequently Asked Questions

Who needs GST registration?

Any business with annual turnover above ₹20 lakhs (₹10 lakhs for NE states) must register for GST.

Can I apply voluntarily?

Yes, any business can register voluntarily even below the threshold to claim input tax credits.

How long does the GSTIN stay valid?

GSTIN has no expiry — it remains valid as long as you file returns regularly.

What if my application is rejected?

Our expert responds to deficiency notices. Covered under our 7-day money-back guarantee.
