The Chef

Arjun Mehta

Executive Chef & Founder

"Indian food is the most complex cuisine on earth. My work is to make that complexity feel effortless — to let a single bite carry forty years of memory and five hundred years of spice trade history without asking the guest to do the archaeology."

Chef Arjun Mehta trained under Paul Bocuse in Lyon before returning to New Delhi to open Indian Prestige in 2009. His cooking draws from classical French technique and the deep regional traditions of the Indian subcontinent — from Chettinad to Kashmir, from the Konkan coast to the Punjabi plains.

In 2015, Indian Prestige was awarded its first Michelin Star and ranked among Asia's 50 Best. The New York outpost followed in 2018, and Mumbai in 2022.
