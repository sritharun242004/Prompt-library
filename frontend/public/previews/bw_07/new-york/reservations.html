Reservation Policy

The following policies are in place to maintain the quality of the dining experience for all guests. We appreciate your understanding.

  • —Minimum age: Guests aged 8 years and above only. We are unable to accommodate younger children.
  • —Advance payment: 100% of the tasting menu fee is collected at the time of booking. This is applied to your final bill.
  • —Cancellation window: Cancellations made fewer than 48 hours before your reservation will forfeit the advance payment.
  • —Dietary requirements: Please communicate all dietary restrictions at the time of booking. We cannot guarantee accommodations made on the day of your visit.
