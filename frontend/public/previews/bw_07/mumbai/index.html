Current Menu

6-Course Chef’s Tasting

  1. 01

    Bombay Toast & Caviar

    Crispy Irani café-style Bombay toast, cultured butter, Osietra caviar, and a chilled glass of ginger-lemon rasam.

    Chef’s Note — A city snack elevated to its rightful position as an opening ceremony.

  2. 02

    Konkan Crab Bisque

    Mud crab bisque infused with Konkan kokum and raw mango, served with a crab claw fritter and fennel frond oil.

  3. 03

    Wild Boar Kheema

    Slow-minced Sahyadri wild boar, stone-ground spices, quail egg yolk, toasted brioche, and a papaya achaar.

    Chef’s Note — The kheema is finished in a copper kadai brought to the table.

  4. 04

    Kingfish Rechado

    Goan rechado spice-marinated kingfish, pan-seared to a mahogany crust, with a vinegar-braised cabbage roulade.

  5. 05

    Goat Shoulder Dhansak

    Twelve-hour braised Dharwad goat, heritage Parsi dhansak lentils, crispy taro, pomegranate molasses jus.

    Chef’s Note — A Sunday ritual from every Parsi household — made for a dining room.

  6. 06

    Sitaphal Soufflé

    Warm custard apple soufflé, cardamom crème anglaise, compressed grape, and a scoop of kokum sherbet.
