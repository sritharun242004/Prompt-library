Current Menu

6-Course Chef’s Tasting

  1. 01

    Blue Cheese Naan

    Tandoor-blistered mini naan filled with Roquefort and walnut, finished with truffle honey and edible gold leaf. A signature since the founding menu.

    Chef’s Note — The blue cheese is sourced from Auroville; the truffle arrives fresh every Thursday.

  2. 02

    Crispy Lamb Kidney

    Slow-braised lamb kidney encased in a turmeric-spiced cornmeal crust, served with a tamarind reduction and pickled shallot gel.

    Chef’s Note — Inspired by the street-side bheja fry of Old Delhi.

  3. 03

    Pork Belly Vindaloo

    Forty-eight hour sous-vide pork belly, Goan vindaloo spice paste, compressed cucumber, and a smear of coconut foam.

  4. 04

    Wild Mushroom Khichdi

    Morel and porcini khichdi served tableside with warm ghee, black truffle shavings, and 18-month aged Parmesan.

    Chef’s Note — A homage to the dish that every Indian grandmother cooks differently.

  5. 05

    Tandoori Bacon Prawns

    King prawns marinated in a smoked paprika-yoghurt base, wrapped in heritage Mangalitsa bacon, char-fired in the tandoor.

  6. 06

    Gulab Jamun Cheesecake

    A deconstructed dessert: warm gulab jamun soaked in rose syrup, resting on a compressed New York cheesecake, with saffron ice cream.

    Chef’s Note — The intersection of two cities, two pastry traditions, one plate.
