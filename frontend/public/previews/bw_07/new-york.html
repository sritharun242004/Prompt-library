6-Course Chef’s Tasting

  1. 01

    Lobster Moilee

    Maine lobster poached in a Kerala coconut milk moilee, served with tapioca pearls, curry leaf oil, and a crisp prawn wafer.

    Chef’s Note — New England meets the Malabar coast — two ocean traditions converge.

  2. 02

    Duck Tikka Masala

    Long Island duck breast, 72-hour tikka masala braise reduction, bitter orange chutney, and a micro herb salad of cilantro and fenugreek.

  3. 03

    Venison Rogan Josh

    Hudson Valley venison, slow-braised Kashmiri rogan josh, porcini-stuffed modi bread, turnip purée, and juniper berry jus.

    Chef’s Note — The elevation of the Kashmiri pantry into the Hudson Valley larder.

  4. 04

    Scallop Ceviche Chaat

    Cape Cod scallop carpaccio dressed in a tamarind-lime chaat masala, pomegranate pearls, and crispy amaranth puffs.

  5. 05

    Truffle Biryani

    Aged Basmati, French black truffle, slow-cooked short rib, dum-sealed with saffron milk, served with a crisp raita.

    Chef’s Note — The dum seal is broken at the table. The aroma is part of the dish.

  6. 06

    Mango Shrikhand Parfait

    Hung curd shrikhand layered with Alphonso mango sorbet, cardamom granola, and a dried rose petal tuile.
