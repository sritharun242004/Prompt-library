Split View. Two pages, one screen.

Open any two URLs side by side. Cross-reference sources, write while you read, or monitor dashboards while you code.

  • Horizontal or vertical split
  • Drag-to-resize panes
  • Works with all web apps
  • 3-way layout on wide screens
