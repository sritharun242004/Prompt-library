Lens Review

Helios 44-2 58mm f/2

58mm · f/2 · M42 (adapter) · 1975

4.5 / 5

The most charismatic lens in the Soviet optical arsenal. The swirly bokeh is real, usable, and unlike anything modern.

Pros

  • · Distinctive swirly bokeh at f/2
  • · Built like a tank
  • · Available for under $30
  • · Renders skin beautifully

Cons

  • · Focus ring direction is reversed
  • · Requires adapter and patience
  • · No autofocus
