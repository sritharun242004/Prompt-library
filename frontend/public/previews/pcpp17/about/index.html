About

We built the program we wish had existed when we were trying to make the leap.

ReforgeElite started as a conversation between a frustrated senior PM and a recently promoted VP who had learned everything the hard way. It has since become the most selective career program in tech.

Our story

Founded by operators, for operators

In 2023, Sarah Chen (then CPO at Google Shopping) and Marcus Rivera (then VP Growth at Airbnb) spent six months interviewing senior professionals who had recently made the leap from IC to exec or from senior to director.

The pattern was consistent: the skills that earned the senior title were not the skills needed for the next level. And no program existed to bridge that gap with real-world, operator-taught curriculum.

They built the first cohort of Product Strategy for Leaders in Q1 2024. It sold out in 48 hours. ReforgeElite has been oversubscribed ever since.

How we operate

Our principles

Operators only

Every instructor has shipped products, led teams, and made decisions at elite companies. We do not hire academics or consultants as instructors.

Small cohorts by design

Our cohort programs cap at 30–60 seats. This is not a constraint we are working around — it is the product. Quality of peer interaction matters.

Outcomes, not outputs

We measure success by career advancement, not completion certificates. 74% of cohort alumni report a title change or significant scope increase within 12 months.

High bar, high trust

We accept 42% of applicants. We turn down candidates who are not yet ready. This selectivity is what makes the alumni network valuable.
