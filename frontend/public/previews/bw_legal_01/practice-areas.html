Practice Areas

Full-service legal counsel across every major domain of Indian and international commercial law, delivered by practice groups of acknowledged depth and experience.

The firm operates through eight principal practice groups, each led by partners with substantial experience in their respective disciplines. Our practices are deeply integrated, enabling us to bring coherent and comprehensive counsel to complex, multi-disciplinary matters.

Mergers & Acquisitions

Our M&A practice advises on the full spectrum of domestic and cross-border transactions, from initial structuring and regulatory approvals to post-closing integration. We have advised on some of India's most consequential corporate combinations, spanning public takeovers, private equity buyouts, and strategic divestments across regulated and unregulated sectors.

Learn More

Banking & Finance

Our Banking & Finance practice provides comprehensive counsel to domestic and international banks, development finance institutions, alternative investment funds, and corporate borrowers on the full range of financing transactions. Our practice encompasses project finance, syndicated lending, structured products, insolvency-linked financing, and regulatory compliance for financial institutions regulated by the RBI and SEBI.

Learn More

Capital Markets

We advise issuers, investment banks, and institutional investors on the complete lifecycle of equity and debt capital market transactions, from pre-IPO structuring to post-listing compliance. Our practice is consistently ranked among the most active in India by deal count and transaction value, with deep expertise in SEBI regulations, FEMA, and international securities law.

Learn More

Dispute Resolution

Our Dispute Resolution practice represents clients before the Supreme Court of India, High Courts, the National Company Law Tribunal, and leading arbitral institutions including the ICC, SIAC, LCIA, and the Mumbai Centre for International Arbitration. We combine deep litigation expertise with a thorough understanding of commercial law to deliver robust advocacy in bet-the-company disputes.

Learn More

Competition Law

Our Competition Law practice advises on the full range of matters regulated by the Competition Commission of India, including merger control filings, anti-competitive conduct investigations, dawn raids, and competition compliance programmes. We are consistently instructed on the most complex and high-profile CCI proceedings, and we advise regularly on the intersection of competition law with other regulatory frameworks.

Learn More

Real Estate

Our Real Estate practice advises developers, investors, lenders, and government entities on acquisitions, joint ventures, structured finance, RERA compliance, and the leasing of large commercial assets. We have particular depth in transactions involving large-format commercial real estate, data centres, logistics parks, and government land development projects.

Learn More

Employment & Labour

Our Employment & Labour practice advises domestic and multinational employers on the full range of workforce matters, from individual employment contracts and executive remuneration to trade union negotiations, retrenchment, and employment-related litigation. We maintain particular expertise in the labour law implications of corporate transactions and workforce restructuring.

Learn More

Tax

Our Tax practice advises domestic and international clients on the full spectrum of direct and indirect taxation, including corporate tax planning, transfer pricing, GST, customs, and FEMA. We represent clients before the Income Tax Appellate Tribunal, High Courts, and the Supreme Court, and advise extensively on the tax structuring of M&A transactions, fund formations, and cross-border investments.
