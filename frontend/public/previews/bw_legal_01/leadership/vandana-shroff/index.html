Profile

Vandana Shroff is a Senior Partner and head of the firm's Capital Markets practice. She advises issuers, investment banks, and institutional investors on IPOs, QIPs, debt capital market transactions, and SEBI regulatory compliance. She has served as a member of the SEBI Primary Market Advisory Committee and the SEBI Takeover Regulations Advisory Committee.

Vandana Shroff is independently recognised by Chambers Asia-Pacific, The Legal 500 Asia Pacific, and IFLR1000. Rankings are based on peer assessment, client feedback, and transaction record.
