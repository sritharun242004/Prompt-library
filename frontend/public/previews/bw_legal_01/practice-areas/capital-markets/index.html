Capital Markets

Overview

We advise issuers, investment banks, and institutional investors on the complete lifecycle of equity and debt capital market transactions, from pre-IPO structuring to post-listing compliance. Our practice is consistently ranked among the most active in India by deal count and transaction value, with deep expertise in SEBI regulations, FEMA, and international securities law.
