Employment & Labour

Overview

Our Employment & Labour practice advises domestic and multinational employers on the full range of workforce matters, from individual employment contracts and executive remuneration to trade union negotiations, retrenchment, and employment-related litigation. We maintain particular expertise in the labour law implications of corporate transactions and workforce restructuring.
