# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## Kit-Style Creator Storefront · dpecom_platform_03

### 1. Product Vision
A "Kit-Style" platform that treats every sale as an opportunity to build a long-term relationship. It moves away from the transactional nature of traditional e-commerce to a model where list-building and digital sales are unified. The design signals "Creative Trust" through high-readability typography and a warm, minimalist aesthetic.

**Success Metric:** 70% of buyers also opt-in to the creator's email list during checkout.

### 2. Personas
**Sam — The Writer (Creator)**
- Writes a popular weekly newsletter and wants to sell a "Best of" ebook.
- Needs: A simple way to pitch the book within the newsletter and capture new emails on the sales page.
- Frustration: Overly complex store builders that don't integrate with their email list.

**Alex — The Supportive Fan (Buyer)**
- Loves Sam's writing and wants to support their work via a Tip Jar or Ebook purchase.
- Needs: A 1-click experience that feels personal, not corporate.
- Frustration: Creating accounts just to buy a $10 PDF.

### 3. Core Features
- **Integrated Email Capture:** Sales pages that double as high-conversion newsletter opt-ins.
- **Virtual Tip Jar:** A frictionless component for fan contributions ($5, $10, $25, Custom).
- **Automated Fulfillment:** Instant delivery of digital assets via Resend immediately post-purchase.
- **Audience Tagging:** Automatically tag buyers in the database (e.g., "Customer: Ebook") for future segmentation.
- **Audience-First Checkout:** A 1-page checkout with a prominent "Join the newsletter" opt-in.

### 4. User Journeys
1. **Growth:** Visitor lands on page -> sees lead magnet -> subscribes -> receives welcome email + product pitch.
2. **Support:** Fan clicks "Tip" -> selects amount -> completes payment -> receives "Thank You" email with heart animation.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No subscription management for physical goods.
- No complex marketplace discovery (Focus is on the creator's own audience).
- No advanced revenue analytics beyond basic sales tracking.

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** Clean Creative. Serif headings, 720px max-width, warm neutrals.
- **Tone:** Human and inviting. First-person copy.
- **Tech:** Resend integration is mandatory for the "Automated Fulfillment" story.
