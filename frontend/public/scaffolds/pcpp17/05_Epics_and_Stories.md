# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · pcpp_platform_17

### Epic 1: Immediate Credibility
*As a visitor, I want a homepage that immediately establishes the platform's industry authority.*
- **Story 1.1:** View a horizontal strip of prestigious tech alumni logos (Google, Meta, etc.).
- **Story 1.2:** See high-impact heros showcasing real artifacts from world-class teams.
- **Story 1.3:** Experience a clean, "Professional" layout that respects my time and intelligence.

### Epic 2: Evidence-Based Learning (Artifacts)
*As an operator, I want to see the actual documentation used by top-tier teams.*
- **Story 2.1:** Search a library of 1,000+ artifacts by keyword, company, or document type.
- **Story 2.2:** View a visual preview of a high-value strategy doc before joining.
- **Story 2.3:** Understand the context of an artifact through operator-provided metadata.

### Epic 3: Structured Career Growth
*As a student, I want to follow a curriculum taught by real-world operators.*
- **Story 3.1:** Browse course modules that transition from theory to document-backed practice.
- **Story 3.2:** View "Operator Host" profiles to verify the host's current industry relevance.
- **Story 3.3:** Read high-authority testimonials from peers at other top-tier companies.

### Epic 4: Elite Community Access
*As a member, I want a frictionless way to access premium documents and tools.*
- **Story 4.1:** Upgrade to a full membership in under 60 seconds via a secure checkout.
- **Story 4.2:** Access "Unlimited" document downloads and AI-assisted synthesis tools.
- **Story 4.3:** Manage my membership and team billing through a professional self-service portal.
