# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## ReforgeElite Professional Growth Hub · pcpp_platform_17

### 1. Product Vision
"ReforgeElite" is a digital hallmark for career acceleration in tech. It moves beyond the generic "e-learning" site to create an "Evidence-Based" environment where professional growth is driven by real-world artifacts (strategy docs, PRDs) and direct mentorship from industry leaders. The platform prioritizes high-signal credibility and data-dense curricula, providing senior operators with a digital presence that signals "Top 1%" relevance and technical mastery.

**Success Metric:** Average "Artifact Engagement" (percentage of users who interact with at least 3 document previews per session) exceeds 40%.

### 2. Personas
**Marcus — The Senior Product Leader (User)**
- Works at a high-growth startup and needs to master "AI Strategy" or "Retention Loops."
- Needs: A fast, authoritative way to access proven documents from companies like Uber or Notion and a clear path to join a live cohort.
- Frustration: Superficial courses that offer theory without the actual documentation used to build successful products.

**Elena — The CPO / Talent Seeker (Client)**
- Looking for a structured training platform for her entire product team.
- Needs: Immediate proof of "Host Authority," evidence of a high-quality peer network, and a seamless team-onboarding flow.
- Frustration: Disorganized curricula and lack of corporate social proof (missing logos of top tech firms).

### 3. Core Features
- **High-Speed Artifact Library:** A searchable repository of 1,000+ real-world professional documents with high-fidelity visual previews.
- **Operator-Led Curriculum Hub:** A dense, hierarchical layout for courses featuring host portraits, module breakdowns, and "Live vs. On-Demand" badges.
- **Prestigious Alumni Wall:** A horizontal strip of grayscale logos from top-tier tech companies to establish immediate authority.
- **Member Access Portal:** A tiered membership layer (Free Trial vs. Full Access) integrated with secure document gating and AI synthesis tools.
- **High-Authority Testimonials:** Large-scale quote cards featuring C-level headshots and corporate logo anchors.

### 4. User Journeys
1. **The Document Hunt:** Visitor lands on "Artifacts" -> searches for "Pricing Strategy" -> views a preview of a Slack PRD -> sees a "Join Membership to Unlock" CTA.
2. **The Curriculum Audit:** User clicks "AI Product Management" -> scans the 8-module breakdown -> reviews the "Host Operator" profiles -> clicks "Start Free Trial."
3. **The Team Onboarding:** Team lead clicks "For Teams" -> views the dashboard preview -> initiates a demo request via a high-contrast inquiry form.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No "Public Forum" or open-to-all social wall (Maintain the elite, peer-to-peer exclusivity).
- No standard "Stock" student photography (Use professional operator portraits and real doc previews).
- No low-value "Completion Certificates" (Focus on actual skill acquisition and document access).

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** Professional Tech Editorial. Pure white, sharp Inter typography, high-signal information density.
- **Search:** Artifact search must be near-instant (Algolia target: <100ms) to maintain professional utility.
- **Security:** Premium artifacts must be protected by server-side session checks to prevent bulk scraping.
