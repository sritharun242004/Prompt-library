# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## TropicalMinimalist Lifestyle Hub · bw_platform_04

### 1. Product Vision
"TropicalMinimalist" is a digital hallmark for modern lifestyle retail. It moves beyond standard e-commerce to create an "Atmospheric Hub" where fashion, home, and travel intersect through high-end storytelling. The platform prioritizes the "Art of Discovery" through editorial content and personalized gifting, providing brands with a digital presence that signals "Island Sophistication" and "Modern Utility."

**Success Metric:** 30% of sessions include cross-category browsing (e.g., a user views an apparel item and then a home decor item via the "Pair it with" engine).

### 2. Personas
**Rahul — The Creative Nomad (Client)**
- Works remotely and values high-quality, breezy materials for his travels and home studio.
- Needs: Immediate proof of "Modern Utility" and a scannable way to find unique, non-corporate gift items.
- Frustration: Cluttered, high-pressure retail sites that lack cultural depth or lifestyle context.

**Saira — The Mindful Host (Audience)**
- Loves hosting at home and seeks a curated, aesthetic cohesive between her barware and her wardrobe.
- Needs: Inspiration from "The Journal" and a simple way to "Shop the Set" or "Shop the Look" from lifestyle imagery.
- Frustration: Disjointed categories and the inability to see how home products complement a personal style.

### 3. Core Features
- **Airy Lifestyle Hero Engine:** A high-impact banner system for showcasing seasonal collections and editorial themes.
- **"Nico Journal" CMS:** A multi-media storytelling layer featuring travelogues, playlists (Nico Radio), and interviews.
- **Cross-Category "Pairing" Engine:** A discovery logic that surfaces home items on fashion pages and vice versa based on collection tags.
- **Gifting Concierge Hub:** A specialized area for curated gift advice, price-point grids, and corporate gifting.
- **Integrated "Shop the Look":** Direct-buy points within high-res lifestyle and editorial photography.

### 4. User Journeys
1. **The Inspiration Path:** Visitor lands on home -> clicks a "Nico Journal" story about Udaipur -> listens to a playlist -> clicks "Shop the Look" -> adds a linen kurta and a brass kulhar to their bag.
2. **The Gifting Path:** User enters "The Gift Shop" -> filters by "Housewarming" and "Under 5k" -> views a curated grid -> selects a barware set -> adds a personalized gift card.
3. **The Utility Discovery:** User browses the "Travel Edit" -> sees a set of bags and organizers -> uses the "Pair it with" carousel to find matching accessories.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No "Flash Sales" or aggressive discount banners (Focus is on "Slow Living").
- No community "Social Feed" (Maintain the curated, editorial voice).
- No complex "Home Design" tool (Focus is on curated retail items).

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** Tropical Minimalism. Airy whitespace, neutral earth tones, bold all-caps typography.
- **Performance:** Sub-1s LCP for the primary homepage hero (Next.js optimization).
- **Data:** Taxonomy must be "Collection-First" (Metadata links items across fashion, home, and travel).
