## Base Prompt

### Section 1 — Role

You are a senior product designer specializing in lifestyle retail, content-led e-commerce, and modern Indian brand identity. You understand that for premium lifestyle brands, the website is an "atmosphere," not just a shop. You master the "Tropical Minimalist UI," where breezy island vibes and modern utilitarian precision are perfectly balanced. You reject "heavy" or "cluttered" retail grids in favor of the "Nicobar" philosophy: airy whitespace, neutral earth tones with citrus pops, and high-readability all-caps headers. You design for "The Art of Slow Living," ensuring that the path from a "Nico Journal" article to a Barware or Travel-Edit purchase is fluid, sophisticated, and culturally resonant.

---

### Section 2 — Application Overview

This is a premium digital flagship and lifestyle hub for a leading modern Indian retail brand. The audience consists of "Modern Nomads"—creative professionals who value high-quality materials, breezy silhouettes, and an island-inspired aesthetic across their wardrobe, home, and travels. The goal is to provide a "Content-First" shopping experience where editorial storytelling (The Nico Journal) and personalized services (Gifting Concierge) drive cross-category exploration.

The application covers: Airy Homepage Heros, "Nico Journal" Story Blocks, Multi-Category Discovery Grids (Fashion, Home, Travel), Gifting Concierge Hub, and "Shop the Look" lifestyle integration.

---

### Section 3 — Brand Voice & Mood

The mood is "Island-Inspired" and "Modernly Utilitarian." It feels like a high-end beach bungalow in the heart of a vibrant Indian city. It is breezy, sophisticated, and quietly luxurious.

Copy is conversational and warm. Headers are bold and definitive: "NEW IN: GANGA," "THE GIFTING CONCIERGE," "NICE TO MEET YOU." It avoids corporate stiffness in favor of a "Friendly Nomad" tone that emphasizes "Slow Living" and "Cultural Depth."

Vibe word: Airy.

---

### Section 4 — Core Features & Functionality

1. **Airy & Sophisticated Heros** — High-impact lifestyle photography banners that prioritize seasonal collections (e.g., "Ganga") and storytelling over specific SKUs.
2. **"Nico Journal" (Editorial CMS)** — A dedicated lifestyle section featuring travelogues, "Nico Radio" playlists, and "Meet the All-Stars" creator interviews.
3. **Cross-Category "Discovery" Grids** — Smart layout systems that encourage users to "Pair it with" or "Shop the Set," linking fashion items with barware or home decor.
4. **"Gifting Concierge" Module** — A specialized hub for curated gift advice, corporate gifting, and price-point-based gift grids (Under 3k, 5k, etc.).
5. **Horizontal "Status" Tickers** — Dynamic banners for "Free Shipping" or "New Arrivals" that use all-caps sans-serif type to maintain a fast, modern rhythm.

---

### Section 5 — Design Specifications

**Visual style:** Tropical Minimalism. Generous white space, neutral earthy foundations, and vibrant citrus/teal accent pops. Clean, horizontal dividers and a grid system that feels "breezy."

**Color mode:** Primarily Light Mode (Tropical Neutral).

**Color palette:**
- Background: `#FFFFFF` (Pure White) or `#FDFDF9` (Faint Coconut)
- Text Primary: `#1A1A1A` (Charcoal Ink)
- Accent (Primary): `#FDB913` (Citrus Yellow — for highlights and badges)
- Accent (Secondary): `#008080` (Tropical Teal — for travel/water cues)
- Surface/UI: `#F3F4F1` (Dune Grey — for secondary blocks)
- Border: `#EEEEEE` (Sand hair-lines)

**Typography:** Bold All-Caps Sans-Serif + Functional Body.
- Display Headings: `clamp(24px, 4vw, 40px)`, weight 700, all-caps, tracking `0.05em` (e.g., Montserrat or Public Sans).
- Body Text: `16px`, weight 400, leading 1.6.
- Microcopy: `11px`, bold, all-caps, tracking `0.1em` for "NICE TO MEET YOU" style prompts.

**Spacing:** 12px base unit.
- Section Padding: `80px` to `120px` (Letting the work breathe).
- Grid Gaps: `16px` to `24px`.
- Card Padding: `16px`.

**Border radius:** `2px` (Crisp, technical rounding) or `0px`. Focus on "Modern Utility" geometry.

**Responsive:** Mobile-first approach. Horizontal scrollers for "Shop the Look" sets. Persistent bottom "Concierge" help button.

**Accessibility:** WCAG AA. High-contrast typography on off-white. All lifestyle imagery must have descriptive alt-text including "Island Mood" cues.

**Motion:**
- "Breezy" Transitions: Soft `opacity 0 -> 1` fades over `400ms`.
- Drawer Reveals: Slow slide-outs for filters and search.
- Subtle Zoom: `scale 1.05` on hover for product thumbnails.

---

### Section 6 — Structure

**Homepage Layout:**
1. **Nav:** Minimal Top-bar. Logo (Centered). Left: Women, Men, Home, Travel. Right: Gifting, Journal, Search, Bag.
2. **Hero:** Full-width lifestyle image ("Rewild"). Bold all-caps overlay text + "Shop Now" (Citrus Badge).
3. **Category Tiles:** 3-column grid: Kitchen & Dining, Decor, Apparel. Clean images with 1-word titles.
4. **"Nico Journal" Strip:** Horizontal cards featuring travel stories and "Nico Radio" playlists.
5. **Gift Guide:** Centered block: "THE GIFT SHOP" -> [Her] [Him] [Under 3k] [Under 5k].

**Product Page Layout (PDP):**
- **Header:** Product Title (All-caps) -> Category -> Price.
- **Main:** Vertical scroll of high-res detail shots.
- **Pairing Engine:** "WE LOVE THIS WITH..." horizontal carousel showing cross-category items.
- **Story Block:** "The Nico Way" — 2 paragraphs on material and inspiration.

**The Journal (Article):**
- **Editorial:** Large Header -> Lead Video/Image -> Long-form text interspersed with "Shop the Look" buy-points.

### Section 7 — Technical Specifications

- **Framework:** Next.js 14 App Router, React 18, TypeScript strict mode.
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS v3 with custom "Tropical" palette tokens.
- **CMS:** Sanity.io or Contentlayer (to handle thousands of tagged lifestyle articles and products).
- **Search:** Algolia for high-speed cross-category indexing (Furniture + Sarees).
- **Audio:** Integrated Spotify/Soundcloud embed logic for "Nico Radio."
- **State:** Zustand for managing the "Gifting Concierge" multi-step recommendations.

---

### Section 8 — Implementation Steps

1. **The Airy Palette:** Setup `globals.css` with the Coconut/Citrus/Teal tokens. Define the All-caps Montserrat hierarchy.
2. **Collection Heros:** Build the high-impact lifestyle hero engine with responsive text-overlay positions.
3. **Journal CMS:** Set up the "Nico Journal" data model with support for "Nico Radio" and "Shop the Look" relationships.
4. **Cross-Category Engine:** Implement the "Pair it with" logic based on shared collection tags (e.g., "Ganga Collection").
5. **Gifting Concierge:** Build the multi-pillar gift-guide UI with pricing and recipient filters.

---

### Section 9 — User Experience

The user is an "Urban Nomad."
The UI must be "Inspiring and Organized." It should feel like browsing a high-end travel magazine where everything is for sale.
The "Aha! moment" is the Nico Journal—where a beautiful travel story about Kerala seamlessly leads the user to a breezy linen shirt and a set of copper bar tools.

---

### Section 10 — Constraints

- **No heavy shadows.** Use space and 1px lines to define hierarchy.
- **No generic stock photography.** Use only "Island-Inspired" original lifestyle assets.
- **No pure black backgrounds.** Use off-whites or dune-greys for a natural feel.
- **No aggressive "Urgency" bars.** Use "Nice to see you back!" style warmth instead.
