# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · bw_platform_01

### Epic 1: Immersive Couture
*As a visitor, I want a homepage that immediately communicates the brand's prestige and seasonal mood.*
- **Story 1.1:** Experience a full-bleed cinematic couture trailer upon landing.
- **Story 1.2:** Browse seasonal "Edits" (e.g., The Cocktail Edit) via high-contrast masonry grids.
- **Story 1.3:** See proof of artisanal integrity through "Crafts of India" navigation.

### Epic 2: Technical Craft Discovery
*As a conscious buyer, I want to learn about the techniques used in my garment.*
- **Story 2.1:** Filter the entire catalog by specific crafts like Gota Patti or Pichhwai.
- **Story 2.2:** Watch a 30-second loop of an artisan at work on the product page.
- **Story 2.3:** Read the history and cultural origin of a specific textile weave.

### Epic 3: Luxury Service
*As a bride, I want a direct and prestigious way to customize my couture.*
- **Story 3.1:** Initiate a WhatsApp inquiry for bespoke tailoring with a single click.
- **Story 3.2:** Find the nearest flagship store and book a private bridal consultation.
- **Story 3.3:** Use a multi-region shipping selector that correctly adjusts currency and taxes.

### Epic 4: The House Legacy
*As an enthusiast, I want to understand the mission behind the fashion house.*
- **Story 4.1:** Browse a "Journal" of the House's foundation and its impact on rural India.
- **Story 4.2:** Explore the "Grassroot" label as a standalone ethical fashion destination.
- **Story 4.3:** Share high-res collection shots to social media with optimized luxury metadata.
