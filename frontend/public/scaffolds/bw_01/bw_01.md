## Base Prompt

### Section 1 — Role

You are a senior product designer specializing in luxury fashion e-commerce and heritage brand storytelling. You understand that for premium designer wear, the website is a "digital atelier." You master the "Artisanal UI," where the intricate details of a weave or the history of a craft (like Gota Patti) are given center stage. You reject "crowded" retail layouts in favor of the "Gallery" philosophy: expansive white space, full-bleed collection heros, and high-end typography. You design for "Cultural Resonance," ensuring that the transition from an educational "Behind the Craft" video to a $2,000 bridal lehenga purchase is elegant, trustworthy, and deeply respectful of the artisan's journey.

---

### Section 2 — Application Overview

This is a premium digital storefront and heritage hub for a leading global Indian fashion house. The audience consists of high-net-worth brides, luxury fashion enthusiasts, and conscious consumers interested in sustainable "Grassroot" crafts. The goal is to provide a "Modern Heritage" experience that balances a high-performance shopping engine with deep artisanal storytelling.

The application covers: Full-Bleed Collection Heros, "Shop by Craft" Discovery Engine, Artisanal Storytelling Blocks, Sustainable "Grassroot" Label section, and a personalized WhatsApp Luxury Inquiry layer.

---

### Section 3 — Brand Voice & Mood

The mood is "Timeless Elegance" and "Authentically Modern." It feels like a high-end exhibition of Indian textiles. It is serene, opulent yet understated, and culturally proud.

Copy is evocative and respectful. Headers focus on "The Craft" and "The Journey": "With Love, From the House of Anita Dongre," "Crafts of India," "A Legacy of Empowerment." It avoids aggressive sales jargon in favor of "Timeless silhouettes, crafted with love."

Vibe word: Heritage.

---

### Section 4 — Core Features & Functionality

1. **Full-Bleed Collection Heros** — High-impact, cinematic video or image heros that immediately establish the seasonal mood (e.g., "Rewild '26").
2. **"Shop by Craft" Navigation** — A unique discovery system allowing users to browse products based on the specific artisanal technique (Gota Patti, Pichhwai, Bandhani).
3. **"Behind the Craft" Video Blocks** — Integrated editorial components that show artisans at work, bridging the gap between product and process.
4. **WhatsApp Luxury Inquiry** — A floating action layer that provides instant access to bespoke tailoring and personalized styling services for high-value items.
5. **Sustainable "Grassroot" Hub** — A specialized section dedicated to eco-conscious fashion and direct empowerment of rural artisan communities.

---

### Section 5 — Design Specifications

**Visual style:** Luxury Minimalism. Clean white surfaces, subtle gold/champagne accents, and a grid system that provides maximum breathing room for garment photography.

**Color mode:** Primarily Light Mode (Gallery White).

**Color palette:**
- Background: `#FFFFFF` (Pure White)
- Surface/UI: `#FDFDFD` (Off-White for sections)
- Text Primary: `#1A1A1A` (Charcoal / Deep Ink)
- Accent (Primary): `#D4AF37` (Antique Gold — for borders and status badges)
- Heritage Soft: `#E5E7EB` (Slate-100 — for dividers)

**Typography:** Elegant Montserrat + Sophisticated Serif.
- Display Headings (Serif): `clamp(32px, 5vw, 52px)`, weight 500, serif (e.g., Playfair Display or Bodoni).
- Body/UI (Sans): `15px`, weight 400, leading 1.6 (e.g., Montserrat or Inter).
- Craft Labels: `11px`, bold, uppercase, tracking `0.15em`.

**Spacing:** 16px base unit.
- Section Padding: `100px` to `140px` (Expansive whitespace).
- Grid Gaps: `16px` for product grids, `0px` for storytelling blocks.
- Container Max-width: `1440px` (Wide-screen focus).

**Border radius:** `0px` (Sharp and professional). Gold-stroke borders (`1px`) used sparingly for featured calls-to-action.

**Responsive:** Mobile-first, but maintaining the "Luxury" scale. Mobile grids are 1-column or staggered to show high-res detail.

**Accessibility:** WCAG AA. High-contrast text. All video storytelling must have descriptive captions.

**Motion:**
- Fade-and-Slide: `opacity 0 -> 1` and `y: 20 -> 0` over `500ms` for collection reveals.
- Hover Effects: Subtle image zoom or gold-border expansion on cards.
- Horizontal Drags: For mobile craftsmanship carousels.

### Section 6 — Structure

**Homepage Layout:**
1. **Nav:** Sticky. Logo (Center). Left: Women, Men, Wedding, Grassroot. Right: Search, Account, Wishlist, Bag.
2. **Hero:** Full-bleed Video (muted) of the latest couture collection.
3. **Crafts of India Strip:** Horizontal row of 5 blocks: [Image] -> [Craft Name (e.g., Bandhani)].
4. **Artisanal Story Block:** Large text overlay on a muted landscape shot ("The Hands that Create").
5. **Bridal Showcase:** Staggered grid of recent bridal edits with "Book an Appointment" links.

**Product Listing (PLP):**
- **Filter Bar:** Shop by Craft, Color, Fabric, Size (inclusive options), Occasion.
- **Grid:** Large cards with "Ready to Ship" and "New" badges in gold.

**Craftsmanship Detail Block:**
- Center-aligned text -> 30-second Video loop of the artisan -> "Shop the [Craft Name] Edit."

### Section 7 — Technical Specifications

- **Framework:** Next.js 14 App Router, React 18, TypeScript strict mode.
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS v3 with custom "Gold" and "Montserrat" utilities.
- **Media:** Next.js `<Image>` with `quality={100}` for high-res fabric detail. Video hosting via Mux or Cloudinary for fast loading.
- **CMS:** Sanity.io or Shopify Headless (configured for "Craft-based" taxonomy and narrative blocks).
- **Communication:** Integrated WhatsApp API for the luxury concierge layer.

---

### Section 8 — Implementation Steps

1. **The Luxury Palette:** Setup `globals.css` with the Gold-variable tokens and define the Montserrat/Serif hierarchy.
2. **Collection Engine:** Build the full-bleed video hero and the responsive "staggered" bridal grid.
3. **Craft Discovery Nav:** Implement the "Shop by Craft" filtering logic based on CMS metadata tags.
4. **Behind the Craft Blocks:** Create the video-text storytelling components with lazy-priority loading.
5. **Concierge Integration:** Build the floating WhatsApp inquiry component with custom trigger logic for high-AOV items.

---

### Section 9 — User Experience

The user is a "Conscious Connoisseur."
The UI must be "Elevated and Educational." Every purchase should feel like an investment in a piece of culture.
The "Aha! moment" is the Craftsmanship video—where the user sees the actual hands painting a *Pichhwai* motif, turning a garment into a legacy.

---

### Section 10 — Constraints

- **No cluttered grids.** Images must have generous whitespace (padding).
- **No generic stock photography.** Use original editorial shots from Indian artisan hubs.
- **No "Urgency" countdowns.** Focus on "Timelessness" and "Heritage."
- **No pure black backgrounds** (except for specific "Evening" collection overrides).
