# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## HeritageLuxury Designer Hub · bw_platform_01

### 1. Product Vision
"HeritageLuxury" is a digital hallmark for world-class fashion houses. It moves beyond standard e-commerce to create a "Digital Atelier" where traditional Indian crafts are documented and celebrated. The platform prioritizes high-end visual storytelling and personalized luxury service, providing elite designers with a digital presence that matches the opulence and integrity of their physical boutiques.

**Success Metric:** 20% of users engage with a "Behind the Craft" video before initiating a high-AOV purchase or inquiry.

### 2. Personas
**Sonia — The HNW Bride (Client)**
- Planning a multi-day luxury wedding and needs a custom-fitted couture wardrobe.
- Needs: Immediate proof of "Artisanal Integrity" and a direct, prestigious way to book consultations.
- Frustration: Sites that look like generic retail and don't provide the personalized attention of a designer.

**Arjun — The Conscious Connoisseur (Audience)**
- Values sustainable luxury and wants to understand the origin of the textiles he wears.
- Needs: Educational content on specific crafts (Gota Patti, Bandhani) and evidence of fair-wage artisan support.
- Frustration: Lack of transparency in sourcing and poor-quality visual documentation of the craft.

### 3. Core Features
- **Cinematic Collection Engine:** A full-bleed video/image hero system for showcasing seasonal couture and bridal edits.
- **"Shop by Craft" Discovery:** A unique navigation pillar that filters the catalog by artisanal technique rather than just product type.
- **Artisanal Storytelling Blocks:** Interspersed editorial components that blend high-end photography with the history of the House and its Foundation.
- **"Behind the Craft" Theater:** A dedicated video layer within product pages showing manual artisanal work.
- **Concierge Inquiry Portal:** A specialized WhatsApp-integrated layer for bespoke tailoring and luxury styling advice.

### 4. User Journeys
1. **The Couture Search:** Visitor lands on home -> watches the cinematic hero -> scrolls to "Crafts of India" -> selects "Pichhwai" -> browses a collection of hand-painted masterpieces.
2. **The Bridal Booking:** Bride-to-be explores the "Wedding" tab -> views the *Rewild* edit -> clicks "Book an Appointment" -> receives a personalized invitation to the nearest flagship.
3. **The Sustainable Discovery:** Ethical consumer enters "Grassroot" -> reads the story of a specific village cluster -> purchases a craft-direct garment.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No "Bargain" or "Discount" sections (Focus is strictly on premium value).
- No community "Social Feed" (Maintain the curated, high-end voice).
- No complex "Customizer" tool in Phase 1 (Bespoke is handled via inquiry).

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** Luxury Minimalism. Pure white, sharp corners, gold structural accents.
- **Performance:** Sub-1s LCP for the first 3 collection heros (Next.js optimization).
- **Data:** Taxonomy must be "Craft-First" (Metadata links products to villages and techniques).
