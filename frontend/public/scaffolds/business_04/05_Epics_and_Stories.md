# 05 — Epics and Stories
## Career Acceleration & Tech Upskilling Platform · business_platform_04

---

### Epic 1 — Placement Proof

Story: working professionals and graduates can verify salary outcomes before engaging with forms.

Acceptance Criteria:
- placement metrics display cohort year and program context labels
- alumni story cards show before/after salary, company, and program
- company logo grid shows hiring partner names with accessible alt text
- placements page filterable by cohort year and program track
- methodology note explains how metrics are calculated

---

### Epic 2 — Program and Track Clarity

Story: visitors can identify the right upskilling track for their current role and career goal.

Acceptance Criteria:
- program index lists all tracks with outcome summary and duration
- each track detail page shows curriculum modules, learning format, and cohort outcome data
- mode (live / hybrid) and schedule clearly displayed per program
- program grid navigable on mobile with no horizontal overflow

---

### Epic 3 — Mentor Credibility

Story: visitors can evaluate mentor quality before committing to a program.

Acceptance Criteria:
- mentor cards show current company, role, years of experience, and specialization
- 1:1 session model vs recorded format explained clearly
- mentor-to-student ratio displayed
- mentor directory searchable/filterable by specialization on the mentors page

---

### Epic 4 — Scholarship Assessment

Story: eligible candidates can complete an assessment, see their scholarship tier, and proceed to enrollment.

Acceptance Criteria:
- assessment steps render correctly and progress indicator is visible
- scoring validated server-side (client cannot manipulate tier result)
- scholarship tier revealed with percentage and eligible program list
- enrollment CTA immediately follows tier reveal
- assessment completion event tracked with tier and program context

---

### Epic 5 — Enrollment Funnel

Story: prospects can book a demo, submit an inquiry, or begin enrollment with minimal friction.

Acceptance Criteria:
- demo booking form collects program interest, current role, experience, city, and contact
- inquiry form submits with full CRM context payload
- fee/EMI section shows total fee, EMI options, and financing partner names before form submission
- confirmation shows counselor assignment or callback time estimate
- all lead payloads include source, program, and background context

---

### Epic 6 — Support and Policy Clarity

Story: prospects can access refund, placement support, and EMI policy before enrolling.

Acceptance Criteria:
- refund and cancellation terms accessible within 2 clicks from demo or enrollment form
- placement support policy (what support is provided post-enrollment) clearly stated
- FAQ addresses top 10 pre-enrollment and post-enrollment questions
- contact path visible with phone, WhatsApp, and email options
