# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · pcpp_platform_09

### Epic 1: Deep Reading Mastery
*As a reader, I want a digital space that respects my attention and sustains my focus.*
- **Story 1.1:** Read 3,000+ word essays without eye strain or UI distractions.
- **Story 1.2:** Enjoy high-end book typography with perfect leading and margins.
- **Story 1.3:** See metadata (Date/Category) in a quiet, non-obtrusive format.

### Epic 2: Intellectual Discovery
*As a lifelong learner, I want to follow a "thread" of ideas across different disciplines.*
- **Story 2.1:** Discover related essays through the "Complement this..." section.
- **Story 2.2:** Browse the entire archive by subject (e.g., On Wonder, On Time).
- **Story 2.3:** Use the "Surprise Me" feature to find evergreen gems I may have missed.

### Epic 3: Reader Support (Donating)
*As a fan of independent thought, I want to support the author directly.*
- **Story 3.1:** Contribute via a warm, emotive donation module without ads.
- **Story 3.2:** Choose between a one-time "gift" or a recurring monthly "patronage."
- **Story 3.3:** Feel like a part of the "resistance against algorithms" through my support.

### Epic 4: Search & Synthesis
*As a researcher, I want to find specific ideas in a colossal archive of essays.*
- **Story 4.1:** Search thousands of posts instantly via a clean search interface.
- **Story 4.2:** Filter search results by "Subject" or "Year" to find specific eras of thought.
- **Story 4.3:** Copy "Marginalia" and blockquotes easily for personal citation.
