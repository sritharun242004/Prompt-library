# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## MarginaliaTimeless Writer Portfolio · pcpp_platform_09

### 1. Product Vision
"MarginaliaTimeless" is a digital oasis for the wandering mind. It moves beyond the ephemeral "blog" to create a permanent, cross-pollinated archive of human wisdom. The platform prioritizes high-readability, long-form essays and curated thematic connections, providing prolific writers with a digital book-like presence that is entirely reader-supported and ad-free.

**Success Metric:** "Thread Depth" (number of complementary essays followed per session) exceeds 2.5, indicating successful intellectual discovery.

### 2. Personas
**Dr. Julian — The Academic Explorer (User)**
- Researches the intersection of poetry and physics.
- Needs: A clean, distraction-free reading environment and the ability to follow "intellectual threads" across 20 years of content.
- Frustration: Over-designed sites with pop-ups and ads that break the flow of complex thought.

**Clara — The Mindful Supporter (Audience)**
- Values the independent voice of the author and wants to ensure the publication's survival.
- Needs: A simple, emotive way to contribute via donations and a direct relationship with the author via newsletters.
- Frustration: Sites that bury donation links or use aggressive, guilt-based fundraising.

### 3. Core Features
- **Digital-Book Reading Container:** A 720px centered text column with book-grade typography and airy leading (1.8).
- **Curated Reading Threads:** A manual "Complement this" component at the end of every post that builds an interconnected web of meaning.
- **Thematic Subject Index:** A hierarchical index of thousands of posts categorized by intellectual topic (e.g., On Wonder, On Resilience).
- **Emotive Donation Module:** A recurring, high-contrast block that frames reader support as an "act of love" and "resistance."
- **Deep-Archive Search:** A high-speed search interface that indexes decades of long-form content instantly.

### 4. User Journeys
1. **The Deep Dive:** Visitor lands on a lead essay -> engages in a 10-minute focused read -> reaches the footer -> follows a "Complementary Thread" to a related 2014 essay.
2. **The Subject Search:** User visits the "Subject Index" -> selects "Astronomy" -> browses a decade-long archive of essays linking stars to poetry.
3. **The Loving Act:** User finishes an essay they love -> clicks the Goldenrod donation block -> completes a 1-click Stripe contribution.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No "Comments" or "User Profiles" (Focus is on author-to-reader relationship).
- No standard display ads or "Sponsored Content" (100% reader-supported).
- No infinite scroll (Reading requires a beginning, middle, and end).

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** Timeless Bookish. Warm paper palette, 0px rounding, zero marketing chrome.
- **Typography:** Mandatory use of premium serifs (Hoefler/Caslon or high-end web equivalents).
- **Commerce:** Must support both one-time and monthly recurring donations via Stripe.
