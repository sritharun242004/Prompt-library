# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · pcpp_platform_10

### Epic 1: Urgent Reporting
*As a visitor, I want a news feed that feels like a high-speed intelligence briefing.*
- **Story 1.1:** Scan the latest headlines and summaries instantly upon entry.
- **Story 1.2:** Filter the news stream by specific tech policy categories.
- **Story 1.3:** See which articles are "Free" vs "Paid" before clicking.

### Epic 2: Premium Engagement
*As a dedicated reader, I want a frictionless way to access high-value reporting.*
- **Story 2.1:** Read a high-quality free excerpt before encountering a paywall.
- **Story 2.2:** Sign in via a "Magic Link" without needing to remember a password.
- **Story 2.3:** View my "Member Dashboard" to update my payment method or plan.

### Epic 3: Intellectual Acquisition
*As a tech executive, I want to upgrade to a paid subscription in seconds.*
- **Story 3.1:** Compare annual vs monthly plans in a clean, high-trust modal.
- **Story 3.2:** Complete a secure checkout via Apple Pay or Credit Card.
- **Story 3.3:** Gain instant access to the full archive and private community links.

### Epic 4: Archive Research
*As a researcher, I want to find historical reporting on platform policy.*
- **Story 4.1:** Search thousands of articles by keyword or tech giant name (e.g., "Meta").
- **Story 4.2:** Filter archives by Date to understand the evolution of a policy shift.
- **Story 4.3:** Listen to weekly podcast summaries of the most important stories.
