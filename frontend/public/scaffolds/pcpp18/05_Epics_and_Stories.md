# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · pcpp_platform_18

### Epic 1: Expert Discovery
*As a professional learner, I want a marketplace that immediately establishes instructor authority.*
- **Story 1.1:** Browse courses by career pillar (e.g., Product, AI) via a dense top-bar.
- **Story 1.2:** See headshot-centric cards that highlight the instructor's technical pedigree.
- **Story 1.3:** Experience a clean, "Conference-Grade" aesthetic that establishes trust.

### Epic 2: Logistical Clarity
*As a busy operator, I want to know exactly when a cohort starts and what it covers.*
- **Story 2.1:** View real-time start dates and enrollment deadlines on every course card.
- **Story 2.2:** Deep-dive into a week-by-week syllabus using scannable accordions.
- **Story 2.3:** See which cohorts are "Live" vs. "On-Demand" before clicking through.

### Epic 3: Professional Commitment
*As a senior employee, I want a frictionless way to buy or expense high-ticket courses.*
- **Story 3.1:** Generate a professional email template to request manager approval in 1 click.
- **Story 3.2:** Complete a secure enrollment via Stripe with support for Apple/Google Pay.
- **Story 3.3:** Receive a high-authority invoice and confirmation for corporate accounting.

### Epic 4: Community Journey
*As a new student, I want a centralized hub for my learning transformation.*
- **Story 4.1:** Access live Zoom links and session recordings in a dedicated dashboard.
- **Story 4.2:** Track my progress across course modules and lessons automatically.
- **Story 4.3:** Portal directly to peer community channels for networking and support.
