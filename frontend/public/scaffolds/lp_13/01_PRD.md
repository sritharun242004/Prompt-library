# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## Glassmorphic Software Platform · lp_platform_13

### 1. Product Vision
"Glassmorphic" is a digital hallmark for the future of computing. It moves beyond standard "utility sites" to create an immersive, translucent environment that reflects the calm and organization of the software itself. The platform prioritizes "Visual Persuasion" through high-fidelity deconstructed mockups and leverages bold, category-challenging value props, providing innovative tools with a digital presence that signals world-class craftsmanship and an elite "A Better Way" philosophy.

**Success Metric:** "Hero-to-Download" conversion rate (percentage of users who interact with a glass feature card and proceed to click a platform-specific download link) exceeds 22%.

### 2. Personas
**Leo — The Digital Perfectionist (User)**
- A power user who is tired of tab-chaos and seeks a "Calm" digital workspace.
- Needs: Immediate proof that the browser is different (Vertical Sidebar), evidence of high-end design, and a fast download path.
- Frustration: Legacy browsers that feel like "clunky office software" from the 2000s.

**The Team — The Product Innovators (Client)**
- Needs to challenge Chrome/Safari dominance through a provocative digital manifest.
- Needs: A platform that showcases the "Dia" AI soul through elegant motion and manages high-volume global downloads.
- Frustration: Generic marketing sites that fail to capture the "App-like" tactile quality of their software.

### 3. Core Features
- **Bold One-Line Value Prop Hero:** A massive centered H1 paired with a high-fidelity deconstructed browser mockup.
- **Glassmorphic Feature Hub:** A grid of modular translucent cards (`backdrop-blur-xl`) explaining "Spaces" and "Organization."
- **Interactive Split-View Visualizer:** A component that allows users to toggle or scroll through a side-by-side multi-tasking demonstration.
- **AI (Dia) Motion Layer:** Subtle pulsing gradient glows that represent integrated intelligence within the UI frames.
- **Multi-Platform Download Portal:** A high-conversion group for Windows, Mac, and Mobile triggers with auto-detection logic.

### 4. User Journeys
1. **The Provocative Entry:** Visitor lands on hub -> sees massive "THE CHROME REPLACEMENT" text -> views the rotating glass browser frame -> clicks "Spaces" card.
2. **The Feature Deconstruction:** User scrolls to "Split View" -> interacts with a mockup that swaps from 1-pane to 2-pane -> understands the productivity gain instantly.
3. **The Multi-Platform Join:** User scrolls to the footer -> sees "Ready for a new home?" -> selects "Download for Mac" -> initiates the 50MB installer fetch.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No community "Forum" or social wall (Maintain the elite, focused brand voice).
- No complex "Blog" feed (Use a simple Release Notes archive instead).
- No "Ads" or third-party sponsorship blocks (Pure product focus).

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** Dynamic Glassmorphism. Deep gradients, generous 24px rounding, high-contrast white typography.
- **Performance:** Sub-150ms LCP (First Contentful Paint) for the hero mockup.
- **Motion:** Must support `prefers-reduced-motion` with static high-res fallback mockups.
