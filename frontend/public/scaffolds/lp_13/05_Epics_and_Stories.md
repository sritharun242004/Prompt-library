# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · lp_platform_13

### Epic 1: Aesthetic Provocation
*As a visitor, I want a homepage that immediately signals that this software is the future.*
- **Story 1.1:** Experience a full-bleed vibrant gradient background that makes the UI "Pop."
- **Story 1.2:** See a provocative one-line H1 ("THE CHROME REPLACEMENT") that challenges my current habits.
- **Story 1.3:** View a floating glass browser mockup that shows a deconstructed vertical navigation.

### Epic 2: Visual Discovery (Features)
*As a power user, I want to see how the software organizes my digital life.*
- **Story 2.1:** Browse a grid of translucent glass cards that explain "Spaces" for work and life.
- **Story 2.2:** Interact with a "Split View" demo that shows side-by-side multi-tasking.
- **Story 2.3:** Experience subtle "AI Glows" that highlight integrated intelligence features.

### Epic 3: High-Fidelity Interaction
*As a designer, I want the website to feel as premium and tactile as the app itself.*
- **Story 3.1:** Hover over glass cards to see smooth inner-glow expansions and scale shifts.
- **Story 3.2:** View high-contrast typography that is scannable and authoritative.
- **Story 3.3:** Experience zero lag or stutter during translucent layout transitions.

### Epic 4: Platform Acquisition
*As a ready user, I want to download the installer for my specific device instantly.*
- **Story 4.1:** Access a "Download" portal that automatically highlights my current platform (Win/Mac).
- **Story 4.2:** View version numbers and release notes to verify platform maturity.
- **Story 4.3:** Click a vibrant CTA that initiates a high-speed download session.
