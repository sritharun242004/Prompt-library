# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · bw_platform_05

### Epic 1: Immersive Storytelling
*As a visitor, I want a homepage that immediately communicates the restaurant's vibrant vibe.*
- **Story 1.1:** Experience a full-bleed vertical narrative scroll upon landing.
- **Story 1.2:** Browse seasonal "Hero Dishes" with high-vibrancy lifestyle imagery.
- **Story 1.3:** See proof of community engagement through the "Canteen Stories" grid.

### Epic 2: Visual Menu Discovery
*As a gourmet, I want to find interesting dishes through an engaging digital menu.*
- **Story 2.1:** Navigate dish categories using custom-drawn illustrative icons.
- **Story 2.2:** Read the regional "Tale of the Plate" for featured seasonal items.
- **Story 2.3:** Filter the menu by dietary needs (Veg/Non-Veg) using clear visual markers.

### Epic 3: Frictionless Booking
*As a busy diner, I want to book a table without leaving the brand environment.*
- **Story 3.1:** Select "Book a Table" from the sticky header to reveal the reservation drawer.
- **Story 3.2:** Provide guest count and time details in a clean, non-disruptive interface.
- **Story 3.3:** Receive an instant, high-trust confirmation from the restaurant's booking system.

### Epic 4: Brand Extension (Shop)
*As a fan, I want to buy brand merchandise and gift cards easily.*
- **Story 4.1:** Browse a curated shop of cocktail books and brand apparel.
- **Story 4.2:** Purchase a digital gift card for a friend with a personalized message.
- **Story 4.3:** Experience a 1-page checkout that remembers my community shipping details.
