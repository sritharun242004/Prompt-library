# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## PrestigeMinimalism Global Hub · bw_platform_07

### 1. Product Vision
"PrestigeMinimalism" is a digital landmark for world-class hospitality institutions. It moves beyond the "local restaurant" site to create a "Global Flagship" experience where architectural clarity and Michelin-grade awards establish immediate trust. The platform prioritizes high-speed routing between global flagship locations and precise, policy-driven booking, providing culinary icons with a digital presence that matches the prestige and technical mastery of their plated dishes.

**Success Metric:** 15% of users from international IP addresses successfully portal through the "Global Switcher" and view a tasting menu within 2 minutes of entry.

### 2. Personas
**Julian — The Global Jetsetter (Client)**
- Travels for business and seeks "World's 50 Best" destinations for high-stakes entertaining.
- Needs: Immediate proof of global status, clear city-specific menus, and a direct way to book via OpenTable.
- Frustration: Sites that are hard to navigate across cities or bury their accolades in hidden pages.

**Sophie — The Culinary Collector (Audience)**
- Values technical innovation and follows specific Chefs across continents.
- Needs: High-end documentation of the "Chef's Tasting" and a prestigious shop to buy signed cookbooks and vouchers.
- Frustration: Low-quality imagery and complex booking forms that don't clearly state the restaurant's strict policies.

### 3. Core Features
- **Global Location Portal:** A high-impact, multi-city switcher that directs users to Delhi, NYC, or Mumbai sub-sites with unique heros.
- **Michelin/Accolade Strip:** A horizontal row of prestigious grayscaled international badges to establish authority.
- **Tasting Menu Engine:** A visual-first component showcasing inventive dishes with "Chef's Notes" and minimalist labels.
- **Policy-Integrated Booking:** A reservation flow that integrates OpenTable while mandating acceptance of strict age and payment terms.
- **Prestige Gift Shop:** A minimalist e-commerce hub for high-AOV digital vouchers and physical culinary collectibles.

### 4. User Journeys
1. **The Global Portaling:** Visitor lands on hub -> sees the New Delhi/New York/Mumbai split hero -> clicks "New York" -> enters the intimate NYC sub-site -> views the current tasting menu.
2. **The Authority Check:** User scrolls the homepage -> sees the "No.1 in India" and "World's 50 Best" badge strip -> understands the brand's global stature -> clicks "Reservations."
3. **The Executive Gift:** User enters "Gifting" -> selects a ₹10,000 Dining Voucher -> completes 1-click checkout -> receives a premium branded e-certificate.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No "Online Ordering" or "Delivery" (Maintain the exclusive dine-in only focus).
- No community "Social Feed" (Focus on the authoritative, curated voice).
- No generic "Contact Form" (Direct, city-specific email and phone only).

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** Michelin-Grade Minimalism. Deep charcoal, brass accents, strictly sharp 0px corners.
- **Performance:** Sub-1s initial load for the hub switcher (Next.js server-side optimization).
- **Compliance:** Full WCAG AA accessibility for menu readability and booking flows.
