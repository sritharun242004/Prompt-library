# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · bw_platform_07

### Epic 1: Global Portaling
*As an international traveler, I want a homepage that directs me to the nearest flagship instantly.*
- **Story 1.1:** Experience a high-impact split-screen hub showing New Delhi, NYC, and Mumbai.
- **Story 1.2:** Navigate between cities via a persistent, prestigious dropdown menu.
- **Story 1.3:** See a grayscaled strip of international awards that proves the brand's global stature.

### Epic 2: Culinary Authority
*As a connoisseur, I want to see the details and philosophy behind the inventive menu.*
- **Story 2.1:** Browse a high-resolution grid of the current "Chef's Tasting" dishes.
- **Story 2.2:** Read technical "Chef's Notes" for signature items like Blue Cheese Naan.
- **Story 2.3:** View prestigious endorsements (Michelin Star, 50 Best) linked to specific menu edits.

### Epic 3: High-Standards Booking
*As a corporate host, I want a reservation experience that confirms all entry policies clearly.*
- **Story 3.1:** Access a reservation flow that mandates acceptance of age limits and payment terms.
- **Story 3.2:** Book a table for a small group via a seamless OpenTable integration.
- **Story 3.3:** Receive a professional, authoritative confirmation email with venue directions and policies.

### Epic 4: Prestige Gifting
*As a fan, I want to purchase high-value gifts for clients or friends.*
- **Story 4.1:** Browse a curated shop of signed cookbooks and digital experience vouchers.
- **Story 4.2:** Purchase a high-AOV voucher in under 60 seconds via a minimalist checkout.
- **Story 4.3:** Send a beautifully branded e-gift directly to a recipient's inbox.
