# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## CrewCinema Production Portfolio · pcpp_platform_08

### 1. Product Vision
"CrewCinema" is a digital powerhouse for large-scale photography and film production firms. It moves beyond the "single artist" model to create a "Studio Headquarters" experience that showcases high-volume expertise across multiple domains (Wedding, Fashion, Corporate). The platform prioritizes high-energy visual impact and operational trust, providing elite studios with a digital presence that commands respect from high-profile clients and planners.

**Success Metric:** 30% of visitors interact with at least two different "Service Categories" (e.g., Photography and Films) within a single session.

### 2. Personas
**Rajesh — The High-Profile Patriarch (Client)**
- Planning a massive 3-day wedding for his daughter and needs a "safe pair of hands."
- Needs: Immediate proof of technical scale, on-time delivery assurance, and a team that can handle 1000+ guests.
- Frustration: "Artsy" sites that don't show the logistical capability of the firm or hide the team size.

**Ananya — The Luxury Wedding Planner (Referrer)**
- Needs a reliable production partner for a destination wedding in Goa.
- Needs: Fast access to 4K trailers and a clear breakdown of services (Candid vs Traditional vs Pre-wedding).
- Frustration: Slow-loading galleries and sites that lack a professional, studio-grade structure.

### 3. Core Features
- **Cinematic Production Hero:** A full-bleed, auto-playing video trailer loop that establishes immediate high-octane production value.
- **Service Mosaic Hub:** A high-density homepage grid that organizes the portfolio into logical service blocks (Films, Photo, Lifestyle).
- **"Meet the Crew" Database:** A dedicated section highlighting the team's scale and expertise, humanizing the production house.
- **Social Proof & Ratings Strip:** A high-impact area showcasing verified 4.6/5 ratings and monochrome press logos.
- **Multi-Category Series Engine:** A layout system that groups related photo and video content into a single, cohesive project narrative.

### 4. User Journeys
1. **The Production Deep-Dive:** Visitor lands on home -> watches the cinematic reel -> scrolls to the "Films" mosaic block -> enters the filtered cinematography hub.
2. **The Trust Check:** User navigates to "Meet the Crew" -> reviews the lead directors' profiles -> reads verified client testimonials with 4.6/5 rating proof.
3. **The Rapid Inquiry:** User clicks "Start Your Production" -> fills out a simplified 4-field inquiry form -> receives a professional studio confirmation.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No client login for "RAW Image Selection" (This is a showcase portfolio only).
- No complex "Blog" (Focus is on high-impact projects and services).
- No standard "Square Grid" uniformity (Prefer dynamic mosaic/masonry layouts).

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** Production Modern. Vibrant colors, 8px rounding, high-contrast slate/white UI.
- **Performance:** Sub-2s initial load for high-density grids; adaptive bitrates for video heros.
- **Data:** Must support high-volume CMS data (500+ projects).
