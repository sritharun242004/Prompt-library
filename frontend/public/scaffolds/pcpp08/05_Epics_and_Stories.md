# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · pcpp_platform_08

### Epic 1: High-Energy Discovery
*As a visitor, I want a homepage that immediately proves this is a world-class production house.*
- **Story 1.1:** Watch a high-octane 4K trailer reel upon landing.
- **Story 1.2:** Navigate the studio's varied services (Photo, Film, Fashion) via a dynamic mosaic.
- **Story 1.3:** See a prestige strip featuring verified 4.6/5 ratings and magazine logos.

### Epic 2: Organized Excellence
*As a wedding planner, I want to find high-quality work categorized by service type.*
- **Story 2.1:** Filter the portfolio by "Films" or "Candid Photography" with a single click.
- **Story 2.2:** View statistical proof of experience (e.g., "500+ Films Delivered") on category headers.
- **Story 2.3:** Browse a masonry grid of trailers with instant hover-video previews.

### Epic 3: The Human Production
*As a client, I want to see the team who will be handling my major event.*
- **Story 3.1:** Browse the "Crew" section to see the scale and leadership of the studio.
- **Story 3.2:** Read brief bios of the artistic directors and lead cinematographers.
- **Story 3.3:** Understand the studio's "Organic Storytelling" philosophy through a video bio.

### Epic 4: Studio Commitment
*As a busy planner, I want a fast, professional way to book the crew.*
- **Story 4.1:** Access a clean inquiry portal from any page.
- **Story 4.2:** Provide event logistics in a structured, studio-grade form.
- **Story 4.3:** Receive an immediate, high-trust automated confirmation.
