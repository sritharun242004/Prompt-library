# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · bw_platform_06

### Epic 1: Sun-Drenched Discovery
*As a visitor, I want a homepage that immediately transports me to a Goan state of mind.*
- **Story 1.1:** Experience a full-bleed environmental hero showing the "shabby-chic" interior.
- **Story 1.2:** Navigate through the brand's world via a modular bento grid (Menu, Story, Order).
- **Story 1.3:** See a prestige strip featuring sister brands like The Bombay Canteen.

### Epic 2: Wit & Charm (The Mascot)
*As a fan, I want to see the brand's personality through Rocky the Rooster.*
- **Story 2.1:** Discover Rocky appearing in various UI corners with playful animations.
- **Story 2.2:** See which dishes or cocktails are "Rocky's Favorites" via visual badges.
- **Story 2.3:** Read about the "Rocky's Favorite Sips" in a dedicated bar narrative section.

### Epic 3: High-Readability Menus
*As a gourmet, I want to find interesting dishes through a clean, text-rich menu.*
- **Story 3.1:** Filter the menu by "Small Plates" or "Cocktails" without full-page reloads.
- **Story 3.2:** Read detailed descriptions of Goan-Portuguese ingredients and techniques.
- **Story 3.3:** Tap a "Order Online" link from the menu to go directly to delivery platforms.

### Epic 4: Coastal Community
*As a local, I want to know what's happening at the restaurant this week.*
- **Story 4.1:** Browse the "Happy Times" strip to see upcoming events and guest shifts.
- **Story 4.2:** Initiate a group booking inquiry via a simple phone or email trigger.
- **Story 4.3:** Receive a professional, "Sussegad" confirmation email for my inquiry.
