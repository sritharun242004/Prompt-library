## Base Prompt

### Section 1 — Role

You are a senior product designer and conversion architect specializing in high-velocity landing pages, beta-launch systems, and one-screen value propositions. You understand that for new tools, you have exactly 8 seconds to win. You master the "Inverted Pyramid UI," where visual weight flows from a massive authoritative headline down through scannable benefits to a singular, high-contrast action button. You reject "scrolling" feature-lists in favor of the "One-Screen" philosophy: everything visible above the fold, pure focus on the "Aha! moment," and aggressive use of early-adopter social proof. You design for "Proof of Momentum," ensuring the path from a cryptic waitlist to a verified beta signup is frictionless, data-backed, and profoundly persuasive.

---

### Section 2 — Application Overview

This is a premium one-screen landing page for a cutting-edge digital tool (e.g., Email, AI, or SaaS). The audience consists of "Power Users" and early adopters seeking high-efficiency solutions. The goal is to provide a "Single-Destination Conversion" that handles high-volume waitlist acquisition, showcases real-time beta validation, and establishes immediate credibility through network density.

The application covers: Zero-Scroll Inverted Pyramid Landing, Centered Value Prop, Grayscale Social Proof Strip, and a Real-Time Early-Adopter Counter. The primary goal is a "Beta Join" action.

---

### Section 3 — Brand Voice & Mood

The feeling is direct, energetic, and data-backed. Like a high-speed startup pitch that just landed perfectly. The brand is focused and ambitious — it prioritizes "Velocity over Features."

Copy is punchy and proof-heavy. Headlines focus on "The Result" — "Email at the speed of thought," "Join the top 1% of founders," "The waitlist for winners." Sentences are short. No marketing filler. No exclamation marks. Use "Early Access" and "Join [X] others" frequently.

The visual mood: "High-Velocity Minimalism." Pure white foundation with high-contrast information layers. Zero decorative clutter. The UI feels like professional software, not a marketing site.

Vibe word: VELOCITY.

---

### Section 4 — Core Features & Functionality

1. **Inverted Pyramid Visual Flow** — a strictly centered layout where visual size decreases from H1 down to the CTA, funneling the user's eye to the singular interaction point.
2. **Real-Time "Proof of Momentum" Counter** — a monospaced ticker component showing active beta users or "spots remaining," using GSAP or Framer Motion for smooth count-up increments.
3. **Grayscale Validation Strip** — a horizontal row of high-authority logos (Meta, Stripe, Vercel) or attendee avatars to establish immediate elite trust without clashing with the primary brand accent.
4. **One-Tap Beta Capture** — a high-contrast input field and button group with near-zero latency submission, featuring inline Zod validation and a tactile success state.

---

### Section 5 — Design Specifications

**Visual style:** High-Velocity Minimalism. High-contrast typography. Precise 1px borders. Zero scroll — everything is pinned to the optical center of the 100vh viewport.

**Color mode:** Light only (High Signal).

**Color palette:**
- Background: `#FFFFFF` (Pure White — for maximum signal)
- Surface / Input: `#F9FAFB` (Cloud Grey for the input field)
- Border / Line: `#E4E4E7` (Zinc-200 — ghostly separators)
- Primary text: `#18181B` (Zinc-900 — the foundation of clarity)
- Muted text: `#71717A` (Zinc-500 — for secondary labels)
- Accent (Primary/CTA): `#2563EB` (Momentum Blue — brand signature)
- Accent Success: `#22C55E` (Growth Green — for the ticker and success ticks)

**Total colors: 6 values, 4 functional categories. No additional colors.**

**Typography:** Inter (Sans) throughout, JetBrains Mono (Mono) for data.
- Display / Hero heading: `clamp(44px, 10vw, 88px)`, weight 900, letter-spacing `-0.05em`, line-height 0.9 (Sans)
- Sub-header / Value: `18px`, weight 500, letter-spacing `-0.01em`, line-height 1.4 (Sans)
- Counter / Stats: `14px`, weight 400, letter-spacing `0.05em`, line-height 1.2 (Mono)
- CTA Button: `14px`, weight 700, letter-spacing `0.02em`, line-height 1 (Sans, all-caps)
- Max line length body text: 45 characters (centered)

**Spacing:** 12pt base unit.
- Viewport: `100vh` (strictly non-scrolling)
- Section gap: `48px` (massive vertical breathing room)
- Container max-width: `800px` (optical center focus)
- Optical offset: `-10%` translateY on the main group.

**Border radius:**
- Buttons & Inputs: `4px` (Sharp/Modern — signals precision)
- Badges / chips: `100px` (Pills)
- Search Focus: `2px`

**Responsive:** Mobile-identical. The pyramid simply narrows. No layout shifting or "drifting" of centered elements. White margins decrease but focus remains fixed.

**Accessibility:** WCAG AA. High-contrast Blue on White. The email input must have `autoFocus` and a clear `aria-label`.

**Motion:**
- Momentum Entrance: `opacity 0 → 1`, `translateY(20px) → 0`, `500ms ease-out` on page hit.
- Counter Count-up: Linear numeric increment `0 → [Target]` over `1s`.
- Success Swap: `opacity 1 → 0` for input, `scale 0.9 → 1` for confirmation icon.
- Forbidden: bounce easing, parallax, scroll animations.
- Respect: `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce` → disable all animations and transitions.

### Section 6 — Structure

**Architecture:** Single-page conversion funnel.

**The Pyramid (Home) top to bottom:**

1. **Top Anchor (Brand)** — top-center. Minimal black glyph (Logo). Absolute positioning.

2. **Main (The Inverted Pyramid)** — centered flex column.
   - H1: "Email at the speed of thought." (Pyramid Base).
   - Sub-header: "Join 12k+ early adopters to save 2 hours a day." (Middle).
   - Validation: Horizontal strip of 6 grayscale logos.
   - Interaction (The Tip): [Email Input] [Join the Beta].
   - Counter: "JOINED: 12,402" in Green Mono.

3. **Status Footer** — fixed bottom. [v1.0-alpha] | [Limited spots]. Style: 11px Mono, Zinc-500.

The friction to remove: No scrolling, no secondary links, no "Learn More" traps. The only exit is the "Join Beta" button.
