# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## PyramidVelocity One-Pager · lp_platform_09

### 1. Product Vision
"PyramidVelocity" is a digital hallmark for growth momentum. It moves beyond standard "landing pages" to create an ultra-focused "Inverted Pyramid" funnel where the user's attention is captured by a massive value prop and guided instantly to a beta signup. The platform prioritizes "Immediacy" and leverages early-adopter social proof to establish trust, providing new tools with a digital presence that signals high-speed execution and market validation.

**Success Metric:** "Time-to-Conversion" (average time from page load to email submission) is under 15 seconds for qualified users.

### 2. Personas
**The Power User — The Early Adopter (User)**
- Always looking for tools to optimize their workflow (Email, AI, SaaS).
- Needs: Immediate proof that the tool solves a real problem and evidence that other industry peers are using it.
- Frustration: Long scrolling pages that bury the "Join Beta" button or don't show who else is in.

**The Founder — The Velocity Architect (Client)**
- Launching a beta and needs to build a high-quality waitlist in days, not months.
- Needs: A near-instant loading page that established professional authority and handles high-concurrency signups.
- Frustration: Complex builders that make it hard to achieve perfect viewport-centering or real-time counters.

### 3. Core Features
- **Inverted Pyramid Layout:** A visual system that funnels attention from a massive H1 down to a singular CTA.
- **Grayscale Validation Strip:** A horizontal row of high-authority logos (Meta, Stripe, Vercel) used to establish immediate trust.
- **Zero-Friction Beta Capture:** A high-contrast, centered input field with near-zero latency submission and validation.
- **Early-Access Counter:** A real-time (or data-backed) count-up component showing active users or limited spots remaining.
- **100vh Viewport Lock:** A strictly non-scrolling environment that ensures the message remains visually dominant.

### 4. User Journeys
1. **The Momentum Join:** Visitor lands on page -> sees massive "Email at the speed of thought" H1 -> scans 5k+ user counter -> enters email -> sees success tick.
2. **The Social Check:** User arrives from Twitter -> sees their peer's company logo in the validation strip -> understands the tool is relevant -> clicks "Join Beta."
3. **The Mobile Entry:** User opens link on mobile -> page centers perfectly -> taps oversized "Email" field -> keyboard appears -> enters info -> taps Blue "Join" button.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No "Features" or "Pricing" sub-pages (The one-pager is the entire product).
- No "Top Navigation" or "Menu" (Remove all exit points).
- No "Hero Images" (The typography is the visual value).

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** High-Velocity Minimalism. #FFFFFF foundation, Momentum Blue accents, sharp corners.
- **Speed:** Sub-150ms LCP (First Contentful Paint). Sub-300ms TTI.
- **Viewport:** Strictly above-the-fold focus. No scroll bars.
