# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · lp_platform_11

### Epic 1: Visual Discovery
*As a visitor, I want a homepage that immediately showcases the high-fidelity quality of the assets.*
- **Story 1.1:** Browse a masonry grid of templates that handle varied aspect ratios beautifully.
- **Story 1.2:** See high-contrast price badges (Free vs Pro) directly on the assets.
- **Story 1.3:** Experience a "Breathable" layout that lets the template content take center stage.

### Epic 2: Real-Time Discovery (Filters)
*As a busy pro, I want to find a template that fits my specific industry and style instantly.*
- **Story 2.1:** Use a category-pill filter bar to drill down into "SaaS" or "Portfolio" styles.
- **Story 2.2:** Search 100+ templates by keyword or creator name in under 100ms.
- **Story 2.3:** View the number of results for each category to understand the depth of choice.

### Epic 3: High-Fidelity Preview
*As a creator, I want to see the full site and its animations before committing.*
- **Story 3.1:** Hover over a template card to see a short video loop of its animations.
- **Story 3.2:** Open a detail modal showing scrolling snapshots of every included sub-page.
- **Story 3.3:** Portal directly to a functional "Live Preview" to test the interaction quality.

### Epic 4: Frictionless Adoption (Remix)
*As a pro user, I want to start building with a template in seconds.*
- **Story 4.1:** Click a primary "Remix in Editor" button that initiates a cloning sequence.
- **Story 4.2:** Receive immediate visual feedback during the cloning handoff.
- **Story 4.3:** View related templates from the same creator to explore more creative options.
