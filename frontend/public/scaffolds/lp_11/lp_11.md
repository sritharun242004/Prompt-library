## Base Prompt

### Section 1 — Role

You are a senior product designer and marketplace architect specializing in digital asset galleries, template ecosystems, and visual discovery engines. You understand that for high-fidelity assets, "The Preview is the Product." You master the "Neutral Gallery UI," where pure white and deep black foundations are used to let colorful template content take center stage. You reject "busy" marketplace layouts in favor of the "Framer" philosophy: modular masonry grids, high-fidelity card interactions (video hovers), and a focus on "Creative Momentum." You design for "Seamless Adoption," ensuring the path from a thumbnail discovery to a "Remix in Editor" action is immediate, high-speed, and aesthetically elite.

---

### Section 2 — Application Overview

This is a premium digital hub for discovering and cloning high-quality web templates. The audience consists of designers, developers, and founders seeking "Instant Quality." The goal is to provide a "Visual Catalog" that handles high-density template data, showcases 100+ creative styles, and manages a frictionless "Remix/Copy" integration with the core product editor.

The store covers four primary views: Gallery Hub (Home), Category Discovery Page, Template Detail Modal, and Creator Profile. The primary goal is a "Remix/Clone" action. Secondary goal is "Category Filtering."

---

### Section 3 — Brand Voice & Mood

The feeling is design-forward, technically pure, and highly organized. Like entering a high-end art gallery where the walls are invisible. The brand is authoritative and curator-focused — it focuses on "The Best of the Community."

Copy is informative, precise, and curator-focused. Headlines focus on "The Style" — "Curated templates for startups," "Build your site in minutes," "Designed by the community." Sentences are precise. No salesy hype. No exclamation marks.

The visual mood: "Designer-Grade Minimalism." Pure white foundation with deep black accents. Strictly 1px borders. Modular masonry rhythm. High-fidelity video thumbnails that play on hover.

Vibe word: CURATION.

---

### Section 4 — Core Features & Functionality

1. **Modular Masonry Discovery Engine** — a flexible, multi-column display component that handles varying template aspect ratios while maintaining a tight, professional vertical rhythm.
2. **"Video Hover" Template Cards** — a core component that plays a 3-5 second loop of the site's animations/motion when the user hovers over a thumbnail, providing instant fidelity proof.
3. **Category-Logic Filtering** — a responsive sidebar navigation that organizes templates by use-case (Portfolio, SaaS, E-com) and style (Dark, Minimal, 3D), with real-time count updates.
4. **Detail Discovery Overlay** — a high-performance modal that shows page-by-page scrolling snapshots and provides a direct "Live Preview" portal before cloning.
5. **Integrated "Remix" Engine** — a primary conversion tool that clones the template project JSON directly into the user's active editor session via a secure API handoff.

---

### Section 5 — Design Specifications

**Visual style:** Designer-Grade Minimalism. Neutral foundation. Masonry rhythm. Strictly 1px borders. Zero decorative "fluff" — the template imagery provides the color.

**Color mode:** Strictly Neutral (Pure Black/White).

**Color palette:**
- Background: `#FFFFFF` (Pure White — the foundation of clarity)
- Surface / subtle: `#F9FAFB` (Subtle grey for filter sidebars and cards)
- Border / hairline: `#E5E7EB` (Slate-200 ghostly separators)
- Primary text: `#111827` (Deep Slate near-black)
- Muted text: `#6B7280` (Zinc-500 — for metadata and tags)
- Accent (Primary/Action): `#0055FF` (Framer Blue — brand signature)
- Accent Hover: `#0044CC` (darker blue)
- Accent Status: `#000000` (for "Premium" badges)

**Total colors: 7 values, 4 functional categories. No additional colors.**

**Typography:** Inter throughout (Google Fonts, free).
- Display / Hero heading: `clamp(32px, 5vw, 48px)`, weight 600, letter-spacing `-0.02em`, line-height 1.1
- Section heading (H2): `clamp(20px, 3vw, 28px)`, weight 600, letter-spacing `-0.01em`, line-height 1.2
- Template Title (H3): `16px`, weight 600, letter-spacing `0`, line-height 1.3
- Body / Meta: `14px`, weight 400, letter-spacing `0`, line-height 1.5
- Price / Label: `12px`, weight 600, letter-spacing `0.05em`, line-height 1.2 (uppercase, Mono font)
- Max line length body text: 60 characters

**Spacing:** 16pt base unit.
- Section vertical padding: `100px` desktop, `64px` mobile
- Grid gap: `24px` standard
- Container max-width: `1440px` (widescreen discovery)
- Sidebar width: `280px`

**Border radius:**
- Buttons: `8px` (Modern rounding)
- Cards & Product images: `12px` (rounded-lg)
- Badges / chips: `100px` (pill-shaped)
- Inputs: `8px`

**Responsive:** Mobile-first discovery. Filter sidebar collapses into a "Category Drawer." Grid becomes 1-column or 2-column masonry. Video hovers are disabled on mobile in favor of static high-res thumbnails.

**Accessibility:** WCAG AA. High-contrast Blue on White. All template cards must have descriptive `alt` tags and clear "Free/Pro" ARIA labels.

**Motion:**
- Smooth Hover: `scale 1.02`, `shadow-lg` transition `0.3s ease`
- Fade Entrance: `opacity 0 → 1`, `y: 20 → 0`, `staggerChildren: 0.1` for the grid.
- Modal Slide: `scale 0.95 → 1`, `opacity 0 → 1`, `300ms cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)`
- Forbidden: bounce easing, neon flickers, autoplay audio.
- Respect: `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce` → disable all animations and transitions.

- Overlay: `bg-white/95 backdrop-blur-xl`.

### Section 9 — User Experience

The visitor arrives seeking **Inspiration and Speed**.

The first 3 seconds must answer: **"Does this look professional?"** The masonry grid of high-fidelity sites does this work. The UI must feel like a "Curated Art Gallery" that happens to be an editor.

The friction to remove: No complex "Cart" (direct 1-click remix), no blurry thumbnails, no intrusive marketing banners. Access to the "Remix" button must be the primary action for every discovered template.

### Section 10 — Constraints

- **No brand colors** — Keep foundations strictly neutral to avoid clashing with template content.
- **No slow loading** — Thumbnails must be WebP/AVIF optimized (<100kb).
- **No carousel heros** — Let the masonry grid lead the discovery.
- **No shadow-heavy UI** — Use strictly 1px borders for separation.
- **No hidden pricing** — Price/Free status must be visible on the card without hover.
- **No Lorem Ipsum** — Use realistic template names ("Orbit — SaaS Landing Page").
