# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## AnalyticalKen Business Portfolio · pcpp_platform_14

### 1. Product Vision
"AnalyticalKen" is the digital source of truth for modern business leaders. It moves beyond standard news reporting to provide original, analytical deep-dives and mobile-first visual stories. The platform prioritizes the "First 60 Seconds" of the user experience through signature Nutgraph summaries, providing a premium, ad-free environment that values the reader's time and intelligence.

**Success Metric:** "Nutgraph Retention" (percentage of users who read the summary and continue to the full story) exceeds 75%.

### 2. Personas
**Vikram — The Startup Founder (User)**
- Needs to track competitors and regulatory shifts in the fintech space.
- Needs: A fast morning summary (Nutgraph) and high-impact visual trends that he can screenshot for his team.
- Frustration: Overloaded news sites that take 5 minutes to get to the point.

**Anjali — The VC Associate (Audience)**
- Researches investment themes across SE Asia.
- Needs: Deep archives of company-specific reporting and the ability to "gift" a specific story to a portfolio CEO.
- Frustration: Paywalls that are hard to share through and lack of scannability in long-form reports.

### 3. Core Features
- **"Beyond the Nutgraph" Module:** A scannable, 3-point summary block at the head of every deep-dive story.
- **Interactive Visual Story Engine:** A full-screen, slide-based layout optimized for mobile trend-reporting and data-viz.
- **Bespoke Data-Viz Integration:** A CMS-controlled system for rendering custom, branded charts and infographics.
- **Premium Podcast Hub:** A dedicated audio area for executive interviews and daily morning briefings.
- **Viral Discovery (Gifting):** A referral engine allowing members to generate time-limited "Free Access" links for individual stories.

### 4. User Journeys
1. **The Morning Sprint:** Visitor lands on the Daily Story -> reads the Nutgraph -> listens to the 2-minute audio summary while commuting.
2. **The Trend Dive:** User enters "Visual Stories" -> swipes through a 12-slide trend report on "Digital Lending" -> sees a CTA to read the full analytical report.
3. **The Executive Share:** Member finishes a story -> clicks "Gift this Story" -> sends a unique link to a colleague via WhatsApp.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No "Opinion/Op-ed" section (Focus is strictly on original, fact-based reporting).
- No community comments or public-facing "Likes" (Maintain the boutique journal feel).
- No automated "Breaking News" tickers (Quality over quantity).

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** Analytical Modernism. Ken Red accents, sharp corners, authoritative serif typography.
- **Speed:** Mandatory sub-1s LCP on mobile 4G/5G networks.
- **Transparency:** All data-viz must include "Source" labels and clear legend keys.
