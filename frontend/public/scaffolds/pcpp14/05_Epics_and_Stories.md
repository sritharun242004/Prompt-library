# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · pcpp_platform_14

### Epic 1: High-Speed Insight
*As a busy leader, I want to understand the core of a story in seconds.*
- **Story 1.1:** Read a 3-point summary (Nutgraph) before committing to a full article.
- **Story 1.2:** Scan the daily feed and see read-times and key subjects instantly.
- **Story 1.3:** Listen to a 2-minute audio version of the Nutgraph directly from the feed.

### Epic 2: Visual Discovery
*As a visual learner, I want to see complex trends simplified into interactive slides.*
- **Story 2.1:** Browse a grid of "Visual Stories" categorized by trend.
- **Story 2.2:** Swipe through a slide deck that combines high-impact text and data-viz.
- **Story 2.3:** Share a specific slide from a visual story to social media.

### Epic 3: Strategic Research
*As a researcher, I want to find every story ever written about a specific startup or policy.*
- **Story 3.1:** Use a high-speed search bar to index the 5-year archive.
- **Story 3.2:** Filter search results by "Subject" or "Geography" (India vs SE Asia).
- **Story 3.3:** Follow a chronological "Series" thread on a specific company's evolution.

### Epic 4: Community Growth (Gifting)
*As a member, I want to share the value of the platform with my professional network.*
- **Story 4.1:** Generate a unique, sharable link for a premium story.
- **Story 4.2:** Track how many of my "Gifted" stories were opened.
- **Story 4.3:** Redeem a "Corporate Membership" code provided by my employer.
