# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · pcpp_platform_06

### Epic 1: Prestigious Browsing
*As a visitor, I want a homepage that immediately communicates "World-Class" status.*
- **Story 1.1:** Experience a full-screen cinematic hero video upon entry.
- **Story 1.2:** Browse a masonry grid of iconic work that handles horizontal and vertical shots flawlessly.
- **Story 1.3:** See a prestige press strip featuring Vogue and Netflix.

### Epic 2: Narrative Excellence
*As a connoisseur, I want to see the "Story" of a wedding through a sequence of iconic images.*
- **Story 2.1:** Scroll through a vertical story with high-resolution imagery and minimal text.
- **Story 2.2:** Enjoy high-contrast editorial captions that provide minimal context.
- **Story 2.3:** Navigate back to the main masonry grid instantly without losing scroll position.

### Epic 3: Educational Leadership
*As an aspiring photographer, I want to find and book industry-leading workshops easily.*
- **Story 3.1:** Browse a list of global conferences and masterclasses.
- **Story 3.2:** View detailed event schedules and curriculum overviews.
- **Story 3.3:** Pay a deposit for a masterclass via a secure, integrated checkout.

### Epic 4: Prestige Engagement
*As a high-end client, I want to initiate a professional commission through a minimalist portal.*
- **Story 4.1:** Access a clean "Inquiry" page that establishes professional boundaries.
- **Story 4.2:** Provide event details in a high-contrast, easy-to-use form.
- **Story 4.3:** Receive an automated, prestigious brand confirmation email.
