# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## IconicElegance Luxury Portfolio · pcpp_platform_06

### 1. Product Vision
"IconicElegance" is a digital hallmark of excellence for world-class photography brands. It moves beyond a simple image gallery to create a multi-layered ecosystem where prestigious client work and industry-leading education coexist. The platform prioritizes high-end editorial aesthetics and sophisticated functionality, providing global leaders with a digital presence that commands authority and signals luxury.

**Success Metric:** 30% increase in workshop/conference registration through integrated "Education Hub" CTAs within the narrative journals.

### 2. Personas
**Alessandra — The Luxury Brand Manager (Client)**
- Needs to hire a photographer for a high-profile global campaign or event.
- Needs: Immediate proof of "Iconic Status" through high-contrast heros and a list of prestigious international features.
- Frustration: Cluttered or "local-looking" sites that don't reflect global luxury standards.

**Karthik — The Aspiring Wedding Pro (Audience)**
- Follows the brand to learn technical skills and business strategy.
- Needs: A structured list of upcoming conferences and masterclasses with easy registration.
- Frustration: Missing out on workshops because they are buried in blog posts or social media links.

### 3. Core Features
- **Sophisticated Masonry Engine:** A performant grid system that elegantly handles mixed aspect ratios with generous whitespace.
- **Cinematic Journal:** A long-scroll vertical storytelling format for wedding series, focusing on "Iconic Single Images" and minimal editorial text.
- **Education & Event Hub:** A dedicated area for managing global conferences (PEP), masterclasses, and mentorship applications.
- **Prestige Press Archive:** A high-impact strip showcasing features in Vogue, Netflix, and Harper's Bazaar.
- **Dynamic Workshop Checkout:** Integrated registration and payment for educational events (Supabase + Stripe).

### 4. User Journeys
1. **The Connoisseur's Scroll:** Visitor lands on home -> experiences the full-screen cinematic hero -> scrolls through the iconic masonry grid -> portals into a "Journal Story."
2. **The Education Path:** Aspiring photographer clicks "Education" -> filters by "Masterclasses" -> views upcoming dates -> completes registration for an intensive workshop.
3. **The Prestige Check:** Brand manager scrolls to the "Press" section -> views Netflix series features -> clicks "Contact" to initiate a high-end commission.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No automated "Instant Quotes" (Luxury services require human vetting).
- No community "Member Area" (Future phase).
- No public "Print Shop" (Focus is on high-end services and education first).

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** Sophisticated Editorial. Pure white/black, sharp 0px corners, wide-tracked sans typography.
- **Media:** Must handle 4K stills and high-bitrate video loops without buffering.
- **Routing:** Must seamlessly bridge the personal brand (Joseph) and the team brand (Stories).
