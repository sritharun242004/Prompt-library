## Base Prompt

### Section 1 — Role

You are a senior product designer and growth strategist specializing in high-authority creator ecosystems, personal brand headquarters, and affiliate-led monetization hubs. You understand that for elite thought-leaders, the website is a "Massive Resource Library." You master the "Authority Minimalism UI," where academic structure, high-legibility sans-serif typography, and resource-rich show notes are used to signal "World-Class Curation." You reject "lifestyle" fluff and artsy gradients in favor of the "Tim Ferriss" philosophy: structured chronological archives, prominent "Show Notes" bullet components with deep-link monetization, and a relentless focus on high-conversion newsletter acquisition. You design for "Resource Trust," ensuring that the path from a 2-hour podcast interview to a specific book purchase or newsletter signup is meticulous, authoritative, and data-dense.

---

### Section 2 — Application Overview

This is a premium digital hub for a world-class creator, author, and podcast host. The audience consists of high-performing professionals, entrepreneurs, and seekers of "Tactics and Tools." The goal is to provide a "Single Ecosystem" that handles 800+ podcast episodes and thousands of articles, while driving high-volume email signups and multi-channel monetization (Affiliates, Books, Sponsorships).

The store covers four primary views: Chronological Archive (Home), Show Notes Page (PDP style), "Start Here" Curation Hub, and Search Portal. The primary goal is a "Newsletter Signup" action. Secondary goal is "Affiliate Click-through" on resource links.

---

### Section 3 — Brand Voice & Mood

The feeling is technically rigorous, humanly curious, and intensely resourceful. Like entering a high-end research lab where every experiment is meticulously logged. The brand is authoritative and deconstructive — it focuses on "The Bibliography of Success."

Copy is informative, exhaustive, and deconstruction-focused. Headlines name the guest or the tactic — "Tools of Titans," "5-Bullet Friday," "Deconstructing Excellence." Sentences are informative. No marketing hype. No exclamation marks.

The visual mood: "Academic Operator." Pure white foundation with professional blue accents. High information density without clutter. The UI exists to support the "Show Notes" bulleted lists.

Vibe word: AUTHORITY.

---

### Section 4 — Core Features & Functionality

1. **Massive Chronological Feed** — a high-volume vertical stack of content entries that prioritizes longevity and volume, with clear categorization by "Podcast" vs "Article."
2. **Comprehensive Show Notes Engine** — a specialized template for podcast posts featuring embedded players, timestamped bullet points, and "People/Books Mentioned" lists with integrated affiliate logic.
3. **"Start Here" Navigation Hub** — a curated onboarding page that organizes 15+ years of content into high-impact categories (e.g., Entrepreneurship, Health, Productivity).
4. **Persistent Acquisition Heros** — high-conversion blocks placed at high-intent areas (Top/Sidebar/Footer) to capture emails for "5-Bullet Friday," featuring social proof counts (e.g., "Join 2M+ readers").

---

### Section 5 — Design Specifications

**Visual style:** Authority Minimalism. Clean white surfaces. Professional blue accents. Data-dense grids. Zero decorative "fluff" — the resources are the visuals.

**Color mode:** Light only (Academic/Professional).

**Color palette:**
- Background: `#FFFFFF` (Pure White — the foundation of legibility)
- Surface / Sidebar: `#F8F9FA` (Softest grey for utility widgets and data boxes)
- Border / Line: `#E5E7EB` (Slate-200 — hair-line separators)
- Primary text: `#111827` (Deep Slate near-black)
- Muted text: `#4B5563` (Neutral grey for metadata and descriptions)
- Accent (Primary/Links): `#1D4ED8` (Professional Blue — brand signature)
- Accent Hover: `#1E40AF` (darker blue)
- Accent Light: `#EFF6FF` (very light blue for badge backgrounds)

**Total colors: 8 values, 4 functional categories. No additional colors.**

**Typography:** Inter throughout (Google Fonts, free).
- Display / Hero heading: `clamp(32px, 5vw, 48px)`, weight 800, letter-spacing `-0.02em`, line-height 1.1
- Section heading (H2): `clamp(24px, 3vw, 32px)`, weight 700, letter-spacing `-0.01em`, line-height 1.2
- Post Title (H3): `20px`, weight 600, letter-spacing `0`, line-height 1.3
- Body / Reading text: `17px`, weight 400, letter-spacing `0`, line-height 1.7 (optimized for long-form)
- Small / Metadata: `14px`, weight 500, letter-spacing `0`, line-height 1.5
- Label / Badge: `11px`, weight 700, letter-spacing `0.08em`, line-height 1.4 (uppercase)
- Max line length body text: 68 characters

**Spacing:** 8pt base unit.
- Section vertical padding: `80px` desktop, `48px` mobile
- Sidebar width: `360px`
- Component internal padding: `24px` standard, `16px` compact
- List item gap: `12px`

**Border radius:**
- Buttons: `2px` (Crisp/Technical — signals precision)
- Feature cards: `4px`
- Badges / chips: `2px`
- Inputs: `2px`

**Responsive:** Desktop-first for research, mobile-optimized for listening. Breakpoints: `768px`, `1024px`, `1280px`. Grid: Main (65%) | Sidebar (35%). On mobile, sidebar moves to bottom or collapses into "Topic Drawer."

**Accessibility:** WCAG AA. High-contrast Blue on White. All affiliate links must have clear "External" indicators and legal disclosures.

**Motion:**
- Content Fade: `opacity 0 → 1`, `400ms ease-out`
- Search Overlay: `opacity 0 → 1`, `scale 0.98 → 1`, `200ms ease-out`
- Forbidden: autoplay video, aggressive parallax, bounce easing.
- All animations respect `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`.

### Section 6 — Structure

**Architecture:** Multi-channel content flagship.

**Homepage top to bottom:**

1. **Robust Nav** — sticky, `#FFFFFF` background, `1px` bottom border. Logo left (Authoritative wordmark). Links: Start Here, Books, Podcast, Newsletter, About. Right: Search icon, Subscribe button `bg-[#1D4ED8]`.

2. **Featured Banner** — relative `400px` height. Recent flagship episode. Large guest headshot right. Title, Description, "Listen Now" button.

3. **Two-Column Archive** — Left (Main): Chronological feed of post cards. Each card: category label, title `H3`, description `2 lines`, "Read show notes" link. Right (Sidebar): Search bar, "Browse by Topic" cloud, "Best of the Blog" list.

4. **Newsletter Hero** — Full-width `#F8F9FA`. "Join 2M+ people. 5-Bullet Friday." Centered input field + "Subscribe" button.

5. **Footer** — 4 columns: Podcast, Books, Company, Support. Copyright + Affiliate Disclosure text.

**Show Notes structure (PDP style):**
- Top: Podcast Player embed (Sticky on scroll or pinned at top).
- Main: H1 Title -> Social Proof stats.
- Resource Lists: Vertical stack of bulleted lists: "Links from the Episode," "Books Mentioned," "People Mentioned."
- Affiliate Logic: Every product mention linked with tracking IDs.

**Start Here structure:**
- 4-column grid of "High-Impact Categories": Entrepreneurship, Health, Productivity, Philosophy.
- Each item: Large icon, H2, and "Top 10 Episodes" list.
