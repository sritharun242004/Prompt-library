# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## AuthorityArchive Creator Hub · lp_platform_05

### 1. Product Vision
"AuthorityArchive" is a digital sanctuary for deconstructing excellence. It moves beyond standard "blogging" to create a high-performance intellectual headquarters where 800+ podcast episodes and thousands of articles are archived for public discovery. The platform prioritizes "Resource Density" through meticulously timestamped show notes and converts audience trust into a multi-channel monetization engine, providing elite creators with a digital presence that signals world-class curation and professional reliability.

**Success Metric:** "Newsletter Conversion" (percentage of users who sign up for the weekly digest after viewing a show-notes page) exceeds 15%.

### 2. Personas
**Ben — The Self-Optimizer (User)**
- Listens to 2-3 hours of the podcast weekly and needs to find specific book or tool recommendations mentioned.
- Needs: A fast, authoritative way to browse timestamped lists and direct links to products.
- Frustration: Vague "episode summaries" that don't provide the granular detail of the actual conversation.

**The Brand — The High-End Sponsor (Client)**
- Needs a prestigious, high-performance environment to showcase their sponsorship.
- Needs: Clear attribution, professional ad-read placements, and high-legibility social proof strips.
- Frustration: Unorganized blogs where sponsorship value is lost in a sea of visual clutter.

### 3. Core Features
- **Authority Chronological Feed:** A high-volume vertical list that organizes 15+ years of content with clear category indicators.
- **Comprehensive Show Notes Engine:** A specialized template for podcast posts featuring embedded players and exhaustive "People/Books/Links Mentioned" lists.
- **Integrated Affiliate Linking:** A system for embedding third-party product links within resource lists with automated disclosure tracking.
- **"Start Here" Curation Hub:** A directed-discovery page that organizes the archive into high-impact "Greatest Hits" categories.
- **Persistent Acquisition Heros:** High-conversion signup blocks for the "5-Bullet Friday" newsletter, placed at key intent moments.

### 4. User Journeys
1. **The Resource Deep-Dive:** Visitor arrives from social -> reads the 4,000-word show notes for a Naval Ravikant interview -> clicks an Amazon link for "Meditations" -> signs up for the newsletter.
2. **The Newcomer Curation:** New user clicks "Start Here" -> selects "Entrepreneurship" -> views a curated list of 10 must-listen episodes -> enters the first episode's show notes.
3. **The Search Quest:** User searches "Metformin" -> sees 5 podcast episodes and 12 articles discussing the topic -> portals directly to the timestamp in the show notes.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No "Direct Course Sales" in this phase (Focus is on content and affiliate/sponsor monetization).
- No "Member Login" for content gating (Maintain the open-archive broadcast model).
- No community "Forum" (Maintain the curated, authoritative voice of the host).

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** Authority Minimalism. Pure white, sharp 0px/2px corners, high-contrast sans typography.
- **Performance:** Sub-1s page loads for long-form text; sub-100ms response for archive search.
- **Legal:** Must support automated "Affiliate Disclosure" banners on all pages containing tracked links.
