# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · lp_platform_05

### Epic 1: Massive Discovery
*As a visitor, I want a homepage that immediately proves the depth and authority of the content.*
- **Story 1.1:** Scan 15+ years of content via a vertical, high-volume chronological feed.
- **Story 1.2:** Find specific topics (e.g., "Ketosis" or "Investing") via a robust sidebar tag cloud.
- **Story 1.3:** See social proof download counts (700M+) that establish world-class status.

### Epic 2: Resource Deep-Dives (Show Notes)
*As a listener, I want to find every resource mentioned in a conversation instantly.*
- **Story 2.1:** View exhaustive bulleted lists of books and tools with direct affiliate links.
- **Story 2.2:** Click a timestamp to jump to a specific moment in the embedded audio player.
- **Story 2.3:** Read about the "People Mentioned" with links to their own authoritative work.

### Epic 3: Strategic Curation
*As a new user, I want a guided path through the thousands of articles available.*
- **Story 3.1:** Click "Start Here" to see the most impactful interviews categorized by goal.
- **Story 3.2:** Search 800+ episodes by guest name or keyword in under 100ms.
- **Story 3.3:** Discover evergreen "Top 10" lists that reduce the fatigue of an immense archive.

### Epic 4: Ecosystem Loyalty (Newsletter)
*As a seeker of "Tactics and Tools," I want to join the inner-circle community.*
- **Story 4.1:** Subscribe to "5-Bullet Friday" via a high-conversion hero block.
- **Story 4.2:** Receive "Exclusive Chapters" or bonuses as a reward for newsletter signup.
- **Story 4.3:** Experience a 1-click subscription management interface that respects my inbox.
