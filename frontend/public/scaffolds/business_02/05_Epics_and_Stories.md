# 05 — Epics and Stories
## Mass-Market Coaching Website · business_platform_02

---

### Epic 1 — Proof and Credibility Communication

Story: users can verify institute outcomes and rankings before committing to an inquiry.

Acceptance Criteria:
- results blocks display year, program, and source attribution
- proof modules render legibly on mobile without horizontal scroll
- no uncontextualized claim ("top results", "best institute") appears without qualifier

---

### Epic 2 — Program and Mode Pathway Clarity

Story: users can identify the relevant program track, format, and batch schedule for their exam target.

Acceptance Criteria:
- program matrix is complete and filterable by exam type (JEE/NEET/Foundation)
- class, exam target, and delivery mode (classroom/online/hybrid) are clearly labelled
- no dead-end pages — every program page links to a center or inquiry CTA

---

### Epic 3 — Center Discovery at Scale

Story: users can find the nearest city center with contact details and available batches.

Acceptance Criteria:
- center pages display address, contact number, map embed, and current batch schedule
- inquiry CTA is visible without scrolling on mobile
- city/center filter works across all active locations

---

### Epic 4 — Admissions and Scholarship Conversion

Story: users can submit counseling inquiries and scholarship applications with minimal friction.

Acceptance Criteria:
- forms validate client-side on blur and server-side on submit
- required fields are clearly marked; optional fields do not block submission
- confirmation message and expected follow-up timeline shown on success
- spam/bot submissions rejected without blocking legitimate users

---

### Epic 5 — Support and Policy Trust

Story: users can access admission policies and support contacts before and after submitting a form.

Acceptance Criteria:
- policy links visible within one scroll of every conversion CTA
- support contact path (phone, chat, or email) accessible from all funnel pages
- refund, deferral, and scholarship terms reachable without requiring login
