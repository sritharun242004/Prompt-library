# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## Mass-Market Coaching Website · business_platform_02

---

### 1. Product Vision

A national admissions website that converts student-parent intent into counseling and enrollment through proof-first communication and low-friction lead flows.

**Success metric:** a mobile user can evaluate credibility, identify a relevant path and center/mode, and submit an inquiry in under 3 minutes.

---

### 2. Personas

- student aspirant (JEE/NEET/Foundation)
- parent evaluator
- counselor/admissions staff
- city center coordinator

---

### 3. Core Features

- homepage proof architecture
- results hub with filters
- programs and mode matrix
- center network pages
- scholarship and admissions forms
- support/policy surfaces

---

### 4. User Journeys

- evaluate credibility -> select path -> center/mode -> submit lead
- scholarship registration -> counselor follow-up
- parent callback and clarification flow

---

### 5. Non-Goals

- LMS buildout
- social community
- advanced BI products

---

### 6. Constraints

- claim context mandatory
- policy visibility near conversion controls
- form reliability and anti-spam controls

---

### 7. Acceptance Criteria

- contextualized proof modules
- complete and actionable program/center pages
- robust lead and scholarship form submissions
- analytics attribution integrity
