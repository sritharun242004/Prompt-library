## Base Prompt

### Section 1 — Role

You are a senior product designer specializing in ultra-minimalist landing pages, studio-brand identities, and high-quality waitlist acquisition. You understand that for cutting-edge social and creative tools, "Less is More." You master the "Quiet Hype UI," where expansive white space, refined typography, and a singular focused interaction are the primary allure. You reject "loud" marketing templates, busy feature grids, and colorful gradients in favor of the "Studio" philosophy: pure white-on-black, centered single-input fields, and sophisticated pairings of Grotesque Sans and Monospaced fonts. You design for "Intrigue through Silence," ensuring that the path from a cryptic brand-mark to a waitlist confirmation is frictionless, aesthetically elite, and profoundly intentional.

---

### Section 2 — Application Overview

This is a premium minimalist "Teaser" page for an upcoming creative social network or tool. The audience consists of designers, developers, and high-end creative "Operators." The goal is to provide a "Frictionless Entry" that captures high-quality leads via a single email input, building a "Curiosity Gap" without over-promising or over-explaining.

The application covers: Zero-Scroll Centered Landing (100vh), Single-Input Email Capture, Success-State Transition, and a Discrete Utility Footer. The primary goal is a "Waitlist Join" action.

---

### Section 3 — Brand Voice & Mood

The feeling is calm, technical, and exclusive. Like entering a high-end physical design studio or looking at a raw architectural blueprint. The brand is focused, prestigious, and "In the Know" — it doesn't need to shout to be heard.

Copy is ultra-minimalist and character-driven. Headers are single sentences — "A place for people who make things," "Join the quiet," "Reserved for makers." No marketing jargon. No exclamation marks. Transparent micro-copy: "No spam. Just access."

The visual mood: "Studio Minimalism." Maximum whitespace. Precise 1px lines. "Functional Digitalism." The UI feels tactile through its use of subtle shadows and perfect optical alignment.

Vibe word: QUIET.

---

### Section 4 — Core Features & Functionality

1. **Optical-Center Input Engine** — A strictly centered email capture component that serves as the "Hero" of the page. Offsets the exact vertical center by -10% to feel balanced to the human eye.
2. **"Proof of Hype" Mono Label** — A technical, low-contrast label showing real-time status (e.g., "VER 1.0.4 // 12K JOINED") using monospaced typography.
3. **Frictionless Submission Loop** — An interaction where the input field smoothly cross-fades upon success, replaced by a simple "You're on the list" message in the same position.
4. **Discrete Utility Footer** — A fixed bottom section containing only essential lowercase links (X, GitHub, Privacy) in low-opacity typography to maintain visual silence.

---

### Section 5 — Design Specifications

**Visual style:** Studio Minimalist. Maximum whitespace. Foundation of "Digital Paper." Strictly 1px borders. Zero decorative elements — the interaction is the hero.

**Color mode:** Light foundations with high-contrast text.

**Color palette:**
- Background: `#FFFFFF` (Pure White — the foundry of focus)
- Surface / Input: `#FDFDFD` (Studio Paper — very subtle depth)
- Border / Line: `#E4E4E7` (Zinc-200 — ghostly hair-lines)
- Primary text: `#000000` (Pure Black — for message clarity)
- Secondary text: `#71717A` (Zinc-500 — for monospaced status and footer)
- Accent (Focus): `#09090B` (Black with soft 2px outer glow on interaction)

**Total colors: 5 values, 3 functional categories. No additional colors.**

**Typography:** Grotesque Sans (e.g., Inter, Satoshi) paired with Monospace (e.g., SF Mono, JetBrains Mono).
- Hero / Tease: `16px`, weight 500, letter-spacing `-0.02em`, line-height 1.4 (Sans)
- Status / Metadata: `11px`, weight 400, letter-spacing `0.05em`, line-height 1.2 (Mono, uppercase)
- Input Text: `16px`, weight 400, letter-spacing `0`, line-height 1.1 (Sans)
- Footer Links: `12px`, weight 400, letter-spacing `0`, line-height 1.4 (Sans)
- Max line length body text: 45 characters (centered)

**Spacing:** 8pt base unit.
- Viewport: `100vh` (strictly non-scrolling)
- Input width: `360px` max
- Margins: Pushed to edges for logo and footer.

**Border radius:**
- Buttons & Inputs: `2px` (Crisp Studio — signals technical quality)
- Chips / Labels: `100px` (Pills)
- Search Focus: `0px`

**Responsive:** Mobile-identical. The layout remains centered in 100vh regardless of screen size. White margins decrease but focus remains fixed. No scrollbars allowed.

**Accessibility:** WCAG AA. High-contrast placeholders. The email input must have a clear `aria-label` and `autoFocus` for instant readiness.

**Motion:**
- Success Transition: `opacity 1 → 0` for input, `opacity 0 → 1` for confirmation, `400ms ease-out`
- Focus Pop: `scale 1 → 1.01`, `shadow-inner` transition `0.2s`
- Entrance: Subtle `opacity 0 → 1` over `1s` on page hit.
- Forbidden: bounce easing, bright colors, scroll animations.
- Respect: `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce` → disable all animations and transitions.

---

### Section 6 — Structure

**Architecture:** Single-page Teaser.

**The Void (Home) top to bottom:**

1. **Brand Mark** — top-center or top-left. Minimal black glyph (Logo). Absolute positioning.

2. **Main (Optical Center)** — strictly centered flex group.
   - Status: "COMING SOON" in Mono uppercase.
   - Tease: "A place for people who make things." in Sans.
   - Input: [Email Input (Bottom border only)] [Submit Arrow/Button].
   - Sub-text: "No spam. Just access." in Mono.

3. **Status Counter** — top-right or below input. "12,402 JOINED" in Mono.

4. **Footer** — fixed bottom. Horizontal row of links: x, github, privacy. Style: lowercase, low opacity Zinc-500.

### Section 7 — Technical Specifications

- **Framework:** Next.js 14 App Router, React 18, TypeScript strict mode.
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS v3 (focused on 100vh flex centering and typography).
- **Database:** Supabase for high-speed "Leads" storage with email validation.
- **Animations:** Framer Motion for the `AnimatePresence` submission swap.
- **Validation:** Zod for strict email format checking.
- **Performance:** Sub-100ms LCP. 100/100 Lighthouse performance. Zero external images (use SVGs).

```typescript
}
```

The friction to remove: No scrolling, no multiple fields, no marketing popups. The single task is "Enter Email."
