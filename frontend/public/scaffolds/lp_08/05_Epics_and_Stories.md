# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · lp_platform_08

### Epic 1: Focused Intent (Layout)
*As a visitor, I want a page that loads instantly and gives me one clear task so that I don't feel overwhelmed.*
- **Story 1.1:** Experience a centered, non-scrolling layout with maximum white space.
- **Story 1.2:** See a 1-sentence value proposition that defines the audience ("For people who make").
- **Story 1.3:** Experience a "Studio" aesthetic that signals high-end quality and technical precision.

### Epic 2: Frictionless Entry (Waitlist)
*As an early adopter, I want to join the list with as few clicks as possible.*
- **Story 2.1:** Enter my email into a centered input field that auto-focuses on hit.
- **Story 2.2:** Receive real-time validation feedback if I enter an incorrect email format.
- **Story 2.3:** Experience a subtle "Success" animation that confirms I am on the list without page reloads.

### Epic 3: Studio Identity (Branding)
*As a curator, I want to see brand markers that establish the tool's pedigree.*
- **Story 3.1:** View a minimalist brand glyph that acts as the "Anchor" of the studio's identity.
- **Story 3.2:** See monospaced labels for version numbers and joined counts that signal "Quiet Hype."
- **Story 3.3:** Access social links (X, GitHub) in a discrete footer to verify the developers.

### Epic 4: Technical Excellence (Performance)
*As a developer, I want the page to load and respond as fast as my local terminal.*
- **Story 4.1:** Experience sub-200ms page loads with zero layout shift (CLS: 0).
- **Story 4.2:** Use a page that works flawlessly on mobile with a centered, thumb-friendly input.
- **Story 4.3:** Experience high-contrast typography that is 100% accessible to screen readers.
