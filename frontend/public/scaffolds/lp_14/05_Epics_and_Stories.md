# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · lp_platform_14

### Epic 1: Deconstructed Discovery
*As a visitor, I want a homepage that immediately shows the internal engineering quality.*
- **Story 1.1:** Experience a high-fidelity hero where the product re-assembles itself on scroll.
- **Story 1.2:** See massive, authoritative metrics (e.g., 100k RPM) that prove machine power.
- **Story 1.3:** Browse a layout that feels like a professional technical briefing rather than an ad.

### Epic 2: The Complete System (Accessories)
*As a buyer, I want to see exactly what tools are included to understand the machine's value.*
- **Story 2.1:** View a clean 2D grid of top-down tool photos in the "In the box" section.
- **Story 2.2:** Click a tool to see a short video demonstrating its specific problem-solving capability.
- **Story 2.3:** Experience a "Versatility" narrative that justifies the premium price-point.

### Epic 3: Scientific Proof (Deep-Dives)
*As a performance seeker, I want to understand the science behind the product.*
- **Story 3.1:** Watch interactive diagrams explaining airflow or microscopic filtration.
- **Story 3.2:** Read about "Hair Science" or "Dust Research" to verify the brand's expertise.
- **Story 3.3:** Transition from an emotional video into a data-backed technical explanation seamlessly.

### Epic 4: Technical Comparison
*As a rational shopper, I want to compare specs to decide which model is right for me.*
- **Story 4.1:** Browse a meticulous table of specs including bin volume, battery run time, and weight.
- **Story 4.2:** Use a sticky sub-navigation to jump directly to the technical details at any point.
- **Story 4.3:** Select between different machine models to see live technical spec differences.
