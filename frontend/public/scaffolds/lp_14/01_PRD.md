# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## IndustrialPrecision Landing Platform · lp_platform_14

### 1. Product Vision
"IndustrialPrecision" is a digital hallmark for engineering excellence. It moves beyond standard "lifestyle marketing" to create a technical briefing environment where the product's internal science is the primary value driver. The platform prioritizes "Performance Visualization" through deconstructed heros and leverages data-dense spec grids to establish authority, providing premium hardware brands with a digital stage that signals innovation, craftsmanship, and measurable success.

**Success Metric:** "Technical-Engagement" (percentage of users who interact with the "Exploded View" and proceed to view the detailed Spec Table) exceeds 35%.

### 2. Personas
**Ben — The Performance Seeker (User)**
- A health-conscious or tech-savvy buyer who wants to understand "how it works" before paying a premium.
- Needs: Immediate proof of internal engineering, scannable technical metrics, and clarity on included accessories.
- Frustration: Vague "magic" claims that lack scientific backing or deconstructed visuals.

**The Team — The Technical Marketers (Client)**
- Managing a high-AOV product with 20+ patents and 10+ accessories.
- Needs: A platform that handles complex technical data elegantly and builds long-term trust through owner-support integration.
- Frustration: Inflexible templates that don't handle the "System-First" (machine + tools) nature of the product.

### 3. Core Features
- **Deconstructed "Motor-First" Hero:** A high-fidelity section showing an exploded view of internal motors, batteries, or filters.
- **Performance Typography System:** Massive, high-contrast text for technical metrics (RPM, Microns, Airflow) as primary visual anchors.
- **Top-Down Accessory Hub:** A signature 2D grid showing the "In the Box" ecosystem with clean functional labels.
- **Scientific Deep-Dive Modules:** Modular sections using video and diagrams to explain the "Science" (e.g., Hair Heat Damage, Dust Filtration).
- **High-Contrast Spec Comparison:** A meticulous tabular hub for machine-specific technical details with hairline separators.

### 4. User Journeys
1. **The Engineering Briefing:** Visitor lands on hub -> scrolls to see deconstructed motor animation -> scans massive "100k RPM" text -> clicks "Explore Technology."
2. **The System Audit:** User reaches "In the box" -> scans 8 specialized tools -> clicks "Anti-tangle brush" to see a video of it in action -> understands the versatile value.
3. **The Logical Close:** User finishes tech-dive -> scrolls to bottom spec table -> verifies weight and battery life -> clicks "Add to Cart" in the sticky sub-nav.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No "Community Feedback" wall (Maintain the authoritative laboratory voice).
- No "Generic Lifestyle" photography (Focus on macro-hardware shots and renders).
- No complex "Personalized Accounts" for browsing (Keep discovery frictionless).

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** Industrial Minimalism. Laboratory white foundations, copper/purple accents, sharp Inter typography.
- **Performance:** Sub-1s initial paint for high-res product renders.
- **Tone:** Must use "Patented," "Intelligent," and "Scientific Proof" terminology.
