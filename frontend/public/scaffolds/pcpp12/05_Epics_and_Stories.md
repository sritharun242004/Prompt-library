# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · pcpp_platform_12

### Epic 1: High-Signal Identity
*As a visitor, I want a profile that immediately establishes professional value and personality.*
- **Story 1.1:** Scan a vertical work timeline with clear role and impact summaries.
- **Story 1.2:** See what a creator is "Currently working on" via a horizontal status scroller.
- **Story 1.3:** Navigate through different identity "tabs" (Work, Writing, About) without page reloads.

### Epic 2: Project Depth (Layers)
*As a recruiter, I want to see the evidence and process behind the job title.*
- **Story 2.1:** Expand a work history card to reveal a beautiful, visual case study.
- **Story 2.2:** View high-resolution imagery and video embeds within the project layer.
- **Story 2.3:** Understand the specific tech-stack used through minimalist tags.

### Epic 3: Thought Leadership (Writing)
*As a peer, I want to read the author's deep-dives on design and tech.*
- **Story 3.1:** Browse a list of long-form articles in a dedicated "Writing" tab.
- **Story 3.2:** Enjoy a bookish reading experience with high-readability typography.
- **Story 3.3:** Discover related work projects linked at the end of an essay.

### Epic 4: Network Discovery
*As a collaborator, I want to find talent based on their actual projects and updates.*
- **Story 4.1:** Browse a curated feed of latest projects from across the network.
- **Story 4.2:** Filter talent by specific roles (e.g., "Product Designer") and view their visual history.
- **Story 4.3:** Portal directly from a project in the feed to the person's full visual CV.
