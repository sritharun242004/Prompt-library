# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## ROIMaster Career Edtech · pcpp_platform_20

### 1. Product Vision
"ROIMaster" is a digital hallmark for professional career acceleration. It moves beyond standard "learning" to create a high-authority "Outcome Engine" where students can bridge the gap between their current role and their dream title through university-certified programs. The platform prioritizes "Evidence of Impact" through salary hike statistics and prestigious university seals, providing elite educators with a digital presence that commands corporate respect and drives high-AOV career enrollments.

**Success Metric:** "Syllabus-to-Lead" conversion rate (percentage of users who download a program brochure and provide a verified phone/email) exceeds 25%.

### 2. Personas
**Sanjay — The Mid-Career Manager (User)**
- Working in IT and needs to transition into a "Senior AI Product" role.
- Needs: Immediate proof of university certification (e.g., IIT Delhi), salary growth stats for previous alumni, and a downloadable curriculum to share with his employer for sponsorship.
- Frustration: Vague course outcomes or sites that don't provide a clear "Placement Support" roadmap.

**Dr. Anita — The University Partner (Client)**
- A director at a top-tier business school looking to scale executive education online.
- Needs: A digital platform that maintains the school's "Premium Brand Aura," handles high-volume global inquiries, and provides structured mentorship UIs.
- Frustration: Cluttered e-learning sites that look "amateur" and cheapen the university's brand.

### 3. Core Features
- **University Trust Strip:** A high-impact horizontal logo wall featuring global academic partners (IIT, Duke, etc.) with verified seals.
- **ROI Visualization Hub:** Data-dense components showing "Average 57% Salary Hike," "800+ Hiring Partners," and "1.5 Lakh+ Alumni."
- **Brochure-Lead Engine:** A high-conversion CTA system on course heros and cards that exchanges a PDF syllabus for a lead.
- **Career Transition Feed:** A video-led section showcasing before-and-after learner profiles (Previous Title/CTC vs. Current Title/CTC).
- **Region-Aware Expert Portal:** A multi-step inquiry form that routes users to "Indian" or "International" admission experts based on IP/Logic.

### 4. User Journeys
1. **The Goal Discovery:** Visitor lands on home -> selects "Get a Promotion" -> enters the "Executive MBA" sub-site -> reviews the Duke University certification badge.
2. **The Impact Check:** User scrolls to "Career Outcomes" -> interacts with a salary-growth chart (Recharts) -> watches a video story of a learner moving from Amazon to Google.
3. **The High-Intent Lead:** User clicks "Download Syllabus" -> enters name/phone/email -> receives the PDF and an instant WhatsApp confirmation with an expert's name.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No "Free-for-all" community (Privacy is mandatory for executive learners).
- No standard "Video Course" player for individual sale (Focus is on long-duration, high-AOV programs).
- No gamified points or badges (Focus is on real-world job titles and salary).

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** Professional Corporate. upGrad Red accents, sharp 4px corners, authoritative Montserrat typography.
- **Data:** Lead data must be securely stored in Supabase with strict encryption and routed to CRMs (Salesforce/Hubspot placeholders).
- **Scale:** Must handle hundreds of program-specific landing pages with unique university branding.
