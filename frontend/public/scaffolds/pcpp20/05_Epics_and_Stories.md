# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · pcpp_platform_20

### Epic 1: Institutional Authority
*As a visitor, I want a homepage that immediately proves the platform's prestige and partnerships.*
- **Story 1.1:** View a horizontal strip of world-class university logos (IIT, IIM, etc.).
- **Story 1.2:** See proof of corporate hiring through an "800+ Partners" logo wall.
- **Story 1.3:** Experience a clean, "Boardroom" aesthetic that respects my professional status.

### Epic 2: Evidence-Based Curation (ROI)
*As an operator, I want to see the specific career outcomes of a program before committing.*
- **Story 2.1:** Browse salary hike statistics for each program in high-contrast visual blocks.
- **Story 2.2:** Watch video testimonials of real learners who successfully transitioned roles.
- **Story 2.3:** Understand the "Success Score" (e.g., 93% impact) of the alumni network.

### Epic 3: Curriculum Lead-Gen
*As a high-intent user, I want to review the detailed syllabus in exchange for a consultation.*
- **Story 3.1:** Click "Download Syllabus" to trigger a clean, scannable lead form.
- **Story 3.2:** Receive a high-quality PDF brochure and an automated follow-up.
- **Story 3.3:** View the "Executive Alumni" benefits included with each certification.

### Epic 4: Personalized Expert Consultation
*As a busy professional, I want to speak with a human who understands my specific career goals.*
- **Story 4.1:** Access a multi-step inquiry form that asks about my "Career Ambition."
- **Story 4.2:** Get matched with an "Indian" or "Foreign" admission expert based on my details.
- **Story 4.3:** Receive an immediate WhatsApp/Email confirmation with the expert's name and availability.
