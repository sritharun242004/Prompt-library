# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · pcpp_platform_19

### Epic 1: High-Speed Goal Discovery
*As a new student, I want to find my specific exam instantly among hundreds of options.*
- **Story 1.1:** Use a searchable goal modal to find "UPSC" or "IIT JEE" in under 1 second.
- **Story 1.2:** Browse exam categories (e.g., Engineering, Medical) on the central hub.
- **Story 1.3:** See massive social-proof stats that reassure me of the platform's scale.

### Epic 2: Pedigree Trust (Educators)
*As an aspirant, I want to see the qualifications of the teachers before I subscribe.*
- **Story 2.1:** Browse "Top Educators" for my specific goal with clear subject tags.
- **Story 2.2:** View "Minutes Watched" and follower counts as evidence of teacher popularity.
- **Story 2.3:** Attend a "Special Class" (Free) to experience the teacher's style before buying.

### Epic 3: Strategic Conversion (Tiers)
*As a serious student, I want to understand the extra value of the premium 'Iconic' tier.*
- **Story 3.1:** Compare Plus vs. Iconic features in a clear, side-by-side table.
- **Story 3.2:** See the "1:1 Mentorship" highlighted in Iconic Gold as the key differentiator.
- **Story 3.3:** Choose a long-term plan (e.g., 24 months) with a clear monthly-savings calculation.

### Epic 4: Real-Time Classroom
*As a subscriber, I want a live learning experience that feels like being in a top-tier institute.*
- **Story 4.1:** Join a live class with zero buffering and high-resolution video.
- **Story 4.2:** Ask questions in real-time chat and participate in live polls for instant feedback.
- **Story 4.3:** "Raise my Hand" to speak directly with the educator during the session.
