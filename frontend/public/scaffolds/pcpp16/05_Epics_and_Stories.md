# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · pcpp_platform_16

### Epic 1: Deep Curation (Notes)
*As a visitor, I want to quickly find and learn from the author's research.*
- **Story 1.1:** Search 250+ book notes by title, author, or genre in under 100ms.
- **Story 1.2:** Read a "Top 3 Lessons" summary to decide if a full book note is for me.
- **Story 1.3:** See a 1-10 rating and a "My Recommendation" score for every book.

### Epic 2: Structured Mastery (Courses)
*As a student, I want a structured path to master technical skills like AI-Building.*
- **Story 2.1:** Browse a clear curriculum that transitions from theory to practical builds.
- **Story 2.2:** Track my progress across lessons and modules automatically.
- **Story 2.3:** Access "Pro" community resources and live workshop dates.

### Epic 3: Professional Intelligence (Newsletter)
*As a subscriber, I want a weekly digest of the best ideas in my inbox.*
- **Story 3.1:** Join 50,000+ readers via a high-conversion, value-first landing page.
- **Story 3.2:** Access a "Lead Magnet" (e.g., a "Most Recommended Books" PDF) upon signup.
- **Story 3.3:** Manage my subscription preferences through a clean, one-click interface.

### Epic 4: Identity & Trust (Profile)
*As a potential collaborator, I want to understand the author's range and authority.*
- **Story 4.1:** Browse a "Start Here" page that curates the most impactful work.
- **Story 4.2:** View a technical list of "Tools I Use" and personal "Building" philosophy.
- **Story 4.3:** Experience a site that feels like high-performance software, signaling technical competence.
