# 05 — Epics and Stories
## Coaching Business Website · business_platform_01

---

### Epic 1 — Proof and Credibility

Story: users can verify institute outcomes quickly.

Acceptance Criteria:
- results are contextualized by year/program
- proof blocks are legible and non-ambiguous

---

### Epic 2 — Program and Pathway Clarity

Story: users can identify relevant program track and format.

Acceptance Criteria:
- program matrix complete and filterable
- class/exam/mode information clear

---

### Epic 3 — Center Discovery

Story: users can find applicable city/center and contact details.

Acceptance Criteria:
- center pages show contact, map, batches, and inquiry CTA

---

### Epic 4 — Admissions Conversion

Story: users can submit counseling and scholarship forms with minimal friction.

Acceptance Criteria:
- forms validate and submit successfully
- confirmation and follow-up messaging clear

---

### Epic 5 — Support and Policy Clarity

Story: users can access admission and support policies before submission.

Acceptance Criteria:
- policy links visible near relevant CTAs
- support contact path accessible from all funnel pages
