# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · lp_platform_03

### Epic 1: Aesthetic Invitation
*As a visitor, I want an event page that feels like a high-quality personal invitation.*
- **Story 1.1:** Experience a clean, centered layout with elegant serif typography.
- **Story 1.2:** See a high-resolution, full-width cover image that sets the event's mood.
- **Story 1.3:** Browse event details in an uncluttered environment with generous whitespace.

### Epic 2: Social Validation (Attendees)
*As a social attendee, I want to see who else is joining before I register.*
- **Story 2.1:** View a row of overlapping avatars representing people I know or admire.
- **Story 2.2:** See the total count of attendees ("+X others") to understand the event scale.
- **Story 2.3:** Hover over an avatar to see the name of a specific attendee.

### Epic 3: Frictionless Join
*As a busy professional, I want to register for an event in as few steps as possible.*
- **Story 3.1:** Click a persistent "Register" button that opens a simple sign-up modal.
- **Story 3.2:** Use my existing social profile to sign up in one tap.
- **Story 3.3:** Add the event to my personal calendar instantly after registration.

### Epic 4: Host Affinity
*As a fan, I want to follow my favorite hosts to stay updated on future events.*
- **Story 4.1:** View a host's profile with their upcoming "Community Calendar."
- **Story 4.2:** Click "Follow" to receive automated notifications about new event launches.
- **Story 4.3:** View the host's previous "Event Gallery" to verify the quality of their gatherings.
