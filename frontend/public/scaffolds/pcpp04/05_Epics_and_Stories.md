# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · pcpp_platform_04

### Epic 1: Multi-Project Universe
*As a visitor, I want to explore different "worlds" of work within a single creator hub.*
- **Story 1.1:** Portal between different project domains (e.g., hub -> matrimania.in).
- **Story 1.2:** Navigate back to the central hub from any project planet instantly.
- **Story 1.3:** See a high-impact summary of each "Universe" before entering.

### Epic 2: The Narrative Sequence
*As an art observer, I want to see how images relate to each other in a purposeful flow.*
- **Story 2.1:** Experience a photo-essay where images are paired to create new meaning.
- **Story 2.2:** Read interspersed field notes that provide context for the visual sequence.
- **Story 2.3:** Scroll through a 100-image story with a persistent progress indicator.

### Epic 3: Journalistic Depth
*As a journalist, I want to read the sociological research behind the photography.*
- **Story 3.1:** Browse a "Journal" of long-form interviews and technical field notes.
- **Story 3.2:** View "The Press" archive for a specific project to see its global impact.
- **Story 3.3:** Read a "Director's Statement" or essay that frames the entire project.

### Epic 4: Portal Discovery
*As a return visitor, I want to find the latest "News" and "AI Experiments" in the universe.*
- **Story 4.1:** Browse a "Latest News" grid on the central hub.
- **Story 4.2:** Access a dedicated section for computational and experimental work.
- **Story 4.3:** Find contact information through a witty and personalized "About" page.
