# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · pcpp_platform_05

### Epic 1: Immersive Grandeur
*As a visitor, I want a homepage that immediately feels like a cinematic masterpiece.*
- **Story 1.1:** Watch an auto-playing 4K trailer loop on the homepage.
- **Story 1.2:** Scroll through a "Featured Chapters" section with grand cover imagery.
- **Story 1.3:** See a high-impact "Celebrity" strip to establish trust and prestige.

### Epic 2: The StoryBook Experience
*As a bride-to-be, I want to see the emotional journey of other couples in a beautiful format.*
- **Story 2.1:** Read a romantic narrative introduction to each wedding story.
- **Story 2.2:** Browse a mix of grand landscape photos and intimate detail shots.
- **Story 2.3:** Watch the 4K wedding trailer within the chapter layout.

### Epic 3: Technical Excellence (Films)
*As a production manager, I want to see the technical range and cinematography skill.*
- **Story 3.1:** Browse a dedicated hub of 4K trailers and highlight films.
- **Story 3.2:** Watch films in a custom "Theater Mode" with high-bitrate playback.
- **Story 3.3:** Read about the equipment and crew used for grand destination shoots.

### Epic 4: Narrative Inquiries
*As a couple, I want to share my vision and story when reaching out to the firm.*
- **Story 4.1:** Fill out a detailed inquiry form that asks for "Our Story."
- **Story 4.2:** Provide wedding logistics (Date, Venue, Guest Count) in an elegant UI.
- **Story 4.3:** Receive a beautiful, branded confirmation email immediately.
