# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## StoryBookOpulence Wedding Portfolio · pcpp_platform_05

### 1. Product Vision
"StoryBookOpulence" is a luxury digital gallery that captures the grandeur and emotion of high-end Indian weddings. It moves beyond static galleries to create a "StoryBook" experience where each wedding is a cinematic chapter. The platform prioritizes high-production value video and vibrant photography, providing premier firms with a digital presence that matches the "Modern Royalty" expectations of their elite clients.

**Success Metric:** 25% of visitors initiate the "Your Story" inquiry flow, indicating strong emotional resonance with the portfolio.

### 2. Personas
**Sonia — The High-Net-Worth Bride (Client)**
- Planning a destination wedding in Udaipur and wants the "stars of the industry" to capture it.
- Needs: To feel an immediate emotional connection and see evidence of celebrity-grade production value.
- Frustration: Sites that look "cheap" or "amateur" with low-quality video or cluttered layouts.

**Vikram — The Celebrity Manager (Referrer)**
- Manages high-profile talent and needs a reliable, top-tier team for a high-stakes wedding.
- Needs: A fast, high-impact showcase of technical range (Drones, Underwater, 4K) and a history of discretion and excellence.
- Frustration: Lack of clear "Cinematography" vs "Photography" distinction and slow page loads.

### 3. Core Features
- **Full-Bleed Video Hero:** A high-impact, auto-playing trailer that immediately sets the "Cinematic" tone.
- **StoryBook Chapter Engine:** A narrative-driven layout system that treats each wedding as a distinct book chapter with its own title and intro.
- **4K Cinematography Hub:** A dedicated section for high-bitrate wedding films with custom-styled theater controls.
- **"Your Story" Inquiry Flow:** A detailed, multi-step form that captures the couple's narrative as part of the lead-gen process.
- **Celebrity Strip:** A specialized highlight section for high-profile client work to establish immediate prestige.

### 4. User Journeys
1. **The Inspiration Path:** Visitor lands on home -> watches the hero trailer -> scrolls to "Featured Chapters" -> clicks a story -> experiences the mix of film and high-vibrancy photos.
2. **The Discovery Path:** User navigates to "Films" -> filters by "Destination" -> watches a 4K Udaipur teaser -> clicks "Read the full story."
3. **The Inquiry Path:** User clicks "Contact" -> fills out the "Tell Us Your Story" form -> receives an automated "Thank You" with a personalized brand message.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No "Direct Booking" or "Pricing Packages" (High-end services require custom quoting).
- No community comments or public photo sharing (Focus is on the firm's curated voice).
- No standard "Stock" icons or generic UI kits.

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** Modern Royalty. Gold (#B59410) accents, rich maroons, sharp 0px corners, high-vibrancy color.
- **Performance:** Mandatory 4K video optimization (proxies for mobile).
- **Data:** Story content must be easily manageable via a CMS to support weekly updates.
