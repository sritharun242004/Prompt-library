# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · lp_platform_02

### Epic 1: Strategic Discovery
*As a visitor, I want a homepage that immediately communicates the forum's strategic themes.*
- **Story 1.1:** Experience a thematic "Innovation X" hero with abstract tech visuals.
- **Story 1.2:** Browse the forum's core "Pillars" via custom-designed iconography.
- **Story 1.3:** See a prestige strip featuring the 90+ industry leaders participating.

### Epic 2: Navigational Intelligence (Agenda)
*As a strategic leader, I want to manage a complex schedule with zero cognitive load.*
- **Story 2.1:** Switch between "Simple List" and "Venue View" to plan my physical movement.
- **Story 2.2:** Filter 45+ sessions by Track, Date, or Skill Level.
- **Story 2.3:** Bookmark sessions to a "My Schedule" list that persists across visits.

### Epic 3: Professional Vetting (Speakers)
*As an attendee, I want to learn about the speakers and their corporate credentials.*
- **Story 3.1:** Search 100+ speakers by name or company in under 100ms.
- **Story 3.2:** View a detailed bio modal that lists every session the speaker appears in.
- **Story 3.3:** Access a speaker's LinkedIn profile directly from their directory card.

### Epic 4: Ecosystem Sync (App)
*As an attendee, I want to bridge my digital planning with the physical networking app.*
- **Story 4.1:** View a persistent prompt to sync my schedule with the Konnect app.
- **Story 4.2:** Scan a QR code to instantly transfer my bookmarked sessions to my phone.
- **Story 4.3:** Use the "Tech Café" section to identify innovators to meet in person.
