# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## LeaderShip-X Summit Platform · lp_platform_02

### 1. Product Vision
"LeaderShip-X" is a digital sanctuary for industry leaders and strategic thinkers. It moves beyond standard "event pages" to create an authoritative, forward-looking environment that handles high-density session data and multi-venue logistics. The platform prioritizes "Navigational Intelligence" through multi-view agendas and establishes prestige via "Star Power" speaker directories, providing national tech forums with a digital stage that reflects their ecosystem dominance.

**Success Metric:** "Agenda Curation" (percentage of users who toggle between views or bookmark at least 5 sessions) exceeds 35%.

### 2. Personas
**David — The C-Level Executive (User)**
- Needs to maximize his time at the 3-day forum by targeting specific high-value keynotes and networking zones.
- Needs: A fast, authoritative way to view the "Venue-based" agenda and a simple way to find speakers from specific partner companies.
- Frustration: Cluttered sites that hide session times or make it difficult to see what is happening in a specific ballroom.

**Meera — The Ecosystem Partner (Audience)**
- Representing a sponsor or startup looking for networking and visibility.
- Needs: A clear display of "Thematic Pillars" and a "Tech Café" section that highlights innovation beyond the main stage.
- Frustration: Lack of thematic context and sites that treat all sessions with equal visual weight.

### 3. Core Features
- **Multi-View Agenda Hub:** A complex timeline UI allowing users to switch between "List," "Venue," and "Personalized" views in real-time.
- **"Star Power" Speaker Directory:** A responsive, authoritative grid featuring industry leaders with detailed bios and linked session itineraries.
- **Thematic Innovation Landing:** A hero and pillar system designed to communicate the year's "North Star" theme (e.g., Innovation X).
- **Tech Café Startup Zone:** A dedicated visual showcase for curated startups, featuring "Meet the Founder" cards and virtual booths.
- **App Sync & Networking Portal:** A component system that bridges the web experience with the "Konnect" mobile app via QR and deep-links.

### 4. User Journeys
1. **The Strategic Plan:** Visitor lands on home -> reviews "Thematic Pillars" -> enters the Agenda Hub -> filters by "Day 2" and "Ballroom A" -> adds 3 keynotes to "My Schedule."
2. **The Speaker Vetting:** User searches for a specific industry leader -> clicks their headshot -> reviews their bio and LinkedIn -> sees they are speaking on the "AI Policy" panel.
3. **The App Handoff:** Attendee clicks "Networking" -> sees the Konnect App promo -> scans the QR code -> instantly syncs their web-bookmarks to their phone.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No "Public Chat" on the website (Networking is handled within the Konnect app).
- No complex "Ticketing" in Phase 1 (Links to external payment portals preferred).
- No community "Photo Gallery" (Focus is on current event hype and logistics).

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** Enterprise Modern. Deep Navy, Orange accents, glassmorphism UI, bold Inter typography.
- **Performance:** Sub-1.2s LCP for the thematic hero visual.
- **Data:** Agenda must handle 50+ sessions across 5 venues with zero layout shift.
