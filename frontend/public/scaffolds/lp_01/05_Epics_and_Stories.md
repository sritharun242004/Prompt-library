# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · lp_platform_01

### Epic 1: Immersive Energy
*As a visitor, I want a homepage that immediately communicates the event's global scale.*
- **Story 1.1:** Experience a full-screen cinematic video hero upon entry.
- **Story 1.2:** See real-time "Ticket Alerts" via a bold, scrolling marquee.
- **Story 1.3:** Browse a high-impact strip of massive attendee and speaker statistics.

### Epic 2: Visual Discovery (Speakers)
*As an attendee, I want to learn about the speakers and find the ones relevant to my industry.*
- **Story 2.1:** Search 500+ speakers by keyword or industry in under 100ms.
- **Story 2.2:** View high-contrast B&W headshots that establish immediate authority.
- **Story 2.3:** Filter the directory by "Track" (e.g., SaaS Monster) to find relevant experts.

### Epic 3: Scarcity-Driven Conversion
*As a value-conscious founder, I want to lock in early-bird pricing before it expires.*
- **Story 3.1:** Compare ticket tiers in a clear grid with checked feature lists.
- **Story 3.2:** View a real-time countdown timer for the next price increase.
- **Story 3.3:** Complete a secure enrollment via Stripe in under 60 seconds.

### Epic 4: Narrative Discovery (Startups)
*As an investor, I want to find the high-growth startups featured in the Alpha program.*
- **Story 4.1:** Browse a grid of featured startups with previous funding success stats.
- **Story 4.2:** Filter startups by "Funding Stage" and "Industry Vertical."
- **Story 4.3:** Portal directly from a startup logo to their detailed founder profile.
