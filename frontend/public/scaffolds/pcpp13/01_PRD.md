# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## AnalystAusterity Business Portfolio · pcpp_platform_13

### 1. Product Vision
"AnalystAusterity" is the digital gold standard for independent business analysis. It moves beyond the transient "newsletter" to create a permanent, searchable strategic knowledge base. The platform prioritizes analytic rigor and deeply personalized delivery (RSS/Podcast), providing world-class analysts with a digital presence that commands authority and secures a "one-to-one" relationship with an elite audience of decision-makers.

**Success Metric:** Average "Knowledge Depth" (number of Concept Index pages viewed per session) exceeds 3.0, indicating successful educational engagement.

### 2. Personas
**Isabella — The Tech Executive (User)**
- Needs to understand market shifts like AI or Antitrust to guide her company's strategy.
- Needs: A fast, authoritative morning read that she can consume via her private RSS reader or podcast app.
- Frustration: Cluttered sites with generic "business news" that lack deep original synthesis.

**Arjun — The Institutional Investor (Audience)**
- Tracks value chains and company moats over decades.
- Needs: A robust "Concept Index" to Catch up on foundational frameworks and a seamless way to manage his "Plus" bundle billing.
- Frustration: Missing critical analysis because it wasn't delivered to his specific preference (e.g., email but he prefers RSS).

### 3. Core Features
- **Austere Reading Container:** A 680px centered text column with authoritative serif typography and zero sidebar distractions.
- **Thompson Diagram Engine:** A custom SVG renderer for hand-drawn strategic charts that illustrate complex market theories.
- **Passport Delivery System:** A proprietary member engine that generates and secures unique RSS and Podcast feed URLs for every subscriber.
- **Thematic Concept Index:** A structured archive of foundational strategic frameworks (e.g., Aggregation Theory) linked to evergreen essays.
- **Stratechery Plus Bundling:** An e-commerce layer that manages multi-stream subscriptions (Articles + Multiple Podcasts) under a single billing identity.

### 4. User Journeys
1. **The Morning Briefing:** Visitor lands on a Daily Update teaser -> reads the 300-word free excerpt -> clicks "Subscribe" -> completes 1-click checkout -> instantly unlocks full text.
2. **The Strategic Research:** User visits "Concepts" -> selects "The Smiling Curve" -> reads the foundational definition -> follows a thread of company earnings analysis using that curve.
3. **The Feed Setup:** New "Plus" member navigates to their dashboard -> generates a unique "Sharp Tech" podcast URL -> pastes it into Overcast for private listening.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No "Public Forum" (Discussion is reserved for private, authenticated member areas).
- No display advertising or "Affiliate Marketing" (100% subscriber-funded).
- No "Trending" or "Viral" social features (Focus is on long-term strategic value).

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** Analytic Austerity. Pure white, 0px sharp edges, high-contrast serif typography.
- **Privacy:** Mandatory support for secure, individualized delivery URLs to prevent bulk scraping.
- **Performance:** Sub-1s page loads; minimal client-side Javascript to ensure reading focus.
