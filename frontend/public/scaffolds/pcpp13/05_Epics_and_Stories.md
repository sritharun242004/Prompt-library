# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · pcpp_platform_13

### Epic 1: Strategic Insight (Reading)
*As a decision-maker, I want a digital space that respects my time and helps me think clearly.*
- **Story 1.1:** Read long-form analysis without eye strain or UI distractions.
- **Story 1.2:** Understand complex market shifts through simple, hand-drawn diagrams.
- **Story 1.3:** See foundational concepts defined clearly at the top of every analysis.

### Epic 2: One-to-One Delivery (Passport)
*As a premium subscriber, I want my analysis delivered to my preferred tools securely.*
- **Story 2.1:** Generate a unique, private RSS URL for my personal reader app.
- **Story 2.2:** Access a private podcast feed with high-bitrate audio for Daily Updates.
- **Story 2.3:** Update my delivery preferences (Email vs RSS) in a clean dashboard.

### Epic 3: Strategic Knowledge Base (Discovery)
*As a researcher, I want to find every analysis ever written about a specific company or framework.*
- **Story 3.1:** Browse the "Concept Index" to learn about frameworks like "Aggregation Theory."
- **Story 3.2:** Search the entire 10-year archive by Company name (e.g., Apple, Google).
- **Story 3.3:** Follow a "foundational thread" from a 2013 essay to a 2024 earnings update.

### Epic 4: Value Expansion (Plus)
*As a power user, I want to bundle all related analysis and podcasts into a single subscription.*
- **Story 4.1:** Upgrade from a single site to a "Plus" bundle in under 60 seconds.
- **Story 4.2:** Access exclusive content streams (e.g., Sharp Tech, Dithering) with a single login.
- **Story 4.3:** Manage my subscription and billing via a secure Stripe-powered portal.
