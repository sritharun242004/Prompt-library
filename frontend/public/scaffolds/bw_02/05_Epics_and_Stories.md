# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · bw_platform_02

### Epic 1: Cinematic Arrival
*As a visitor, I want a homepage that immediately communicates the brand's dramatic aura.*
- **Story 1.1:** Experience a massive vertical high-fashion portrait upon entry.
- **Story 1.2:** Navigate through "Thematic Collections" via evocative image-first links.
- **Story 1.3:** See a prestige strip featuring global flagship locations (NY, Mumbai).

### Epic 2: Thematic Discovery
*As a connoisseur, I want to explore the "Visual Poetry" of a specific collection.*
- **Story 2.1:** Portals into a collection story with a narrative, atmospheric introduction.
- **Story 2.2:** Scroll through a grid of vertical portraits with generous whitespace.
- **Story 2.3:** View technical detail shots of embroidery or gemstones with "Craft" labels.

### Epic 3: Prestigious Inquiry (The Barrier)
*As a high-net-worth client, I want a direct and exclusive way to inquire about couture.*
- **Story 3.1:** Select "Inquire" on a high-value item to initiate a human connection.
- **Story 3.2:** Provide my contact details and preferred boutique in a minimalist form.
- **Story 3.3:** Receive an automated, prestigious brand confirmation email.

### Epic 4: The Global Temple (Stores)
*As an international client, I want to find the nearest physical flagship store.*
- **Story 4.1:** Browse a list of global flagships with high-res interior photography.
- **Story 4.2:** Access store-specific WhatsApp links for instant luxury styling advice.
- **Story 4.3:** Understand the brand's "Art of Retail" philosophy through a dedicated section.
