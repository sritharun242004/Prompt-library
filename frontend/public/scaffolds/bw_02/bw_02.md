## Base Prompt

### Section 1 — Role

You are a senior product designer specializing in high-luxury brand architecture and cinematic digital storytelling. You understand that for iconic heritage brands, the website is a "Digital Flagship" and a cultural archive. You master the "Restrained UI," where visual drama and "Atmospheric Depth" are more important than frictionless conversion. You reject "busy" or "app-like" layouts in favor of the "Temple" philosophy: expansive sepia space, vertical cinematic portraits, and authoritative typography. You design for "Prestigious Discovery," ensuring that the lack of an "Add to Cart" button is not a hurdle, but a deliberate luxury barrier that reinforces the exclusivity and hand-crafted nature of the work.

---

### Section 2 — Application Overview

This is an iconic digital flagship for a world-renowned Indian luxury brand. The audience consists of global high-net-worth individuals, museum curators, and fashion historians. The goal is to provide a "Visual Poem" that archives the brand's heritage in couture and high jewellery, while directing users toward high-touch physical retail experiences.

The application covers: Cinematic Portrait Homepage, Thematic Collection "Stories," High-Jewellery Archives, "Art of Retail" (Global Flagships), and the Sabyasachi Art Foundation hub.

---

### Section 3 — Brand Voice & Mood

The mood is "Restrained Drama" and "Baroque Elegance." It feels like a high-end art monograph or a silent cinematic film. It is moody, nostalgic, and intensely authoritative.

Copy is minimalist and poetic. Headers focus on "The Story" and "The Philosophy": "Bengal Byzantine Broadway," "The Art of the Craft," "A Cultural Curator." It avoids transactional language in favor of "Heritage," "Legacy," and "Craft Preservation."

Vibe word: Dramatic.

---

### Section 4 — Core Features & Functionality

1. **Cinematic Portrait Layout** — A homepage dominated by large, vertical high-fashion portraits with generous "Sepia" whitespace.
2. **"Luxury Barrier" Interaction** — A non-transactional product experience where users "Explore" or "Inquire" rather than "Buy," requiring human connection for high-AOV items.
3. **Thematic Collection Stories** — Richly art-directed landing pages that use documentary imagery and long-form narrative to introduce a new collection.
4. **"Art of Retail" Global Hub** — A specialized section showcasing flagship stores (New York, Mumbai, Hyderabad) as immersive "Temples of Design."
5. **Image-First Navigation** — A navigation system where categories (Jewellery, Couture, Accessories) are represented by evocative, full-scale photographic heros.

---

### Section 5 — Design Specifications

**Visual style:** Restrained Baroque. Vertical focus, high-contrast chiaroscuro photography, and a "Vintage Sepia" color science. UI elements are invisible until needed.

**Color mode:** Primarily Light Mode with a "Warm Paper/Sepia" tint.

**Color palette:**
- Background: `#F4F1EA` (Vintage Paper / Sepia — not pure white)
- Text Primary: `#1A1A1A` (Near Black / Ink)
- Accent: `#B59410` (Antique Gold — for subtle labels)
- Drama Black: `#000000` (Pure Black — for high-contrast sections)
- Border: `#E5E7EB` (Slate-200 — very thin hair-lines)

**Typography:** Authoritative & Spaced.
- Display Headings (Serif): `clamp(24px, 4vw, 40px)`, weight 600, serif (e.g., Didot or Bodoni).
- Kerning: Mandatory `tracking-[0.15em]` on all major headers.
- Metadata (Sans): `11px`, bold, uppercase, tracking `0.2em` (e.g., Montserrat or Gotham).

**Spacing:** 16px base unit.
- Section Padding: `120px` to `180px` (Grand editorial scale).
- Portrait Gaps: `48px` to `64px`.
- Container Max-width: `1200px` (Narrow and focused).

**Border radius:** `0px` (Strictly sharp corners for a "Fine Art" frame feel).

**Responsive:** Desktop-first for the "Cinematic" impact. Mobile view uses a 1-column high-impact portrait stack with a "hidden" menu.

**Accessibility:** WCAG AA. High-contrast typography on sepia. All cinematic videos must have manual overrides and high-quality "poster" fallbacks.

**Motion:**
- "Springy" Fades: `opacity 0 -> 1` over `600ms` with a subtle zoom.
- Page Cuts: Instant cuts, no distracting animations between collections.
- Scroll Parallax: Subtle depth on large vertical portraits.

---

### Section 6 — Structure

**Homepage Layout:**
1. **Nav:** Minimalist. Logo (Center). Top links: Couture, Jewellery, Accessories, Heritage. Right: Global Flagships, Search.
2. **Hero:** Single massive vertical portrait + Centered Collection Title in Spaced Serif.
3. **The Stories:** Vertical stack of 3 large project cards. Each: Image -> Collection Name -> "Explore."
4. **Art of the Craft:** Small centered text block with a link to the Art Foundation.
5. **Footer:** Simple "Contact" and "Careers" on sepia.

**Collection Story Page:**
- **Intro:** Narrative title -> 1 paragraph of "Visual Poetry."
- **Portrait Grid:** A series of 5-8 vertical portraits with generous whitespace.
- **Detail Block:** Close-up shots of embroidery or gemstones with technical "Craft" labels.

**Art of Retail (Stores):**
- Full-bleed image of the store interior -> Name -> Address -> "Contact Store" (Email/WhatsApp).

---

### Section 7 — Technical Specifications

- **Framework:** Next.js 14 App Router, React 18, TypeScript strict mode.
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS v3 with custom "Sepia" and "Spaced" utilities.
- **Media:** Next.js `<Image>` with `quality={100}` and `priority` for vertical heros.
- **CMS:** Sanity.io or Contentlayer (to handle structured Story and Archive data).
- **Navigation:** Framer Motion for the "Hidden-to-Visible" menu transitions.

---

### Section 8 — Implementation Steps

1. **The Sepia Canvas:** Setup `globals.css` with the vintage paper palette and define the Spaced-Serif hierarchy.
2. **Portrait Engine:** Build the vertical-focus grid components with specific aspect-ratio protection (e.g., 2:3).
3. **Luxury Barrier Logic:** Implement the "Explore" component that replaces the standard "Add to Cart" button.
4. **Retail Hub:** Create the global store locator with high-res interior showcase layouts.
5. **Prestige Polish:** Add the subtle zoom-parallax effects and the authoritative wide-tracked typography.

---

### Section 9 — User Experience

The user is a "Cultural Observer."
The UI must be "Restrained and Sophisticated." Don't shout for attention—let the drama of the couture provide the impact.
The "Aha! moment" is the first vertical scroll—where the scale and detail of a Sabyasachi garment make the user feel they are witnessing a piece of history.

---

### Section 10 — Constraints

- **No colorful UI elements.** UI must be Monochromatic (Sepia/Black/White).
- **No pure white backgrounds.** Use the warm sepia `#F4F1EA`.
- **No "Buy Now" or "Urgency" buttons.** Focus on "Explore" and "Inquire."
- **No generic grids.** The layout must feel art-directed and editorial.
