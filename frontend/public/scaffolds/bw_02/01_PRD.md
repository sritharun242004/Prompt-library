# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## DramaticLuxury Brand Flagship · bw_platform_01

### 1. Product Vision
"DramaticLuxury" is a digital hallmark for world-class heritage houses. It moves beyond the "frictionless" norms of e-commerce to create a "Digital Temple" where brand prestige and cultural legacy are documented. The platform prioritizes atmospheric depth and visual poetry, providing iconic brands with a digital presence that matches the aura and exclusivity of their physical flagships.

**Success Metric:** 15% of users initiate a "Contact Boutique" inquiry after exploring a High Jewellery or Couture collection story.

### 2. Personas
**Isabella — The Global Collector (Client)**
- Based in London or Dubai and seeks one-of-a-kind couture masterpieces.
- Needs: Immediate proof of "Cultural Authority" and a direct, prestigious way to inquire about bespoke items.
- Frustration: Sites that look "cheap" or transactional with "Add to Cart" buttons for $10,000+ items.

**Vikram — The Art Historian (Audience)**
- Researches the preservation of traditional Indian crafts.
- Needs: In-depth narrative content on craft techniques and historical archives of the brand's work.
- Frustration: Lack of narrative depth and sites that prioritize "New Arrivals" over "Legacy."

### 3. Core Features
- **Cinematic Portrait Homepage:** A high-impact opening that uses large vertical portraits to establish the brand's atmospheric tone.
- **Luxury Barrier Interaction:** A deliberate non-transactional product experience where high-value items are "Explored" via inquiry rather than simple purchase.
- **Thematic Collection Stories:** Deeply art-directed landing pages that weave together documentary imagery, film, and poetic narrative.
- **"Art of Retail" Locator:** A global hub showcasing flagship stores as immersive destinations with integrated WhatsApp/Email inquiry triggers.
- **Craft Preservation Hub:** A dedicated section for the brand's Art Foundation, documenting the manual techniques and artisans behind the work.

### 4. User Journeys
1. **The Aspirational Scroll:** Visitor lands on home -> experiences the full-screen cinematic portrait -> scrolls to "Jewellery" -> portals into a thematic story (e.g., *Bengal Byzantine*) -> views the high-res gallery.
2. **The Global Discovery:** User navigates to "Stores" -> views the New York flagship interior -> reads about the "Art of Retail" -> clicks "Contact Store" to book a private viewing.
3. **The Heritage Deep-Dive:** Student visits the "Heritage" section -> reads about the Sabyasachi Art Foundation -> views the documentary on hand-painting *Pichhwai*.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No standard "Checkout" or "Payment Gateway" for high-end couture (Focus is on lead-gen for stores).
- No community "Likes" or "Comments" (Maintain the silent gallery aura).
- No animated page transitions (Maintain the "physical book" cut-edit feel).

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** Restrained Baroque. Vintage Sepia (#F4F1EA), pure black, sharp 0px corners, wide-tracked serif typography.
- **Performance:** Mandatory sub-2s load for massive vertical heros (ISR and Image Optimization).
- **Data:** Story content must be highly structured in the CMS to allow for "Chapter" based navigation.
