# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · lp_platform_07

### Epic 1: Immediate Authority
*As a visitor, I want a homepage that immediately establishes the creator's scale and influence.*
- **Story 1.1:** View a high-impact hero section with professional portraiture and a clear mission statement.
- **Story 1.2:** See a horizontal strip of massive social-proof statistics (e.g., 16M+ Followers).
- **Story 1.3:** Experience a "Breathable" but bold layout that signals technical and educational mastery.

### Epic 2: Proven Authenticity (Failure Resume)
*As an aspirant, I want to see the real journey behind the success to verify the creator's credibility.*
- **Story 2.1:** Browse a vertical timeline of career rejections and startup failures.
- **Story 2.2:** Read the "Lesson Learned" for every failure to understand the wisdom being sold.
- **Story 2.3:** Feel an emotional connection to the brand through radical, documented transparency.

### Epic 3: Educational Discovery (Courses)
*As a student, I want to find a course that fits my budget and solves my specific problem.*
- **Story 3.1:** Browse a scannable grid of WebVeda courses with upfront pricing (INR).
- **Story 3.2:** View high-contrast "Outcome Chips" (e.g., Lifetime Access) as primary value signals.
- **Story 3.3:** Enroll in a course in under 60 seconds via a direct-checkout portal.

### Epic 4: Narrative Continuity (Newsletter)
*As a fan, I want to stay connected to the creator's thoughts and curated insights.*
- **Story 4.1:** Subscribe to the "Warikoo Wanderings" newsletter via a high-conversion 1-field hero.
- **Story 4.2:** Access a searchable archive of previous editions to vet the content quality.
- **Story 4.3:** Download a "Free Resource" (e.g., Failure Resume Template) as a reward for joining the list.
