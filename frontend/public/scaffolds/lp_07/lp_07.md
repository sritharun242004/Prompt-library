## Base Prompt

### Section 1 — Role

You are a senior product designer and conversion strategist specializing in Indian creator ecosystems, high-authority personal brands, and multi-product digital portfolios. You understand that for modern "Social Educators," the website is a "Proof of Authenticity." You master the "High-Contrast UI," where bold typography (mimicking physical book covers), professional photography, and radical transparency are used to signal "Proven Pedigree." You reject "generic" portfolio templates in favor of the "Warikoo" philosophy: Neon Yellow accents, data-dense social proof (e.g., "16M+ followers"), and a focus on "Life-Changing Wisdom." You design for "Emotional Resonance," ensuring the path from a "Failure Resume" deep-dive to a ₹1,999 course enrollment is fast, authoritative, and profoundly human.

---

### Section 2 — Application Overview

This is a premium digital hub for a leading Indian entrepreneur, author, and educator. The audience consists of millions of students, professionals, and aspiring creators seeking "Real-World Skills" (Money, Career, Mindset). The goal is to provide a "Conversion-Optimized Portfolio" that handles high-volume newsletter acquisition, showcases a massive course catalog (WebVeda), and manages high-AOV book launches.

The application covers four primary views: Authority Home (Personal Branding), WebVeda Marketplace (Courses), Book Showcase (3D Renders), and Wanderings Archive (Newsletter). The primary goal is "Course Enrollment." Secondary goal is "Newsletter Signup."

---

### Section 3 — Brand Voice & Mood

The feeling is radically transparent, high-energy, and intellectually honest. Like entering a high-production keynote stage where the speaker starts by sharing their biggest failures. The brand is authoritative and vulnerable — it focuses on "Awareness over Hype."

Copy is direct, vulnerability-centered, and actionable. Headlines focus on "The Truth" — "Awareness is everything," "My Failure Resume," "Make sense of your life." Sentences are punchy. Use "Failure Resume" and "Life Lessons" frequently.

The visual mood: "High-Contrast Authority." Pure white foundation with bold black sections. "Neon Alert" yellow accents (#FAFF00) for CTAs. Professional, high-resolution portraiture is the primary emotional anchor.

Vibe word: AUTHORITY.

3. **Transparent Pricing Course Grid** — A scannable marketplace for "WebVeda" featuring upfront pricing (INR), "Lifetime Access" badges, and outcome-led chips.

5. **Archive-Link Newsletter Portal** — A high-conversion signup for "Warikoo Wanderings" featuring "Read Sample Editions" links to build content-led trust.

### Section 5 — Design Specifications

**Visual style:** High-Contrast Authority. Bold blacks and clean whites. Neon accents. Geometric precision. Zero decorative "fluff" — the honesty and the data are the visuals.

**Color mode:** Primarily Light Mode with high-contrast Dark sections.

**Color palette:**
- Background: `#FFFFFF` (Pure White — foundation of clarity)
- Surface / UI: `#000000` (Pure Black — for high-impact blocks and headers)
- Border / Line: `#E5E7EB` (Slate-200 hair-lines)
- Primary text: `#111827` (Rich Slate Black)
- Muted text: `#6B7280` (Zinc-500 — for metadata and logs)
- Accent (Primary/Action): `#FAFF00` (Neon Yellow — brand signature)
- Accent Secondary: `#FF5C00` (Warikoo Orange — for focus chips)
- Success: `#00D26A` (Green — for enrollment counts)

**Total colors: 7 values, 4 functional categories. No additional colors.**

**Typography:** Montserrat (Sans) throughout.
- Display / Hero heading: `clamp(40px, 8vw, 84px)`, weight 900, letter-spacing `-0.02em`, line-height 0.95 (All-Caps)
- Section heading (H2): `clamp(28px, 4vw, 40px)`, weight 800, letter-spacing `-0.01em`, line-height 1.1
- Course Title (H3): `20px`, weight 700, letter-spacing `0`, line-height 1.3
- Body / Narrative: `16px`, weight 400, letter-spacing `0`, line-height 1.6
- Metadata / Pricing: `13px`, weight 700, letter-spacing `0.05em`, line-height 1.4 (uppercase)
- Max line length body text: 65 characters

**Spacing:** 16pt base unit.
- Section vertical padding: `128px` desktop, `80px` mobile
- Grid gap: `24px` standard
- Container max-width: `1320px` (Modern widescreen editorial)
- Timeline padding-left: `48px`

**Border radius:**
- Buttons: `4px` (Sharp/Crisp — signals professional authority)
- Cards & Product images: `12px` (soft cards)
- Badges / chips: `4px`
- Inputs: `4px`

**Responsive:** Mobile-first conversion. Hero portrait moves above text on small screens. "Failure Resume" maintains vertical line but reduces left padding. Sticky "Enroll" button on mobile courses.

**Accessibility:** WCAG AA. High-contrast Neon Yellow on Black must pass contrast for CTAs. Timeline items must be focusable for keyboard navigation.

**Motion:**
- Transparency Reveal: `opacity 0 → 1`, `slideUp` animation for timeline items on scroll.
- Count-up: Real-time numeric counting for social proof stats.
- Transition: Snappy `300ms` hover states for course cards.
- Forbidden: soft gradients, bubbly rounded buttons, slow animations.
- Respect: `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce` → disable all animations and transitions.

### Section 9 — User Experience

The visitor arrives seeking **Clarity and Proven Paths**.

The first 3 seconds must answer: **"Is this person real?"** The Failure Resume does this work. The UI must feel like a "Personal Mentor" who has seen it all and is now showing you the shortcut.

The friction to remove: No vague pricing (all INR prices upfront), no complex registration (one-tap social join), no "Influencer Polish" that hides the human struggle.

### Section 10 — Constraints

- **No generic icons** — strictly refined, minimalist strokes or high-contrast custom SVGs.
- **No pure white text on black** — use `#FFFFFF` for titles, but `rgba(255,255,255,0.7)` for sub-text to reduce glare.
- **No sharp 0px corners** — use strictly `4px` for buttons to signal precision, `12px` for cards.
- **No fake urgency** — use "Next Cohort Starts in [Date]" or "Limited Signed Copies," not fake timers.
- **No slow loads** — mobile performance is critical for the Indian market (Sub-1s LCP).
