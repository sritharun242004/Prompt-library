# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## WarikooStyle Creator Hub · lp_platform_07

### 1. Product Vision
"WarikooStyle" is a digital hallmark for authentic leadership. It moves beyond standard "personal portfolios" to create a high-performance "Trust Engine" where the creator's journey—including failures—is the primary value anchor. The platform prioritizes "Emotional Scannability" through bold typography and organizes courses, books, and newsletters into a cohesive conversion funnel, providing Indian educators with a digital stage that signals world-class pedigree, radical honesty, and actionable wisdom.

**Success Metric:** "Trust-to-Conversion" rate (percentage of users who interact with the "Failure Resume" and proceed to view a course or book) exceeds 20%.

### 2. Personas
**Siddharth — The Ambitious Aspirant (User)**
- A college student or young professional looking for career and money guidance.
- Needs: Immediate proof of the creator's authority (follower counts), relatable success/failure stories, and affordable "entry-level" courses.
- Frustration: Overly polished influencers who lack transparency or sites that hide the actual price of courses.

**The Team — The Multi-Product Manager (Client)**
- Managing a complex business with book launches, cohort-based courses, and weekly newsletters.
- Needs: A unified CMS that handles "Failure Logs," "Course Pricing," and "Retailer Affiliate Links" in one place.
- Frustration: Siloed platforms that make it hard to link a "Newsletter Insight" to a "Paid Course."

### 3. Core Features
- **Narrative "Failure Resume" Engine:** A vertical timeline component that builds emotional resonance by documenting rejections and life lessons.
- **Book-Inspired Typography Suite:** A high-contrast design system derived from physical book cover aesthetics for digital authority.
- **WebVeda Course Marketplace:** A transparently priced grid featuring outcome-led chips ("Lifetime Access," "Bestseller") and 1-click enroll CTAs.
- **Massive Social-Proof Strips:** High-impact horizontal bars showcasing "16M+ Followers," "NYT Bestseller," and "As Seen On" logos.
- **Wanderings Newsletter Hub:** A high-conversion sign-up block featuring "Sample Editions" and social proof of the 75,000+ subscriber count.

### 4. User Journeys
1. **The Authenticity Audit:** Visitor lands on hub -> scrolls to the "Failure Resume" -> reads about the 2013 startup failure -> feels deep trust -> clicks "Courses."
2. **The Digital Skill-Up:** User filters the WebVeda grid by "Money" -> sees "Personal Finance Masterclass" -> reviews the ₹1,999 price -> enrolls instantly.
3. **The Book Launch:** User clicks "Books" -> views a 3D render of *Do Epic Shit* -> clicks "Buy on Amazon" -> receives the book and returns to download the free "Digital Bonus" PDF.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No "Public Community Forum" (Keep the focus on structured curriculum and content).
- No "Dark Mode" as default (Maintain the bright, high-signal "Academic Clarity" feel).
- No "Vague Pricing" (All products must show INR/USD price upfront).

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** High-Contrast Authority. Pure white, 4px sharp corners, bold Montserrat typography.
- **Performance:** Sub-800ms page load for mobile users (optimized for 4G/5G Indian networks).
- **Compliance:** Support for Indian UPI/Netbanking payment indicators in the UI.
