# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · pcpp_platform_15

### Epic 1: High-Conversion Entry
*As a visitor, I want a landing page that immediately communicates the value of the publication.*
- **Story 1.1:** Subscribe to the free tier with a single email entry on the welcome page.
- **Story 1.2:** See proof of quality through "Bestseller" badges and high-impact pitch text.
- **Story 1.3:** Experience a clean, "Writer's Desk" aesthetic that establishes trust.

### Epic 2: Content Discovery
*As a reader, I want an organized archive that is easy to navigate and search.*
- **Story 2.1:** Browse the latest posts in a visual "Magazine" grid or a dense "Newspaper" list.
- **Story 2.2:** Filter the publication by specific "Sections" or "Tags" (e.g., Tech, Policy).
- **Story 2.3:** Search years of archived reporting instantly via a clean search interface.

### Epic 3: Premium Access (Paywall)
*As an independent writer, I want a frictionless way to monetize my high-value work.*
- **Story 3.1:** Read a high-quality free teaser before encountering a "Locked" post.
- **Story 3.2:** Upgrade to a paid annual plan in under 60 seconds within the post context.
- **Story 3.3:** Access exclusive "Member-only" chat threads and comment sections.

### Epic 4: Audience Ownership
*As a creator, I want to manage my subscriber list and community directly.*
- **Story 4.1:** Post short-form "Notes" to update my community between long essays.
- **Story 4.2:** Export my entire email list to a CSV at any time for data independence.
- **Story 4.3:** Send "Gift Links" for individual paid posts to drive viral discovery.
