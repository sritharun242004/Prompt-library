# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · pcpp_platform_21

### Epic 1: Immersive Energy
*As a visitor, I want a homepage that immediately communicates the event's high production value.*
- **Story 1.1:** Experience a full-screen cinematic video hero upon entry.
- **Story 1.2:** See real-time "Ticket Alerts" via a bold, scrolling marquee.
- **Story 1.3:** Browse a high-impact grid of featured speakers with smooth hover reveals.

### Epic 2: Visual Discovery (Speakers)
*As an attendee, I want to learn about the speakers and find the ones relevant to my role.*
- **Story 2.1:** Browse 100+ speakers categorized by their role or company.
- **Story 2.2:** View a B&W headshot that reveals the person's professional aura on hover.
- **Story 2.3:** Read a detailed bio and view linked sessions for a specific speaker.

### Epic 3: Personalized Schedule
*As a designer, I want to build a custom 3-day learning path across multiple tracks.*
- **Story 3.1:** Filter the 3-day schedule by "Design," "Dev," or "AI" tracks.
- **Story 3.2:** View all sessions in my local time zone automatically.
- **Story 3.3:** Add sessions to my personal "My Config" list and see them in a saved view.

### Epic 4: Event Logistics
*As a traveler, I want a simple way to find venue and badge information.*
- **Story 4.1:** Access a high-res map of the Moscone Center and "Config Commons."
- **Story 4.2:** Find details on local "Watch Parties" near my current location.
- **Story 4.3:** Use a 1-page "Registration" portal to choose between in-person and virtual tickets.
