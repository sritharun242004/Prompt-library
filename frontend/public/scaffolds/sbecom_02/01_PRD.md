# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## ChaiRitual Beverage Subscription · sbecom_platform_02

### 1. Product Vision
"ChaiRitual" is a "Home Cafe" platform that brings the authentic Indian tea experience to the daily pantry. It moves beyond one-off beverage sales to a model where tea is a recurring "Daily Fix." The platform prioritizes high-frequency subscriptions (Instant Chai, Loose Leaf) and high-AOV bundles (Chai + Snacks) through a warm, trustworthy, and culturally resonant digital storefront.

**Success Metric:** 50% of first-time buyers convert to a "Subscribe & Save" plan for their second order.

### 2. Personas
**Kavita — The WFH Professional (Subscriber)**
- Drinks 2-3 cups of tea daily while working.
- Needs: A reliable monthly delivery of instant chai sachets, "Quick Add" for snacks, and a way to easily pause the subscription when traveling.
- Frustration: Running out of tea unexpectedly and complex checkout processes for single items.

**Amit — The Traditional Head of Household (Subscriber)**
- Manages the pantry for a family of six.
- Needs: Bulk loose-leaf tea boxes, consistent quality, and a "Secure Checkout" that supports UPI and recurring mandates.
- Frustration: Inconsistent flavor profiles and hidden shipping charges on bulk orders.

### 3. Core Features
- **"Home Cafe" Marketplace:** A lifestyle-centric homepage featuring bestsellers and ritual-based collections.
- **Subscribe & Save Engine:** Automated 15% discount and recurring delivery scheduling for pantry staples.
- **Intelligent Shopping Bag:** A cart that incentivizes higher spending via "Free Gift" progress bars and "Gift Note" options.
- **Experience Box Builder:** A custom UI for building curated "Chai Time" bundles (Tea + Biscuits + Snacks).
- **"My Rituals" Dashboard:** A subscriber portal for managing recurring frequencies, swapping flavors, and skipping months.

### 4. User Journeys
1. **The Monthly Fix:** User selects "Instant Chai Time Combo" -> toggles "Subscribe & Save" -> selects "Monthly" -> completes checkout via UPI mandate.
2. **The Gifting Path:** User adds a "Cookie Monster Collection" to the bag -> adds a personalized jute bag (unlocked via progress bar) -> adds a gift note -> ships to a friend.
3. **The Swap:** Existing subscriber logs in -> goes to "My Rituals" -> swaps "Assam Masala" for "Ginger Instant" for the next delivery.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No cold beverage / RTD (Ready-to-Drink) logistics for this phase.
- No B2B / Office machine management (Focus is strictly on the "Home Cafe").
- No physical tea-room booking (Digital product focus).

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** Modern Indian. Earthy tones, 4px-12px rounding, focus on "Aromatic" visual cues.
- **Market:** Primary focus on the Indian domestic market (Razorpay/UPI priority).
- **Speed:** Sub-1.5s LCP on mobile 4G networks.
