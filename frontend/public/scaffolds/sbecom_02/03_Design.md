# 03 — Design System
## Modern Indian Specifications · sbecom_platform_02

### 1. The Home Cafe Manifesto
1. **Authenticity over Tech.** Use textures (paper, wood) and warm tones (#FFFDF6) to ground the digital experience.
2. **Green is Fresh.** Deep Green (#004B23) signals premium tea quality. Use it for trust markers and buttons.
3. **Ritual over Retail.** Category names should reflect the use-case (e.g., "Morning Kickstart") rather than the SKU type.
4. **Mobile First.** Floating conversion bars and carousel-centric product browsing for the Indian mobile user.

### 2. Color Palette
- **Primary:** `#004B23` (Deep Green) - All primary CTAs.
- **Secondary:** `#D4A373` (Terracotta) - Snack tags and warm sections.
- **Background:** `#FFFDF6` (Warm Paper) - Overall page background.
- **Surface:** `#FFFFFF` (White) - Cards and Modals.
- **Highlight:** `#FFD338` (Vivid Yellow) - Used ONLY for gifting progress and "New" badges.

### 3. Typography
- **Headlines:** Plus Jakarta Sans (Weight 800) - `font-extrabold`, `tracking-tight`.
- **Body:** Inter (Weight 400) - `font-normal`, `leading-relaxed`.
- **Pricing:** Bold Sans-serif with smaller Currency Symbol.

### 4. Component Specs
**The "Ritual Block"**
- Aspect Ratio: `16:9` or `1:1`.
- Overlay: Gradient scrim (bottom-to-top) with Title and "Shop Now" text link.
- Radius: `0px` for lifestyle blocks; `12px` for snack cards.

**The "Gift Tracker" (Cart)**
- Height: `8px` progress bar.
- Color: `#FFD338` fill on `#E5E7EB` track.
- Icon: Moving "Gift Box" icon on the progress tip.
