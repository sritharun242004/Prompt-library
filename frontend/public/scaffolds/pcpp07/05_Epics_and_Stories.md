# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · pcpp_platform_07

### Epic 1: Sky-Open Browsing
*As a visitor, I want a homepage that feels as expansive and grand as a destination landscape.*
- **Story 1.1:** Experience a slow-fade "Aero" hero slideshow upon entry.
- **Story 1.2:** Browse location-based story tiles featuring grand wide-angle photography.
- **Story 1.3:** See a prestigious Fujifilm Ambassador badge to establish technical authority.

### Epic 2: The Chronological Day
*As a bride-to-be, I want to see the sequence of a 3-day wedding from morning to night.*
- **Story 2.1:** Scroll through a story organized by "Time of Day" markers (e.g., 08:00 AM).
- **Story 2.2:** Enjoy high-resolution landscape shots that show the venue's scale.
- **Story 2.3:** Read a brief narrative excerpt that grounds the visual sequence in a specific moment.

### Epic 3: Technical Trust (The Pilot)
*As a high-end planner, I want to understand the photographer's unique technical background.*
- **Story 3.1:** Read about the "Pilot's Perspective" on composition and precision.
- **Story 3.2:** View a technical list of gear and presets used for high-performance documentary work.
- **Story 3.3:** Understand the brand's commitment to "Technical Honesty" and "Red Leaf" color science.

### Epic 4: Global Inquiry
*As an international client, I want a simple, high-trust way to start a remote collaboration.*
- **Story 4.1:** Access a clean "Where are we going?" form that respects my time.
- **Story 4.2:** Provide destination and date details in a high-contrast, minimalist UI.
- **Story 4.3:** Receive an instant, professional confirmation of my inquiry.
