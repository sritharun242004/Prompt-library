# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## CreatorEcosystem Hub · lp_platform_06

### 1. Product Vision
"CreatorEcosystem" is a digital hallmark for the modern creator-as-business. It moves beyond standard "personal sites" to create a cohesive multi-venture flagship where courses, books, and software tools are organized into "Transformational Pathways." The platform prioritizes intent-based discovery and leverages "Feel-Good" social proof to drive high-volume growth, providing world-class experts with a digital stage that signals approachability, technical mastery, and sustainable success.

**Success Metric:** "Pathway Selection" (percentage of users who click an intent block in the Pathways Grid and proceed to a product page) exceeds 25%.

### 2. Personas
**Leo — The Ambitious Learner (User)**
- A fan of Ali's content who wants to take his productivity or YouTube journey seriously.
- Needs: A clear starting point based on his specific intent, proof of course outcomes (e.g., "Part-Time YouTuber"), and a simple way to sign up for free resources.
- Frustration: Cluttered sites that sell "hustle" rather than structured, evidence-based systems.

**The Team — The Operations Lead (Client)**
- Managing a complex business with multiple launches (Book, Cohorts, Software).
- Needs: A modular CMS that allows for easy scaling of new ventures and a fast archive for 1,000+ articles and videos.
- Frustration: Inflexible templates that don't handle the relationship between a "Book Note" and a "Course Module."

### 3. Core Features
- **Intent-Based Pathways Grid:** A high-impact navigation component that routes users based on their goal (e.g., "Start Business," "Study Better").
- **Outcome-Labeled Product Catalog:** A scannable grid for the "Academy" and software tools, featuring high-contrast badges like "Free Crash Course" or "Masterclass."
- **3D Book Launch Showcase:** A specialized landing component for bestsellers featuring interactive renders, celebrity blurbs, and "Bonus Unlock" gates.
- **Searchable Wisdom Archive:** A data-dense directory of articles, book notes, and videos with high-speed Algolia filtering.
- **High-Social-Proof Acquisition Heros:** Conversion blocks for the "LifeNotes" newsletter featuring real-time subscriber counts (400k+) and "Hey Friend" intro headers.

### 4. User Journeys
1. **The Intent Routing:** Visitor lands on hub -> clicks "Start a Business" in the Pathways Grid -> enters the business-specific discovery page -> reviews the "Part-Time YouTuber" course card.
2. **The Wisdom Discovery:** User searches "Atomic Habits" -> sees a high-density book note with "Evidence-Based" highlights -> signs up for the newsletter to get more notes.
3. **The Bonus Unlock:** Book buyer clicks "Unlock Bonuses" -> enters their order ID -> gains access to a gated "Bonus Hub" with exclusive templates.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No "Dark Mode" (Focus is on the bright, airy productivity aesthetic).
- No "High-Pressure" scarcity (No fake countdowns; use "Hey Friend" transparency).
- No "Public Community Feed" (Focus is on the structured curriculum and content).

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** Friendly Tech Modernism. Pure white, rounded-2xl corners, Ali Blue accents.
- **Performance:** Sub-1s page load for content archives; sub-100ms Algolia search response.
- **Tone:** Must use "Hey Friend 👋" and "Evidence-Based" terminology throughout the UI.
