# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · lp_platform_06

### Epic 1: Transformational Pathways
*As a visitor, I want a homepage that guides me based on my specific career or life goal.*
- **Story 1.1:** Choose between 4 intent-based blocks (YouTube, Productivity, Business, Study).
- **Story 1.2:** See a personal "Hey Friend" intro that establishes immediate warmth and trust.
- **Story 1.3:** Experience a "Breathable" layout that makes me feel calm and focused on my growth.

### Epic 2: Multi-Venture Discovery
*As a fan, I want to see everything the brand offers without feeling overwhelmed.*
- **Story 2.1:** Browse a categorized grid of Academy courses, books, and software tools.
- **Story 2.2:** See high-contrast "Outcome Labels" that clarify what I will achieve (e.g., Free vs Masterclass).
- **Story 2.3:** Watch a high-performance video trailer for the "Academy" directly on the card.

### Epic 3: Evidence-Based Wisdom
*As a researcher, I want to find specific book notes and articles from a massive archive.*
- **Story 3.1:** Search 1,000+ content items by keyword or book title in under 100ms.
- **Story 3.2:** Filter the archive by "Content Type" (e.g., Book Note) or "Industry" (e.g., Tech).
- **Story 3.3:** Read high-density metadata (reading time, source) as proof of content quality.

### Epic 4: Community Growth (Acquisition)
*As a seeker, I want to join the inner-circle and unlock free resources.*
- **Story 4.1:** Subscribe to "LifeNotes" via a frictionless 1-field hero with 400k+ social proof.
- **Story 4.2:** Unlock "Book Bonuses" by entering my order ID into an elegant verification modal.
- **Story 4.3:** Receive an intent-based "Bonus PDF" (e.g., Productivity Framework) instantly.
