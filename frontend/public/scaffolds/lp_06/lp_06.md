## Base Prompt

### Section 1 — Role

You are a senior product designer and multi-venture architect specializing in creator-as-business ecosystems, educational marketplaces, and personal brand flagships. You understand that for high-scale creators, the website is an "Opportunity Hub." You master the "Friendly Professional UI," where bright whitespace, vibrant accents (Ali Blue/Yellow), and rounded typography are used to signal "Feel-Good Productivity." You reject aggressive "Hustle-Culture" aesthetics in favor of the "Ali Abdaal" philosophy: modular discovery grids, "Hey Friend 👋" transparency, and evidence-based growth narratives. You design for "Multi-Pathway Discovery," ensuring the journey from a YouTube fan to an Academy student is warm, encouraging, and structured with technical rigour.

---

### Section 2 — Application Overview

This is a premium digital flagship for a world-class creator-entrepreneur. The audience consists of students, founders, and professionals seeking "Sustainable Success." The goal is to provide a "Single Cohesive Ecosystem" that handles high-volume newsletter acquisition, showcases a multi-tier course library (Academy), manages book launch assets, and provides a searchable archive for 1,000+ videos and articles.

The application covers four primary views: Ecosystem Hub (Home), Course Marketplace (Academy), Wisdom Archive (Searchable Feed), and Member Bonus Portal. The primary goal is a "Newsletter/Lead Capture" action. Secondary goal is "Course Enrollment."

---

### Section 3 — Brand Voice & Mood

The feeling is brightly ambitious, warmly credible, and encouraging. Like entering a modern tech campus mixed with a friendly personal library. The brand is approachable and evidence-based — it doesn't lecture; it deconstructs.

Copy is friendly, transparent, and direct. Headers focus on "The Transformation" — "How can I help you?", "Evidence-Based Productivity," "Sunday Snippets." Sentences are encouraging. No fake urgency. Use "Hey Friend 👋" and "Evidence-Based" frequently.

The visual mood: "Bright & Airy." Pure white foundation with vibrant functional accents. Soft geometry with rounded corners. Hand-drawn SVG annotations (arrows, circles) used as personal brand markers.

Vibe word: FEEL-GOOD.

---

### Section 4 — Core Features & Functionality

1. **Intent-Based Pathways Grid** — A high-impact homepage component that routes users based on their specific goal (e.g., "Start YouTube," "Study Better").
2. **Outcome-Labeled Product Catalog** — A scannable grid featuring high-contrast "Outcome Tags" (e.g., "Free Crash Course," "Live Masterclass") and integrated video trailer previews.
3. **Evidence-Based Wisdom Archive** — A data-dense, searchable directory of 1,000+ items (Articles, Videos, Book Notes) organized by high-quality metadata tags.
4. **Social-Proof Acquisition Engine** — High-conversion newsletter blocks featuring real-time subscriber counts (e.g., "Join 400k+ friends") and "What's Inside" content previews.

---

### Section 5 — Design Specifications

**Visual style:** Friendly Tech Modernism. Clean white backgrounds. Rounded-2xl corners (12px-16px). Hand-drawn personal touches. Zero dark-mode elements — the brand is built on "Daylight" clarity.

**Color mode:** Light only.

**Color palette:**
- Background: `#FFFFFF` (Pure White — for productivity focus)
- Surface / Card: `#F9FAFB` (Cloud Grey — for modular blocks and separators)
- Border / Line: `#E5E7EB` (Slate-200 hair-lines)
- Primary text: `#111827` (Soft Slate Black — not pure black)
- Muted text: `#6B7280` (Zinc-500 — for metadata and labels)
- Accent Blue (Ali Blue): `#3B82F6` (Primary CTAs and Pathway links)
- Accent Yellow (Productivity): `#FDE047` (Category labels and highlights)
- Accent Purple (Academy): `#A855F7` (Mastery badges)

**Total colors: 8 values, 5 functional categories.**

**Typography:** Circular or Inter (Sans).
- Display / Hero heading: `clamp(32px, 5vw, 56px)`, weight 800, letter-spacing `-0.02em`, line-height 1.1
- Section heading (H2): `clamp(24px, 3vw, 32px)`, weight 700, letter-spacing `-0.01em`, line-height 1.2
- Product Title (H3): `18px`, weight 600, letter-spacing `0`, line-height 1.3
- Body / Reading text: `16px`, weight 400, letter-spacing `0`, line-height 1.6
- Metadata / Tags: `11px`, weight 700, letter-spacing `0.08em`, line-height 1.4 (uppercase)
- Max line length body text: 64 characters

**Spacing:** 16pt base unit.
- Section vertical padding: `112px` desktop, `64px` mobile
- Grid gap: `24px` standard
- Container max-width: `1200px` (Modern scannable hub)
- Pathway card padding: `32px`

**Border radius:**
- Cards & Product images: `16px` (rounded-2xl)
- Buttons: `12px` (modern soft rounding)
- Badges / chips: `100px` (pill-shaped)
- Inputs: `12px`

**Responsive:** Mobile-first discovery. Pathways grid becomes 1-column stack. Course catalog uses horizontal swiping on mobile. All hand-drawn annotations remain fixed or hidden on mobile to avoid clutter.

**Accessibility:** WCAG AA. High-contrast Blue on White. Hand-drawn SVG annotations must be `aria-hidden`.

**Motion:**
- Warm Entry: `opacity 0 → 1`, `y: 20 → 0`, `500ms ease-out`
- Card Hover: `scale 1.02`, `shadow-md` transition `0.2s`
- Success Modal: Center scale-up with hand-drawn "Checkmark" animation.
- Forbidden: dark mode, hustle-reds, aggressive parallax, sharp geometric cuts.
- Respect: `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce` → disable all animations and transitions.

### Section 6 — Structure

**Architecture:** Multi-venture flagship.

**Ecosystem Hub (Home) top to bottom:**

1. **Minimalist Nav** — sticky, `#FFFFFF` background, blue CTA "Enroll Now". Logo left (Hand-drawn style mark). Links: Academy, Book, Newsletter, Blog, Deep Dive.

2. **Personal Brand Hero** — 2-column: Left text ("Hey Friend 👋 I'm Ali"), H1, Sub-line, Email Input block with 400k+ proof. Right: High-fidelity lifestyle portrait with hand-drawn "Scribble" background.

3. **Pathways Grid** — 4-column block: "Start YouTube," "Study Better," "Be Productive," "Grow Business." Each card: Icon, H3, description, hover-blue.

4. **Academy Spotlight** — 3-card grid of courses. Each card: 16:9 render, Yellow Outcome Label ("Free Course" or "Masterclass"), Title, "Join [X] students."

5. **Wisdom Strip** — Grid of 3 latest videos/articles with high-res thumbnails and metadata labels.

6. **Footer** — 4 columns: Education, Content, About, Community. 1px top border.

**Course Marketplace structure:**
- Filter bar: All, Free, Live, Premium.
- Grid: High-density cards with "Curriculum Preview" triggers.
- Sticky sub-nav: "View Roadmap" link.

**Wisdom Archive structure:**
- Search bar: "Search 1,000+ resources..." (Algolia instant).
- Category pills: Productivity, Happiness, YouTube, Tech.
- List view: Metadata-rich entries [Reading Time] [Type] [Source].

### Section 9 — User Experience

The visitor arrives seeking **Encouragement and Systems**.

The first 3 seconds must answer: **"How can you help me?"** The Pathways Grid does this work. The UI must feel like a "Personalized Mentor" that organizes a complex life into 4 clear buckets.

The friction to remove: No "Register for account" before seeing value, no dark-mode (too aggressive for productivity), no cluttered sidebar. The transition between "Free Content" and "Paid Academy" must be seamless.
