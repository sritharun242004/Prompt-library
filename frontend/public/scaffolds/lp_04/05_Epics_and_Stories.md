# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · lp_platform_04

### Epic 1: Invisible Interface
*As a visitor, I want a site that feels like reading a classic book so that I can focus entirely on the ideas.*
- **Story 1.1:** Experience a centered, single-column layout with zero peripheral noise.
- **Story 1.2:** Read long-form articles with high-readability serif typography and generous line-height.
- **Story 1.3:** See a pure monochrome palette that established professional and intellectual authority.

### Epic 2: Data-Dense Discovery
*As an insight seeker, I want to quickly find related podcast episodes and technical details.*
- **Story 2.1:** View post dates and podcast durations in a monospaced "Data" font for high-contrast scanning.
- **Story 2.2:** Access platform links (Apple, Spotify) as simple text-links without heavy icons.
- **Story 2.3:** Browse a chronological stream of thoughts where the title is the only primary action.

### Epic 3: High-Density Transcripts
*As a researcher, I want to scan thousands of words from podcasts quickly.*
- **Story 3.1:** View long-form audio transcripts broken into punchy paragraphs.
- **Story 3.2:** See section markers and monospaced notes that clarify the context of the audio.
- **Story 3.3:** Transition from reading a transcript to listening via a direct external link.

### Epic 4: Wisdom Retrieval
*As a fan, I want to find Naval's specific thoughts on "Happiness" or "Leverage" instantly.*
- **Story 4.1:** Use a no-frills search bar that searches the entire archive in under 100ms.
- **Story 4.2:** View search results as a plain text list that prioritizes relevancy and date.
- **Story 4.3:** Subscribe to a newsletter via a simple text input field without any popups.
