## Base Prompt

### Section 1 — Role

You are a senior product designer specializing in extreme minimalism, intellectual archives, and content-first personal brand systems. You understand that for philosophical and thought-leadership platforms, the interface is "Noise." You master the "Invisible UI," where typography, white space, and structural purity are the only tools allowed. You reject "modern" web trends—gradients, shadows, and rounded buttons—in favor of the "Zen" philosophy: pure black-on-white, centered single-column layouts, and high-contrast typographic pairings (Serif + Monospaced). You design for "Intellectual Clarity," ensuring that the path from a philosophical insight to a deep-dive podcast transcript is devoid of friction, ego, and distraction.

---

### Section 2 — Application Overview

This is a premium digital archive for a world-renowned thinker and entrepreneur. The audience consists of lifelong learners, founders, and seekers of timeless wisdom. The goal is to provide a "Digital Library" that handles thousands of podcast transcripts and articles while maintaining a sense of "Visual Silence."

The application covers four primary views: Chronological Feed (Home), High-Density Transcript Page, Topic-Based Wisdom Search, and a No-Frills Newsletter Utility. The primary goal is a "Deep Read" session. Secondary goal is "Wisdom Retrieval" via search.

---

### Section 3 — Brand Voice & Mood

The feeling is technically raw and humanly profound. Like reading a classic book or a terminal-based intelligence report. The brand is authoritative and immune to trends — it focuses on "Signal over Noise."

Copy is concise, aphoristic, and high-signal. Headlines focus on "The Insight" — "Specific Knowledge," "Leverage," "Happiness is a choice." Sentences are short. No marketing "Hype." No exclamation marks.

The visual mood: "Digital Paper." Pure white foundation with sharp black typography. Zero interface "packaging." The site looks like it could have existed in 1995 and will still look relevant in 2055.

Vibe word: ZEN.

---

### Section 4 — Core Features & Functionality

1. **Centered Single-Column Engine** — a strictly focused layout component (`700px` max-width) designed for optimal reading line-length and zero peripheral distractions.
2. **Serif/Monospaced Typographic Pairing** — a sophisticated system using Serif for narrative flow and Monospaced for technical metadata (Dates, Durations, Source Links).
3. **High-Density Transcript Viewer** — a specialized template that organizes long-form audio into short, punchy paragraphs with clear section markers and zero decorative elements.
4. **Wisdom Search Engine** — high-performance search interface that indexes thousands of "nuggets" of wisdom for instant retrieval, prioritizing text relevancy over visual flashy results.

---

### Section 5 — Design Specifications

**Visual style:** Extreme Minimalist (Digital Paper). Strictly text-based. Massive white-space margins. Zero decorative elements — the typography is the interface.

**Color mode:** Strictly Light Mode (Pure Black on White).

**Color palette:**
- Background: `#FFFFFF` (Pure White — the foundation of clarity)
- Surface / Data box: `#F9FAFB` (Subtle grey for blockquotes or mono-metadata boxes)
- Border / Line: `#000000` (strictly 1px solid black)
- Primary text: `#000000` (Pure Black — for narrative authority)
- Secondary text: `#555555` (Zinc-600 — for dates, metadata, and links)
- Link hover: `#000000` (with `text-decoration: underline`)

**Total colors: 4 values, 3 functional categories. No additional colors.**

**Typography:** Lora (Serif) for narrative, JetBrains Mono (Mono) for technical data.
- Display / Post heading: `clamp(24px, 4vw, 36px)`, weight 600, letter-spacing `-0.01em`, line-height 1.2 (Serif)
- Body / Article text: `18px`, weight 400, letter-spacing `0`, line-height 1.65 (Serif)
- Metadata / Dates: `13px`, weight 400, letter-spacing `-0.02em`, line-height 1.5 (Mono)
- Section Markers: `11px`, weight 700, letter-spacing `0.1em`, line-height 1.4 (Mono, uppercase)
- Max line length body text: 75 characters (optimized for reading speed)

**Spacing:** 12pt base unit.
- Section vertical padding: `96px` desktop, `64px` mobile
- Paragraph bottom margin: `24px`
- Column max-width: `700px`

**Border radius:**
- Everything: `0px` (Strictly sharp and geometric)

**Responsive:** Mobile-identical. The single column scales to viewport width. White margins decrease but focus remains centered.

**Accessibility:** WCAG AAA. Perfect contrast (21:1). Text-only navigation ensures 100% screen-reader compatibility.

**Motion:**
- Forbidden: animations, transitions, loading spinners, parallax.
- Interaction: Instant page loads (SSG). Only hover underlines for links.
- Respect: `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce` (though no motion is present).

### Section 6 — Structure

**Architecture:** SSG-first Content Archive.

**Chronological Feed (Home) top to bottom:**

1. **Minimal Nav** — centered links: Articles, Podcasts, Books, Search. `Zinc-600` text, hover to `Black`.

2. **Hero Header** — centered. H1 Title ("Naval"). Sub-title: single sentence in Mono.

3. **Archive List** — vertical stack of entries. Each row: [Date (Mono)] [Title (Serif Bold)] [Platforms: Apple / Spotify (Mono Links)]. `48px` gap between items.

4. **Pagination** — centered. "Earlier" / "Later" in Mono.

5. **Footer** — 1px top border. Copyright + simple Email Input field (Bottom-border only).

**Transcript Page structure:**
- Header: Title H1 -> Date -> Reading Time.
- Content: Centered single-column Serif text. Short paragraphs.
- Metadata Box: `#F9FAFB` background at the bottom with "Mentioned in this episode" monospaced list.

**Search Page structure:**
- Large centered Input: `border-b border-black text-2xl font-serif`.
- Results List: Plain text links with highlighted match segments.

### Section 7 — Technical Specifications

- **Framework:** Next.js 14 App Router, React 18, TypeScript strict mode.
- **Content Engine:** Contentlayer or MDX-Remote for processing `.md` wisdom files.
- **Search:** Algolia for sub-100ms full-text indexing of transcripts.
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS v3 (strictly using typography and spacing utilities).
- **State:** Zustand for managing search overlay state and read-progress persistence.
- **Performance:** Sub-200ms FCP. 0ms hydration delay for text. 100/100/100/100 Lighthouse metrics.

2. **Single-Column Shell** — Build the strictly centered layout component with `700px` constraint.
3. **Markdown Processor** — Setup Contentlayer to map H1-H3 to Lora and blockquotes to Mono.
4. **Chronological Feed** — Implement the SSG feed with text-only links and dates.
5. **Wisdom Search** — Build the Algolia-powered text search overlay.
6. **No-Frills Newsletter** — Add the simple email capture with server-side validation.

### Section 9 — User Experience

The visitor arrives seeking **Timeless Wisdom**.

The first 3 seconds must provide **Silence**. Zero ads, zero popups, zero "Hero Images." The typography does the work. The user must feel like they've entered a digital sanctuary.

The friction to remove: No social share buttons, no comment sections, no "You might also like" distractions. The focus is on the text currently being read.

---

### Section 10 — Constraints

- **No colors** — Strictly #000000 and #FFFFFF.
- **No rounded corners** — Every border and button must be `0px`.
- **No hero images** — No cover photos for posts. The title is the visual.
- **No animations** — Page transitions must be instant.
- **No third-party trackers** — Zero JS for analytics (use server-side logs).
- **No Lorem Ipsum** — Use actual Naval aphorisms for placeholders.
