# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · pcpp_platform_01

### Epic 1: The Immersive Overview
*As a visitor, I want a homepage that feels like a high-end magazine cover.*
- **Story 1.1:** Browse a responsive grid of project covers with smooth hover effects.
- **Story 1.2:** Filter projects by "Series," "Motion," or "Commercial."
- **Story 1.3:** Experience a subtle "fade-in" transition when entering the site.

### Epic 2: Visual Storytelling
*As an art observer, I want to see the work at full scale without any distractions.*
- **Story 2.1:** Scroll through a project with massive, edge-to-edge imagery.
- **Story 2.2:** Read the narrative behind a series in a clean, centered text block.
- **Story 2.3:** View 2-column "diptych" grids that compare related portraits.

### Epic 3: Motion in Focus
*As a creative director, I want to see the photographer's motion work in high resolution.*
- **Story 3.1:** Watch auto-playing video loops in the Director's section.
- **Story 3.2:** Open a video in "Theater Mode" with a single click.
- **Story 3.3:** View a comprehensive "Director's Reel" with high-bitrate playback.

### Epic 4: Print Acquisition
*As a fan, I want a simple, premium way to buy prints of my favorite work.*
- **Story 4.1:** Browse a curated shop of signed, limited-edition prints.
- **Story 4.2:** View detailed print specs (size, paper type, edition number).
- **Story 4.3:** Complete a checkout in under 60 seconds via a minimalist overlay.
