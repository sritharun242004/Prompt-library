# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## BlueChef Culinary Subscription · sbecom_platform_04

### 1. Product Vision
"BlueChef" is a high-end culinary platform that empowers users to master restaurant-quality cooking at home. It differentiates itself through chef-led education and perfectly paired wine subscriptions. The platform prioritizes technical precision and ingredient storytelling over simple convenience, signaling "Professional Authority" to a demographic of culinary enthusiasts.

**Success Metric:** 40% of meal kit subscribers also opt-in to the "Wine Cellar" subscription within their first month.

### 2. Personas
**Elena — The Culinary Enthusiast (Subscriber)**
- Loves fine dining and wants to learn professional techniques like *braising* or *pan-searing*.
- Needs: Detailed instructional guides, perfectly paired wines, and high-quality seasonal ingredients.
- Frustration: "Simple" meal kits that lack flavor depth and don't teach her anything new.

**David — The Sophisticated Gifter (Gifter)**
- Wants to send a "Premium Experience" to a friend for a celebration.
- Needs: High-end presentation, one-off "Celebration Kits" (e.g., Holiday Roast), and professional kitchen tool add-ons.
- Frustration: Sub-standard packaging and generic meal options in other platforms.

### 3. Core Features
- **Educational Onboarding:** A step-by-flow that profiles the user's culinary interests and suggests a personalized "Signature" or "Wellness" plan.
- **Integrated Wine Cellar:** A secondary subscription layer that pairs specific bottles with the weekly meal menu.
- **Selection Dashboard:** A complex grid allowing users to customize their weekly box, view wine notes, and add "Market" items.
- **Interactive Recipe UI:** Post-purchase access to step-by-step videos, *mise en place* lists, and chef tips for plating.
- **Seasonal Market:** A standalone storefront for professional kitchenware and limited-edition celebration kits.

### 4. User Journeys
1. **The Professional Start:** User completes the "Chef's Profile" quiz -> receives a "Gourmet Explorer" recommendation -> subscribes to a 2-person Signature plan.
2. **The Perfect Pairing:** During weekly meal selection, user sees a "Pair with Pinot" badge on a Steak dish -> clicks "Add to Cellar" -> completes the meal experience.
3. **The Holiday Prep:** Existing subscriber visits the "Market" -> purchases a "Thanksgiving Roast Kit" and a "Premium Knife Set" as one-off add-ons.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No "Budget" or "Value" tiers (Focus is strictly on premium).
- No community-contributed recipes (Focus is on curated Chef-led content).
- No grocery delivery outside of the meal kits and market items.

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** Culinary Editorial. Deep navy accents, sharp 4px rounding, high-contrast serif headings.
- **Data:** Must handle complex "Add-on" logic where items can be recurring (wine) or one-time (tools).
- **Media:** High-resolution video and image support is mandatory for the "Recipe Guide."
