# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## GlowProfile Beauty Discovery · sbecom_platform_03

### 1. Product Vision
"GlowProfile" is a data-driven beauty platform that removes the risk from high-end cosmetic purchases. By delivering a monthly box of five personalized samples tailored to a user's unique "Beauty Profile," the platform acts as a personal curator. The ultimate goal is to bridge the gap between sampling and loyalty, converting explorers into long-term subscribers and full-size product buyers.

**Success Metric:** 30% of subscribers purchase at least one full-size product from the shop within their first 3 months.

### 2. Personas
**Mia — The Curious Explorer (Subscriber)**
- Loves trying new products but is overwhelmed by the thousands of options at beauty stores.
- Needs: Curated guidance based on her sensitive skin and wavy hair type.
- Frustration: Wasting money on full-size products that don't work for her specific concerns.

**Sophie — The Luxury Gift-Giver (Gifter)**
- Wants to send a thoughtful, personalized gift to a friend without knowing their exact skin type.
- Needs: A simple "Gift Subscription" flow where the recipient takes the quiz themselves.
- Frustration: Hard-to-redeem gift cards and lack of "premium" presentation in digital gifting.

### 3. Core Features
- **Visual Beauty Profile Quiz:** A multi-step onboarding process collecting skin, hair, and style data via high-quality imagery.
- **Tiered Commitment Plans:** Incentivized pricing for 3-month and 12-month commitments with "Best Value" markers.
- **Digital Unboxing Experience:** A subscriber dashboard showing current box contents with "How-to" tutorials and rating prompts.
- **"Sample-to-Full-Size" Shop:** An integrated e-commerce store where samples are directly linked to their full-size counterparts.
- **Loyalty "Glow Points":** A points-based system rewarded for ratings, referrals, and purchases, redeemable in the shop.

### 4. User Journeys
1. **The Joiner:** User lands on homepage -> clicks "Take the Quiz" -> selects skin/hair type via icons -> picks 12-month plan -> completes checkout.
2. **The Reviewer:** Subscriber receives physical box -> logs into dashboard -> clicks "Rate Sample" -> provides 5-star review -> receives 10 Glow Points -> clicks "Buy Full Size" with 10% discount.
3. **The Gifter:** User selects "Gift a Box" -> picks 3-month option -> enters friend's email -> adds custom note -> checkout.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No "Virtual Try-On" (AR) features in this phase.
- No community forums or public profile walls (Focus is on personal curation).
- No physical unboxing photo-upload social wall (Future phase).

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** Playful & Premium. Pastel pink/coral accents, 16px rounding, high-end grain-filtered photography.
- **Privacy:** Beauty profile data must be encrypted and used only for box curation.
- **Performance:** Quiz transitions must be under 200ms to maintain a "Fun" feel.
