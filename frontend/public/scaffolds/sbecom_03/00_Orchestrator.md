STEP 3 — Read 03_Design.md

STEP 4 — Read 04_Plan.md

STEP 7 — Reference 07_Guide.md

WORKING RULES:
1. Sequential execution.
2. "Glow & Delight" UI is mandatory (Soft transitions, no harsh blurs).

4. Every sample rating must link directly to the full-size product purchase.
