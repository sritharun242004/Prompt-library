# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · bw_platform_03

### Epic 1: Narrative Discovery
*As a visitor, I want a homepage that feels like a soulful introduction to the brand's world.*
- **Story 1.1:** Swipe through high-vibrancy "New Arrival" reels to see latest work.
- **Story 1.2:** Read "The Suta Book" to understand the poetry and craftsmanship behind a collection.
- **Story 1.3:** See evocative product names (e.g., "Village In Heaven") that create an emotional bond.

### Epic 2: Community Affirmation
*As a conscious buyer, I want to see real people of all sizes wearing the products.*
- **Story 2.1:** Browse a masonry grid of customer testimonials and "Influencer Picks."
- **Story 2.2:** Filter the portfolio by "Real People" photos to see the drape on different body types.
- **Story 2.3:** Click "Shop the Look" on a customer photo to add those exact items to my bag.

### Epic 3: Frictionless Quick-Buy
*As a busy shopper, I want to add items to my bag without deep page navigation.*
- **Story 3.1:** Tap "Choose Options" on any grid card to reveal a size-selector modal.
- **Story 3.2:** View clear "Out of Stock" indicators for specific sizes while seeing the full range.
- **Story 3.3:** Experience a "1-Tap Add" from the quick-option modal that syncs instantly with the cart.

### Epic 4: Rewarded Loyalty (Offers)
*As a loyal customer, I want to be rewarded for my multi-item purchases.*
- **Story 4.1:** View a real-time progress bar in my cart for "Buy 3 Get 1 Free" offers.
- **Story 4.2:** See a countdown timer for "Sunami Sales" that creates a fun sense of urgency.
- **Story 4.3:** Use a single-page checkout that remembers my community shipping details.
