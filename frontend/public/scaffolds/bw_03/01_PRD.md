# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## SutaStyle D2C Boutique · bw_platform_03

### 1. Product Vision
"SutaStyle" is a digital community hub for modern fashion enthusiasts who value tradition and inclusivity. It moves beyond standard e-commerce to create a "Homely" environment where each product is introduced through a personal story. The platform prioritizes high-vibrancy discovery and inclusive sizing, providing a welcoming digital storefront that turns casual shoppers into a loyal "Suta Community."

**Success Metric:** 25% of purchases include at least one item discovered through a "Suta Book" story or a community testimonial link.

### 2. Personas
**Ananya — The Soulful Professional (Client)**
- Loves sarees but finds traditional stores intimidating or outdated.
- Needs: A simple, modern way to drape (Ready-to-wear), evocative product naming that resonates with her mood, and proof of ethical craftsmanship.
- Frustration: Sites that are purely transactional and don't provide the "vibe" or context of the garment.

**Priya — The Community Advocate (Audience)**
- Values brand ethics and inclusivity across all body types.
- Needs: To see real people (Influencers/Customers) wearing the clothes and a size range that respects her XXS to 4XL needs.
- Frustration: Lack of representative imagery and hiding "plus-size" options in separate, hard-to-find sections.

### 3. Core Features
- **Horizontal Product Reels:** Swipe-based homepage sections for fast-paced discovery of "Bestsellers" and "New In."
- **"The Suta Book" CMS:** A narrative-led section for collection backstories, poetry, and artisanal diaries.
- **Quick-Option Mini-Modal:** A frictionless grid interaction for selecting sizes and adding to bag in under 3 seconds.
- **Community "Shop the Look":** A staggered masonry grid of customer-contributed photos with direct product links.
- **"Su-nami" Offer Engine:** Integrated countdown timers and "Buy X Get Y" progress bars to drive urgency and higher AOV.

### 4. User Journeys
1. **The Story Path:** Visitor lands on home -> clicks a "Suta Book" feature ("Village In Heaven") -> reads a poem about the saree -> clicks the integrated "Buy" button within the text.
2. **The Community Path:** User scrolls to "Suta Community" -> sees a real customer photo they admire -> clicks "Shop the Look" -> adds the exact saree and blouse combo to their bag.
3. **The Quick-Buy:** User browses the "New Arrival" reel -> sees a saree they love -> taps "Choose Options" -> selects XL -> taps "Add to Bag" without leaving the reel.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No "Resale" or "Pre-owned" marketplace for this phase.
- No physical store appointment booking (Focus is on the global D2C engine).
- No complex "Custom Stitching" tool (Blouses are sold in fixed sizes or bespoke via inquiry).

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** Homely Modern. Suta Red and Teal accents, soft 12px rounding, high-vibrancy lifestyle imagery.
- **Performance:** Sub-1.2s LCP for the primary homepage lifestyle carousel.
- **Data:** Product titles must remain evocative (e.g., "Latte Freckles") rather than just technical (e.g., "Tan Cotton Saree").
