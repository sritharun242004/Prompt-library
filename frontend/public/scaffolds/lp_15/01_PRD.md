# 01 — Product Requirements Document
## ElectricYouth Ecommerce Platform · lp_platform_15

### 1. Product Vision
"ElectricYouth" is a digital hallmark for the modern lifestyle explorer. It moves beyond standard "storefronts" to create a high-energy "Product Launchpad" where celebrity-led storytelling and aggressive FOMO-driven mechanics are the primary value drivers. The platform prioritizes "Cultural Momentum" through visual-first discovery and leverages real-time urgency to drive high-volume growth, providing youth-centric brands with a digital stage that signals energy, community, and unbeatable value.

**Success Metric:** "Flash-Sale Conversion" (percentage of users who add an item to cart from a countdown widget and proceed to checkout) exceeds 12%.

### 2. Personas
**Aryan — The Trend-Seeking boAthead (User)**
- A college student or young professional who wants "cool" tech that fits his active lifestyle.
- Needs: Immediate proof of "the vibe" (celebrity shots), easy access to big discounts (Daily Deals), and a frictionless way to join the rewards program.
- Frustration: Slow, boring ecommerce sites that focus on specs rather than how the product makes you feel.

**The Team — The Velocity Marketers (Client)**
- Managing a high-growth brand with weekly product drops and multiple celebrity ambassadors.
- Needs: A modular CMS that allows for instant "New Launch" deployments and real-time management of flash-sale timers.
- Frustration: Rigid templates that don't handle the "High-Energy" nature of a celebrity-led drop.

### 3. Core Features
- **High-Impact Hero Launchpad:** A full-bleed slider component for new product drops featuring vibrant celebrity promotions.
- **Flash-Sale Countdown Engine:** A real-time timer utility that creates immediate urgency on high-intent grids.
- **Celebrity-Curated "Tribe" Grids:** A specialized discovery system organized by brand ambassador personalities.
- **Visual "Vibe" Navigation:** An image-first category hub where "Bass," "Active," and "Party" are represented by atmosphere-rich visuals.
- **Gamified Rewards Integration:** A high-conversion sign-up widget for the "Tribe" program with points-based social proof.

### 4. User Journeys
1. **The Launch Hype:** Visitor lands on hub -> scrolls through the high-energy hero slider -> clicks "Join the Tribe" launch banner -> enters the celebrity story page.
2. **The Deal Snatcher:** User sees the "Daily Deals" ticker -> notices an 80% discount on a smartwatch -> views the `00:59:59` timer -> adds to cart instantly.
3. **The Lifestyle Filter:** User clicks "Shop by Vibe" -> selects "Fitness" -> views a curated collection of sweat-resistant earbuds endorsed by an athlete ambassador.

### 5. Non-Goals
- No "Tech-Heavy" spec comparisons as primary navigation (Prioritize lifestyle over hardware).
- No community "Social Wall" (Maintain the focused, high-conversion ecommerce funnel).
- No "Minimalist/Zen" modes (The brand is unapologetically loud and vibrant).

### 6. Constraints
- **Design:** Electric High-Contrast. Pitch black foundation, #FF0000 Red accents, bold Montserrat typography.
- **Performance:** Sub-200ms TTI for commerce widgets; Sub-1.5s LCP for heavy lifestyle banners.
- **Format:** All product sections must support horizontal carousels for mobile-first scanning.
