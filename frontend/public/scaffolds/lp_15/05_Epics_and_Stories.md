# 05 — Epics & Stories
## Work Breakdown · lp_platform_15

### Epic 1: Launchpad Excitement
*As a visitor, I want a homepage that immediately feels like a high-energy product drop.*
- **Story 1.1:** Experience a full-bleed hero slider with vibrant celebrity-led launch stories.
- **Story 1.2:** See "New Launch" badges with neon cyan accents that signal freshness.
- **Story 1.3:** Browse a layout that feels like a premium street-wear festival rather than a generic store.

### Epic 2: Aggressive Discovery (FOMO)
*As a deal-hunter, I want to find the best discounts before they expire.*
- **Story 2.1:** View real-time countdown timers on "Daily Deals" that create immediate urgency.
- **Story 2.2:** See massive percentage discount labels (e.g., "75% OFF") as primary visual anchors.
- **Story 2.3:** Experience "Selling Fast" tickers that build social proof and high-velocity demand.

### Epic 3: Aspirational Trust (The Tribe)
*As a fan, I want to see what my favorite celebrities are using.*
- **Story 3.1:** Browse product collections curated by celebrity personalities (e.g., Hardik Pandya).
- **Story 3.2:** View lifestyle photography of ambassadors that helps me imagine the product in my life.
- **Story 3.3:** Join the "Tribe" rewards program via a 1-tap sign-up to unlock exclusive early-access.

### Epic 4: Visual Navigation (Vibe)
*As an active user, I want to find products based on my current activity.*
- **Story 4.1:** Use an image-first category hub to "Shop by Vibe" (Gym, Travel, Party).
- **Story 4.2:** Access top categories via high-fidelity product renders in the main navigation.
- **Story 4.3:** Experience a dense, mobile-optimized swiping interface for quick product comparison.
