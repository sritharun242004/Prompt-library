Models Overview

![Image 5: 61cc0918403fa5644b7778bce4bd4020e8aef7cd-1920x1440](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/2gpum2i6/production/61cc0918403fa5644b7778bce4bd4020e8aef7cd-1920x1440.jpg?w=960&h=412&fit=crop&auto=format)

## [FLUX.2 **Production-Grade Image Generation & Editing** Our most powerful model combining text-to-image generation with multi-reference image editing capabilities. Generate photorealistic images with precise control over colors, poses, and composition, or reference **up to 10 images** simultaneously.](http://docs.bfl.ai/flux_2/flux2_overview)

![Image 6: 58165886dd6712fa4d405e23743815a8ef13bc37-2048x1143](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/2gpum2i6/production/58165886dd6712fa4d405e23743815a8ef13bc37-2048x1143.jpg?w=960&h=412&fit=crop&auto=format)

## [FLUX.1 Kontext **Previous-generation editing + generation** Text-to-image generation with single-image editing capabilities. For new projects, we recommend [FLUX.2](http://docs.bfl.ai/flux_2/flux2_overview) which offers superior quality, multi-reference support, and up to 4MP output.](<http://docs.bfl.ai/kontext/kontext_text_to_image>)

![Image 7: 1400bc43fceb2e5f91b84f10cbfee67dcbcac78e-2048x1143](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/2gpum2i6/production/1400bc43fceb2e5f91b84f10cbfee67dcbcac78e-2048x1143.jpg?w=960&h=412&fit=crop&auto=format)

## [FLUX1.1 [pro] Ultra **Ultra-high resolution** Generate up to 4MP images with Raw mode for authentic photography aesthetics. Perfect for high-resolution creative work.](http://docs.bfl.ai/flux_models/flux_1_1_pro_ultra_raw)

![Image 8: ccf3a9490ff1e793b9cdb078ee55146550f88998-2048x1143](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/2gpum2i6/production/ccf3a9490ff1e793b9cdb078ee55146550f88998-2048x1143.jpg?w=960&h=412&fit=crop&auto=format)

## [FLUX1.1 [pro] **Fast & reliable standard** The proven baseline for text-to-image generation. State-of-the-art speed with consistent, stunning results.](http://docs.bfl.ai/flux_models/flux_1_1_pro)

![Image 9: 65b0d9e7e620d6e8552931648e25340e0977d431-1568x884](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/2gpum2i6/production/65b0d9e7e620d6e8552931648e25340e0977d431-1568x884.jpg?w=960&h=412&fit=crop&auto=format)

## [FLUX Tools **Targeted image editing** Prompt-free outpainting and object removal. Extend any image beyond its borders or erase unwanted elements with coherent scene reconstruction.](http://docs.bfl.ai/flux_tools/flux_outpainting)

Our latest model, [FLUX.2](https://bfl.ai/models/flux-2), combines text-to-image generation with multi-reference image editing capabilities. Test it in our [playground](https://playground.bfl.ai/) or read our [Docs page](http://docs.bfl.ai/flux_2/flux2_overview) to get the most out of it.
