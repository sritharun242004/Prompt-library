## Making Your First Image

**Video tutorial:** Watch this quick [video tutorial on making your first image](https://youtu.be/jDexcrChwRc?si=lEjLAuj_D1IixMaU) below.

1. **Visit your Create page:** Go to the [Create page](https://www.midjourney.com/imagine) on midjourney.com. This is where your images come to life, and you can see them appear in real-time.
2. **Use the Imagine bar:** At the top of the page, you'll see the Imagine bar. Here, type what you'd like to see—this is your prompt.

![Image 2: imagine-prompt-bar.png](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/article_attachments/37435945755533)
3.   **Generate your images:** After pressing Enter or the send button if you're using a mobile device, Midjourney creates a set of four images. Watch as your images take form.
4.   **Congratulations!** Once it reaches 100%, you're done! You've created your first Midjourney images.

![Image 3: create-feed.png](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/article_attachments/37435945762573)

Writing and crafting prompts can be an art in and of itself. To learn more, check out our [Prompt Basics](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/en-us/articles/32023408776205) and [Art of Prompting](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/en-us/articles/32835253061645) articles.

## Using Images in Your Prompts

Did you know that you can also provide Midjourney with images—not just text—to guide your creations?

* [Starting Frame](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/en-us/articles/37460773864589) lets you create a video from your image.
* [Image Prompts](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/en-us/articles/32040250122381) let you influence content, composition, style, and colors.
* [Style References](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/en-us/articles/32180011136653) help you match the look and feel of another image.
* [Omni References](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/en-us/articles/36285124473997-Omni-Reference) help you use the same character or object in multiple images.

**Video tutorial:** Watch this quick [video tutorial on using images](https://youtu.be/lEKeShkzKnE?si=umKfUjroX9mCeZIr) below.

For more information about all the tools and features available on your Create page, check out our [Creating on Web](https://docs.midjourney.com/hc/en-us/articles/33390732264589) article.
